# Quickcinder

Quickcinder is a random event in the game [Legends%20of%20Kingdom%20Rush](Legends of Kingdom Rush) in the map [Extradimensional%20Menace](Extradimensional Menace).
= Quickcinder =
We inadvertently walk into a strange-looking puddle of quicksand, and we start to get pulled into it. The more we fight it, the quicker we sink, and it is already up to our hips.
Options.
Relax and crawl out!
Requires one of: [Orc%20Cleaver](Orc Cleaver), [Ranger](Ranger)
We took a long breath and lay on the surface on our chests, to better distribute our weight.
Soon we were able to slowly pull our legs out of the watery ashes and crawl out of there.
