# The Mirror Pond

The Mirror Pond is a mystery node in the game [Legends%20of%20Kingdom%20Rush](Legends of Kingdom Rush) in the map [Krum%27Thak%27s%20Dominion](Krum'Thak's Dominion).
A strange pond calls to us from inside a cave.
After a quick glance, we notice how the pond offers visions of our future.
However, the longer we stare into it, the weaker we feel.
= Options =
Stare into the pond!
We stared into the pond and received visions of future versions of ourselves.
We continued our journey wiser than before but with an intense pain pressing against our temples.
