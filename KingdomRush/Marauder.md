# Marauder

Marauders are larger, tougher versions of the [Brigand](Brigand). They are moderately armored and have lots of hit points.
## Overview

"Hulking, well-trained armored outlaws, often seen leading [Brigand](brigands) and [Bandit](bandits)."
Marauders are one of the toughest enemies to defeat in the early stages. Their high health and a moderately strong attack allow them to plow through defenses. Even when defeated, the hordes that follow can bring about a swift end. Use plenty of and to stave off these foes. A fully upgraded Rain of Fire can also kill them quickly.
