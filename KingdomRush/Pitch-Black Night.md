# Pitch-Black Night

Pitch-Black Night is a dice challenge in the game [Legends%20of%20Kingdom%20Rush](Legends of Kingdom Rush) in the map [Extradimensional%20Menace](Extradimensional Menace).
We find a shelter safe enough to camp for the night, but the ominous darkness and the sounds of the Wastelands make us too anxious to be able to rest.
= Options =
Light a torch.
Requires: torch
Start a bonfire.
Requires: ?
Find a solution.
Requires: [Gerald%20Lightseeker](Gerald Lightseeker)
We tried to agree on a solution, but kept arguing for a long while as our weariness got the better of us.
Eventually, one of us reluctantly proposed to watch over the camp for the rest of the night
