# Inner Demon

Inner Demon is a random event in the game [Legends%20of%20Kingdom%20Rush](Legends of Kingdom Rush) in the map [Extradimensional%20Menace](Extradimensional Menace).
= Inner Demon =
All of a sudden, one of us starts to writhe on the ground, muttering words in a guttural voice.
Just as we notice their now possessed face, they immediately start attacking us!
Options.
Cleanse with light!
Requires: ?
Tie them down!
Requires: rope
We pinned the hero down and restrained them using one of our ropes.
After a while throwing insults and curses at us, a frustrated demon emerged and fled through a fiery portal.
Knock them out!
We had no choice but to knock our possessed friend out, making a demon emerge and flee through a fiery portal.
After our friend came to, we had to explain the reason for the bump on their head.
