# Hushwood

 "General, you must once more rouse your troops, and protect our kingdom from its enemies! Scout reports indicate that a vicious band of delinquents is amassing far into the depths of Hushwood. Yes, Hushwood, where few of our citizens dare wander... but you are no mere citizen!"
 "Lead your forces into the woods, and vanquish these miscreants. Make them rue the day they decided to embark on a life of crime!"
Hushwood is the seventeenth stage in the game. It requires the [Premium%20Content](Premium Content) in the Flash version. It must be beaten to unlock [Bandit%27s%20Lair](Bandit's Lair).
