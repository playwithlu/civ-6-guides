# Zapper

Description.
Using their advanced technology and gadgets, these electricity-loving engineers love to shock their enemies to the core.
Traits.
 Shocking Presence : Deals [level] damage to adjacent targets at the start of his turn. Does not apply to scenery like TNT barrels, tentacles, or bosses. 
 Lock 'N' Load : Gains extra range for the next turn if the character ends their turn without using any skills. Extra Range: +2
 This unit has +2 Range for this turn.
