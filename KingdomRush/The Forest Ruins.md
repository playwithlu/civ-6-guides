# The Forest Ruins

The Forest Ruins is a in the game [Legends%20of%20Kingdom%20Rush](Legends of Kingdom Rush) in the map [Krum%27Thak%27s%20Dominion](Krum'Thak's Dominion).
"We reach some forgotten ruins full of derelict statues."
"The vine-covered statues are but a shadow of their former glory."
"However, the vines could prove useful, were we to craft healing potions."
= Options =
Collect some vines.
"We harvested as many vines as we could."(+1 health to the whole party, 800 XP)
Study the statues.
"We thoroughly studied the statues and their inscriptions."
"We learned valuable information about the Old Kingdom's history." (450 XP)
Cast restore on the statues.
Requires one of: [Witch%20Doctor](Witch Doctor)
