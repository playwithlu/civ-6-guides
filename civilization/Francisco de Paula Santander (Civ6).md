# Francisco de Paula Santander (Civ6)

Francisco de Paula Santander is a [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to land units within 2 s.
Santander can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to unlock a random [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](Civic). In ' and ', retiring Santander will instead grant a Governor Title.
