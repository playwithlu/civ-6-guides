# Troyes Trade Fair (CivWorld)

The {{CleanPageName}} is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 25% bonus to Gold output. It is available after discovering [Currency%20%28CivWorld%29](Currency) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Globalization%20%28CivWorld%29](Globalization).
