# Nobel Prize (Civ6)

Nobel Prize is a Wildcard [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". 
In "", it was removed from the game and replaced with [Science%20Foundations%20%28Civ6%29](Science Foundations).
Strategy.
Nobel Prize provides a lot of {{Scientist6}} points per turn, greatly hastening your generation of Great Scientists. It's immensely valuable if you're trying to attract [Carl%20Sagan%20%28Civ6%29](Carl Sagan) and [Stephanie%20Kwolek%20%28Civ6%29](Stephanie Kwolek), whose abilities will spur you toward a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory).
