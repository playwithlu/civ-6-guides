# Great Library of Alexandria (CivWorld)

The is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 25% bonus to Science output. It is available after discovering [Writing%20%28CivWorld%29](Writing) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [University%20%28technology%29%20%28CivWorld%29](University).
