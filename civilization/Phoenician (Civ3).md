# Phoenician (Civ3)

The people represent Phoenicia, a playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) from the [Mesopotamia%20%28Civ3%29](Mesopotamia) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ3](scenario) in "". They are led by [Hiram%20%28Civ3%29](Hiram).
Description.
The Phoenicians are [Commercial%20%28Civ3%29](commercial) and [Seafaring%20%28Civ3%29](seafaring). They start the game with [Wood%20Working%20%28Civ3%29](Wood Working), [Cuneiform%20%28Civ3%29](Cuneiform) and [Sailing%20%28Civ3%29/Mesopotamia](Sailing) and build [Tyrian%20Guard%20%28Civ3%29](Tyrian Guards) instead of [Spearman%20%28Civ3%29](spearmen).
