Great Library entry.
Explorers have always been fascinated by stories of ruins. Ruined cites often contained the graves of the rich filled with a lifetime of accumulated gold and jewelry. Colonial explorers took great pride in looting the most beautiful art of their subjected nations, grave robbing without compunction. The collections of the world's finest museums owe thanks to these men of ill repute.
