# Threat (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
When a rival empire rejects or counters a proposal, a leader may find it necessary to Threaten them with action in order to force them to agree. Other leaders never take Threats likely, and for good reason. To Threaten another with, for instance, a city, or their trade routes can run the risk of making them angry. One may jeopardize their diplomatic if the find themselves in the habit of using Threats often.
