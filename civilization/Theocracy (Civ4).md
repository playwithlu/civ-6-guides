# Theocracy (Civ4)

Theocracy is a religion [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)".
Strategy.
Theocracy prevents the spread of non-state [Religion%20%28Civ4%29](religions) and grants free [Experience%20%28Civ4%29](experience) to new [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ4](units) in [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities) that follow the [State%20religion%20%28Civ4%29](state religion).
