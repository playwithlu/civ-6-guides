# Rajendra Chola (Civ6)

Rajendra Chola is a [Great%20Admiral%20%28Civ6%29](Great Admiral) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Medieval and Renaissance era naval units within 2 s.
Rajendra Chola can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to gain 50 (on Standard speed), and to permanently increase rewards for plundering sea s by +40%. In "[Gathering%20Storm](Gathering Storm)", his retirement instead grants 3 Combat Strength to all naval units.
