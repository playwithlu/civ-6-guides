# Caballarii (Civ6)

The Caballarii is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Heavy%20cavalry](heavy cavalry unit) of the [Frankish%20%28Civ6%29](Frankish) [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) from the [Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21%20%28Civ6%29](Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It replaces the [Knight%20%28Civ6%29](Knight).
Strategy.
The Caballarii can be produced without strategic resources and shares the [Samurai%20%28Civ6%29](Samurai's) ability to fight at full strength when damaged. It is otherwise identical to a standard Knight.
