# Proximity Fuses (Civ6)

Proximity Fuses is a Tier III for [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Naval%20Ranged](naval ranged units) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It requires [Preparatory%20Fire%20%28Civ6%29](Preparatory Fire) or [Rolling%20Barrage%20%28Civ6%29](Rolling Barrage).
Effects.
+7 Combat Strength when defending against air attacks.
