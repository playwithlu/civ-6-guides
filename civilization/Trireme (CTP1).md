# Trireme (CTP1)

The Trireme is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
The Trireme gives the player access to the inland seas for the first time. Triremes can only move along [Beach%20%28CTP1%29](Beaches) and [Shallow%20Water%20%28CTP1%29](Shallow water). Both combatant and ferry, the Trireme can endure limited naval battle and, by transporting up to two units, can expand the player's empire across the sea.
