# Veteran (Civ1)

Veteran is a status that can be awarded to [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ1](units) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)". It provides a 50% bonus to the unit's attack and defense.
After [Combat%20%28Civ1%29](combat), the winning unit has a 50% chance of becoming a Veteran. All units trained in a [City%20%28Civ1%29](city) with a [Barracks%20%28Civ1%29](Barracks) become Veterans automatically.
