# Suez Canal (Civ5)

Game Info.
World wonder in the [Scramble%20for%20Africa%20%28Civ5%29](Scramble for Africa) [Scenario%20%28Civ5%29](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". Requires [Market%20%28Civ5%29](Market).
Strategy.
The Suez Canal increases the amount of a city generates. Trade routes other players make to a city with an Suez Canal will generate an extra 4 for the city owner and the trade route owner gains an additional 2 for the trade route. Only one allowed in the world.
