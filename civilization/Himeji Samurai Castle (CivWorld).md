# Himeji Samurai Castle (CivWorld)

 is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a +1 defense bonus for all [List%20of%20units%20in%20CivWorld](military units). It is available after discovering [Monarchy%20%28CivWorld%29](Monarchy) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Communism%20%28CivWorld%29](Communism).
