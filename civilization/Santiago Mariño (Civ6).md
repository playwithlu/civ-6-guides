# Santiago Mariño (Civ6)

Santiago Mariño is a [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to land units within 2 s.
Mariño can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to grant a permanent bonus of +4 Combat Strength to the player's melee and anti-cavalry [Units%20%28Civ6%29](units) within 2 tiles.
