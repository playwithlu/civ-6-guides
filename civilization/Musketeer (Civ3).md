# Musketeer (Civ3)

[French%20%28Civ3%29](French) Musketeers are troops specifically trained to use the new weapon, the musket. They replace [Musketman%20%28Civ3%29](musketmen).
A French city must have saltpeter in its Strategic Resource box to build a musketeer.
The Musketeer originally had A/D/M of 3/4/1, but this was changed to 2/5/1 in "[Conquests](Conquests)".
