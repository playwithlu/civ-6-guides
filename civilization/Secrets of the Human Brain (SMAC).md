# Secrets of the Human Brain (SMAC)

All past research on memory, learning, the senses, and other aspects of the brain did not answer one basic question: what is the biological mechanism of self-awareness? Research into [Biogenetics%20%28SMAC%29](Biogenetics) and [Social%20Psych%20%28SMAC%29](Social Psych) finally resolves the last of the Secrets of the Human Brain, providing an understanding of the fundamentals of consciousness.
Miscellaneous effects.
First faction to discover Secrets of the Human Brain gains a free technological advance. Enables [Social%20Engineering%20%28SMAC%29%23Fundamentalist](Fundamentalist) Politics.
