# Theatre (Civ4)

The Theatre is replaced by the following [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique buildings):
Strategy.
A Theatre increases a [City%20%28Civ4%29](city's) culture and increases the happiness output from using the culture slider. A Theatre also provides culture and is required to build the [Globe%20Theatre%20%28Civ4%29](Globe Theatre) national wonder.
