# Seoul (Civ6)

Seoul is a scientific [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Under rulesets in which [Korean%20%28Civ6%29](Korea) is a playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization), Seoul becomes a [Korean%20cities%20%28Civ6%29](Korean city) and is replaced with [Anshan%20%28Civ6%29](Anshan).
Strategy.
Seoul's bonuses focus on advancement through the [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) tree, and although it can be useful there is little possibility of making it work compared with the other scientific city-states. This is mainly because advancing through the [Era%20%28Civ6%29](eras) is difficult, and a single, unpredictable is usually not worth having to maintain [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerainty) until you research the last tech of the era.
