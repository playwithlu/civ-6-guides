# Request (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
A Request is one of the three diplomatic options players have in formulating proposals.
Requests may involve asking for or a . They may be calls to honor agreements or reduce unconventional weapons arsenals. In a proposal, players may combine them with either an or a . One cannot make a proposal comprised of two Requests.
