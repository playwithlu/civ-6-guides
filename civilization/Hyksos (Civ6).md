# Hyksos (Civ6)

The Hyksos people represent a non-playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". A reused [Scythian%20%28Civ6%29](Scythia), they are led by [Sakir-Har%20%28Civ6%29](Sakir-Har) and have no [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](civilization ability).
