# Trading Post (CivRev)

The Trading Post is a [Building%20%28CivRev%29](building) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Gameplay.
Trading Posts increase the amount of [Trade%20Fair%20of%20Troyes%20%28CivRev%29](trade) produced from desert squares! You cannot build a Trading Post if there are no Desert regions around your city.
Civilopedia entry.
Trading posts are buildings where goods and services are traded. Trading posts are usually smaller and less profitable than markets. They often appear at the edges of civilization, where farmers, trappers and natives trade crops, furs, animal skins and the like for tools, cloth, medicine and other products of civilization.
