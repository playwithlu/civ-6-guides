# Space Tourism (Civ6)

Space Tourism is a Wildcard [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "".
Strategy.
This policy's use is rather straightforward: adopt it when one or more of your opponents is closing in on a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Cultural Victory) and you need some time to either improve your empire's output or achieve victory in your own way.
