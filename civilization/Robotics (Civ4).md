# Robotics (Civ4)

Strategy.
Robotics enables the extremely powerful [Mechanized%20Infantry%20%28Civ4%29](Mechanized Infantry), [Missile%20Cruiser%20%28Civ4%29](Missile Cruiser), [Stealth%20Destroyer%20%28Civ4%29](Stealth Destroyer) and [Stealth%20Bomber%20%28Civ4%29](Stealth Bomber) units. It lets you build the [Space%20Elevator%20%28Civ4%29](Space Elevator) wonder and the [SS%20Docking%20Bay%20%28Civ4%29](SS Docking Bay) project.
