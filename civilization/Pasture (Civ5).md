# Pasture (Civ5)

Game Info.
Basic domestication improvement. Requires [Animal%20husbandry%20%28Civ5%29](Animal Husbandry).
Strategy.
The Pasture improves and and allows you to connect to your strategic resource network. In addition to allowing you to field [Unit%20%28Civ5%29](mounted units), it improves the growth rate and of nearby [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) (especially for the [Hunnic%20%28Civ5%29](Huns)).
