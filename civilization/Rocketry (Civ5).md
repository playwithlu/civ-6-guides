# Rocketry (Civ5)

Game Info.
Rockets are devices capable of self-propelling through the air (or space), and thus independent of initial acceleration. Their invention opens up whole new avenues of development in military technology.
But more than that, the development of Rocketry also proves possible that humanity can leave the confines of its home planet and fly into space! The [Apollo%20Program%20%28Civ5%29](Apollo Program) opens up the late-game race to conquer space and win a [Science%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](Technological victory).
