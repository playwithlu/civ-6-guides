# Research Lab (Civ3)

The Research Lab is a [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ3](building) in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)".
Strategy.
The Research Lab increases scientific research by 50%. This is in addition to any benefits from a [Library%20%28Civ3%29](Library) and/or [University%20%28Civ3%29](University). A University is required to build a Research Lab. Note that while constructed Research Labs produce [Pollution%20%28Civ3%29](pollution), those granted by do not.
