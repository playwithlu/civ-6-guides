# Traveling Merchants (Civ6)

Traveling Merchants is a Wildcard [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Traveling Merchants is the next Wildcard Policy in the game, dedicated to attracting [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchants). Although these aren't the best in the game, they still have some very interesting abilities, and any player who isn't pursuing a particular kind of [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victory) yet or is interested in economic development can make use of them.
