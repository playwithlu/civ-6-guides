# Morale (C-evo)

Morale in "[C-evo](C-evo)", like [happiness](happiness) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, is one factor in efficient [City%20%28C-evo%29](city) operation. A city needs one morale point (shown as a heart icon) for every population point to avoid disorder and the consequent lack of production.
