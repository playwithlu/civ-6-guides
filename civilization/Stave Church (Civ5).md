# Stave Church (Civ5)

The Stave Church is a [Unique%20building%20%28Civ5%29](unique) [Building%20%28Civ5%29](building) of the [Swedish%20%28Civ5%29](Swedish) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) from the [Into%20the%20Renaissance%20%28Civ5%29](Into the Renaissance) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". Replaces the [Temple%20%28Civ5%29](Temple) and requires [Shrine%20%28Civ5%29](Shrine).
Game Info.
Unique [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religious) building. Replaces the Temple. Requires Shrine.
Strategy.
The Stave Church increases the of a city, speeding up the rate at which you can found and spread religions. The city must contain a Shrine before the Stave Church can be constructed. The Stave Church has no maintenance cost and takes half the time to produce as the Temple, which it replaces.
