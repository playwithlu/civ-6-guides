# Torus Dynamo (Civ5)

Game Info.
Unique building of the [Vedria%20%28Civ5%29](Vedria) civilization in the [Empires%20of%20the%20Smoky%20Skies%20%28Civ5%29](Empires of the Smoky Skies) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) of "". Replaces the [Tesla%20Coil%20%28Civ5%29](Tesla Coil).
Strategy.
The Torus Dynamo can only be built by Griggs. It provides a boost to a city's greater than the Tesla Coil, which it replaces. This bonus can be very helpful for picking up older technologies you may have missed, or when working towards expensive Future Era technologies.
