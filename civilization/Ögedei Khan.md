# Ögedei Khan

Ögedei Khan, also known as Ögedei Khagan or Ogodei, is a [Great%20People](Great Person) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, the son of [Genghis%20Khan](Genghis Khan) and [Bortei](Bortei), and the uncle of [Kublai%20Khan](Kublai Khan) and [M%C3%B6ngke%20Khan](Möngke Khan).
