# Tokyo

Tokyo, also known as Edo, is the [Japanese](Japanese) [capital](capital) in some of the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and a Japanese and [Yamato](Yamato) [city](city) in others and the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it is the capital of and most populous city in present-day Japan.
