# Mongol (Civ3)

The Mongol people (or Mongolians) represent a [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) in "". They are led by [Temujin%20%28Civ3%29](Temujin).
The Mongols are [Expansionist%20%28Civ3%29](expansionist) and [Militaristic%20%28Civ3%29](militaristic). They start the game with [Warrior%20Code%20%28Civ3%29](Warrior Code) and [Pottery%20%28Civ3%29](Pottery) and build [Keshik%20%28Civ3%29](Keshik horse archers) instead of [Knight%20%28Civ3%29](knights).
Strategy.
Keshik.
The Keshik is cheaper to build and requires less strategic resources, needing only Horses as opposed to Horses and Iron for the unit it replaces. It also is able to travel more easily over difficult terrain like mountains and hills. It's only downside compared to the Knight is that it suffers from less defense and cannot hold positions as easily as a Knight would.
