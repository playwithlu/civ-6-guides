# Professional Sports (Civ6)

Professional Sports is an [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by building 4 [Entertainment%20Complex%20%28Civ6%29](Entertainment Complexes) (2 Entertainment Complexes in ""). 
Strategy.
Discovering Professional Sports provides many new ways for an empire to earn . Some come from the [Stadium%20%28Civ6%29](Stadium) and the [Aquatics%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](Aquatics Center) (the top-tier buildings for the Entertainment Complex and the [Water%20Park%20%28Civ6%29](Water Park), respectively), others come from [Sports%20Media%20%28Civ6%29](Sports Media) (an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) that further increases the yield of Stadiums), and still others come from [Est%C3%A1dio%20do%20Maracan%C3%A3%20%28Civ6%29](Estádio do Maracanã) (a [Wonder%20%28Civ6%29](wonder) that also increases the output of all its owner's [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities)). In "", it also allows [Builder%20%28Civ6%29](Builders) to build [Ski%20Resort%20%28Civ6%29](Ski Resorts), which provide extra and from [Mountains%20%28Civ6%29](Mountains). The [Brazilian%20%28Civ6%29](Brazilians), being one of the few [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) with an incentive to build a lot of s that provide , should make it a high priority to discover this civic as soon as they can.
