# Toys (Civ6)

 Toys are a unique [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](Luxury Resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are provided exclusively by the [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchant) [John%20Spilsbury%20%28Civ6%29](John Spilsbury).
Strategy.
 Toys are the first luxury resource available exclusively from a . Spilsbury provides only one copy of them, so they are most beneficial to either [Montezuma%20%28Civ6%29](Montezuma) (for the Combat Strength bonus they provide his [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units)) or the [Scottish%20%28Civ6%29](Scots) (for the and bonuses they receive when their cities are Happy or Ecstatic).
