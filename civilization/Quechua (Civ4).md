# Quechua (Civ4)

The Quechua is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [Incan%20%28Civ4%29](Incan) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Warrior%20%28Civ4%29](Warrior) that it replaces, the Quechua has double effectiveness against [Archery%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](Archery Unit)s. This unit is most effective when used to rush enemies who rely heavily on [Archer%20%28Civ4%29](Archers). The Quechua also starts with [Combat%20%28promotion%29%20%28Civ4%29](Combat I).
