# Sweyn Forkbeard

Sweyn Forkbeard, also known as Svein Forkbeard, is a [Danish](Danish) and [Viking](Viking) [Leaders](leader) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, the son of [Harald%20Bluetooth](Harald Bluetooth), the father of [Canute](Canute), and the grandfather of [Sweyn%20II](Sweyn II).
