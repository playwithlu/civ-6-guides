# Riflemen (CivRev)

Riflemen are strong defensive units that are a tier above [Pikemen%20%28CivRev%29](Pikemen) and a tier below [Modern%20Infantry%20%28CivRev%29](Modern Infantry). They are acquired by researching the [Gunpowder%20%28CivRev%29](Gunpowder) [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technology) and are made [obsolete](obsolete) by the Modern Infantry. They are the defensive unit of the [Industrial%20Era%20%28CivRev%29](Industrial Era).
Bonuses.
Egyptians.
The Egyptians receive a bonus movement square for Riflemen in the Industrial Era
Zulu.
The [Zulu%20%28CivRev%29](Zulus) receive a bonus movement square for Riflemen in the [Modern%20Era%20%28CivRev%29](Modern Era).
Russia.
[Russian%20%28CivRev%29](Russia) receives half cost Riflemen in the Industrial Era.
