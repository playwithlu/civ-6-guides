# Taranto

Taranto, also known by its historical names of Tarentum and Taras, is a [Greek](Greek), [Italian](Italian), and [Roman](Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)". In real life, it is the capital of the province of Taranto in present-day Italy.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
