# Datalinks (SMAC)

The Datalinks are the database in the game that shows you all the information. It is basically the [Internet](Internet).
If anyone completes the Voice of Planet [Secret%20Project%20%28SMAC%29](secret project) the whole content of the Datalinks are inserted into the [Planet%20%28SMAC%29](planet's) organic neural network, allowing the planet to achieve sentience, and allowing humans enough time to transcend.
The male voice of the Datalinks is provided by [Robert%20Levy](Robert Levy), and the female voice of the Datalinks is provided by [Katherine%20Ferguson](Katherine Ferguson).
