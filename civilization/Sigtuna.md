# Sigtuna

Sigtuna is a [Swedish](Swedish) and [Viking](Viking) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it is a locality situated in the eponymous Sigtuna Municipality, in Stockholm County in present-day Sweden.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the Swedish [capital](capital) in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
