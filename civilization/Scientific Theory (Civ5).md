# Scientific Theory (Civ5)

Game Info.
The application of Scientific Theory - the principle that everything around us will work better if thought of logically - elevates science in your civilization to the next level through the . It also increases from [Lumber%20Mill%20%28Civ5%29](Lumber Mills)!
Finally, it always allows the development of [Steam%20Power%20%28Civ5%29](Steam Power) and crucial strategic resource techs. [Biology%20%28Civ5%29](Biology) is required for (and hence many Modern and Atomic era military vehicle units, including Tank types and planes). In the expansions it also leads to the cornerstone of modern industry [Electricity%20%28Civ5%29](Electricity), including to revealing the [Aluminum%20%28Civ5%29](Aluminum) strategic resource (although it can't be used for units until nearing the Information Era). Its role involving [Coal%20%28Civ5%29](Coal) and the [Factory%20%28Civ5%29](Factory) was replaced by [Industrialization%20%28Civ5%29](Industrialization).
