# Oromo Warrior (Civ4)

The Oromo Warrior is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [Ethiopian%20%28Civ4%29](Ethiopian) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Musketman%20%28Civ4%29](Musketman) it replaces, the Oromo Warrior has a [First%20strike%20%28Civ4%29](first strike), is immune to first strikes, and gets free [Drill%20%28Civ4%29](Drill) I and II [promotion%20%28Civ4%29](promotions).
