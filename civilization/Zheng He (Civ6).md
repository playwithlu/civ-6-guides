# Zheng He (Civ6)

Zheng He is a {{Link6|Medieval Era}} [Great%20Admiral%20%28Civ6%29](Great Admiral) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 {{Strength6}} Combat Strength and +1 {{Movement6}} to Medieval and Renaissance era naval units within 2 {{Link6|tile}}s.
[Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](Retiring) Zheng He grants 1 {{Envoy6}}. In "", retiring Zheng He instead grants a free [Trader%20%28Civ6%29](Trader) unit and increases {{TradeRoute6}} capacity by 1, and foreign {{TradeRoute6}}s to this [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) grant +2 {{Gold6}} to both cities.
