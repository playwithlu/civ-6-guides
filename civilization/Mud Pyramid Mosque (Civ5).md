# Mud Pyramid Mosque (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20building%20%28Civ5%29](Unique building) of the [Songhai%20%28Civ5%29](Songhai) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Requires [Shrine%20%28Civ5%29](Shrine). Replaces the [Temple%20%28Civ5%29](Temple).
Strategy.
In [vanilla](vanilla) "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)", the Mud Pyramid Mosque is a great replacement for the [Temple%20%28Civ5%29](Temple). It allows an extra boost in relatively early on and costs no maintenance, so don't hesitate to build it in your [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) right away!
