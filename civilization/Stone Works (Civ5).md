# Stone Works (Civ5)

Game Info.
Bonus building of the Ancient Era. Can only be constructed in cities with at least one improved or resource nearby. City must not be on [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](plains).
Strategy.
The Stone Works is an early building with a very nice effect: increasing both and productivity. Unfortunately, this building requires an improved or resource nearby and cannot be built in a city on a [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](plains) tile.
