# Nurhaci (Civ5)

Nurhaci is a playable [Leaders%20%28Civ5%29](leader) from [The%20Samurai%20Invasion%20of%20Korea%20%28Civ5%29](The Samurai Invasion of Korea) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". He leads the [Manchu%20%28Civ5%29](Manchu). He is a reused [Genghis%20Khan%20%28Civ5%29](Genghis Khan).
Unique Ability.
Eight Banners - Upon destroying a Barbarian encampment, the former camp guard joins your side.
