# Neophyte (Civ6)

Neophyte is the starting title for the [Hermetic%20Order%20%28Civ6%29](Hermetic Order), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked by discovering a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ6%29](Natural Wonder).
