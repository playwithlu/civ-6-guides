# York

York, also known by its historical names of Jórvík, Eoforwic, Eboracum, or Eburacum, is a [British](British), [Celtic](Celtic), [English](English), [Roman](Roman), and [Viking](Viking) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)", and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). It refers to several places in real life:
York, England is a cathedral city in North Yorkshire in present-day England, with Roman origins, sited at the confluence of the rivers Ouse and Foss.
York, Western Australia is the oldest inland town in the state of Western Australia in present-day Australia, situated on the Avon River.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as an [Anglo-Saxon](Anglo-Saxon) city in , a [Western%20Australian](Western Australian) city in [Outback%20Tycoon](Outback Tycoon), and a [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
