# Ashurbanipal (Civ6)

Ashurbanipal is a non-playable [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". A reused [Gilgamesh%20%28Civ6%29](Gilgamesh), he leads the [Assyrian%20%28Civ6%29](Assyrians) and has no [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability).
