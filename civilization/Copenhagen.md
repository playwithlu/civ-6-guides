# Copenhagen

Copenhagen is the [Danish](Danish) [capital](capital) and a [Viking](Viking) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the capital of and most populous city in present-day Denmark.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a Danish city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
