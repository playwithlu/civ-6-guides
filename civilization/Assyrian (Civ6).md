# Assyrian (Civ6)

The Assyrian people represent Assyria, a non-playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". A reused [Sumerian%20%28Civ6%29](Sumeria), they are led by [Ashurbanipal%20%28Civ6%29](Ashurbanipal) and have no civilization ability.
