# Privateer (Civ3)

Privateers are small frigates that carry no nationality markings, allowing them to attack and be attacked
"without revealing the nationality" of the ship. Thus, you can attack another civilization's shipping without precipitating war.
A coastal city needs both iron and saltpeter in its Strategic Resource box to build privateers.
