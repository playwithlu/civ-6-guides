# Palace (Civ5)

Game Info.
Indicates that a given [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) is the of the empire. Built automatically in the first city you establish.
Strategy.
Your Palace is the seat of your government. In the very beginning of the game it provides a significant portion of your 's and , boosts output, and is the only source of . It also forms the heart of your empire's trading network.
At all times, the Palace is considered the seat of your government in regards to [Domination%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](domination victory). Although the Palace is automatically rebuilt in another city if your is captured, it can still be a terrible blow to your [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) (and also makes you ineligible to win a domination victory until you get it back). Protect your Palace well!
