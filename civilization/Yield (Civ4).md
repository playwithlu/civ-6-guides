# Yield (Civ4)

A yield is one of the numerical statistics provided to a [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) when it [Work%20%28Civ4%29](works) a [Tile%20%28Civ4%29](tile) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)" and its expansions. Yields include {{Food4}} [Food%20%28Civ4%29](Food), {{Hammer4}} [Production%20%28Civ4%29](Production), and {{Commerce4}} [Commerce%20%28Civ4%29](Commerce), the last of which is converted into {{Culture4}} [Culture%20%28Civ4%29](Culture), {{Coins4}} [Gold%20%28currency%29%20%28Civ4%29](Gold), {{Beaker4}} [Science%20%28Civ4%29](Science), and {{Espionage4}} [Espionage%20%28Civ4%29](Espionage) (in "") each [turn](turn).
