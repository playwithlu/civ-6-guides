# Spices (Civ6)

 Spices are a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are found in [Rainforest%20%28Civ6%29](Rainforests) and [Woods%20%28Civ6%29](Woods).
Strategy.
 Spices provide +3 and +2 once improved with the proper [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ6](technological research). This allows cities with access to Spices to grow large and wealthy in short order.
