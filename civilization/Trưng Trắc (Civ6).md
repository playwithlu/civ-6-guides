# Trưng Trắc (Civ6)

Trưng Trắc is a [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". She was added in the [Babylon%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Babylon Pack). Her unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Classical and Medieval era land units within 2 s.
Trưng Trắc can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to permanently reduce your empire's [War%20weariness%20%28Civ6%29](war weariness) by 25%.
