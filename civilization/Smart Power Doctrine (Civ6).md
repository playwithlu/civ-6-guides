# Smart Power Doctrine (Civ6)

Smart Power Doctrine is a [Future%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Future Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "".
Strategy.
When faced with the deadly arms of a military superpower, you have no other option but to get 'smart' for your defense. The main idea behind developing Smart Power is not to get some fancy ultra-efficient weapons, but to make the existing ones stronger via non-conventional means. This usually includes appealing to other powers through diplomacy, making alliances, and generally making the non-warfare life of the potential aggressor sufficiently hard so that he backs down without you having actually defeated him on the field! 
The [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](policy cards) unlocked by Smart Power Doctrine can facilitate a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Diplomacy](Diplomatic Victory) and hinder other players' [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination Victory) progress.
