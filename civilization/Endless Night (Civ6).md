# Endless Night (Civ6)

Endless Night is the Level 3 title for the [Sanguine%20Pact%20%28Civ6%29](Sanguine Pact), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era).
Effects.
Grants a {{Link6|Vampire}} unit in your {{Capital6}}. Increases maximum Vampire Castle builds to four. Units can teleport between {{Link6|Vampire Castle}}s.
