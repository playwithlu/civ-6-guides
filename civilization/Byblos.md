# Byblos

Byblos, also known as Byblus, is an [Egyptian](Egyptian) and [Phoenician](Phoenician) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in the Keserwan-Jbeil Governorate in present-day Lebanon.
