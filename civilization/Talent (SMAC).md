# Talent (SMAC)

Talents are citizens in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)".
Talents are the cream that rises to the top of your society, your pool of highly skilled professionals. Talents are created by [Base%20facility%20%28SMAC%29](facilities), s, certain specialists, and energy allocated to (each two energy units allocated results in one additional worker becoming a talent.
