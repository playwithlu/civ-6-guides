# Tea (Civ6)

 Tea is a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is found on [Grassland%20%28Civ6%29](Grassland) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Strategy.
 Tea is one of three luxury resources that provides when worked. It also provides and when improved, making it a rather valuable resource to acquire.
