# Recordkeeping (Civ6)

Recordkeeping is a Military [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Recordkeeping reduces the maintenance cost of each [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit) by 1, just like [Conscription%20%28Civ6%29](Conscription) from the base game.
