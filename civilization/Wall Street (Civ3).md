# Wall Street (Civ3)

Wall Street is a [Small%20Wonder%20%28Civ3%29](Small Wonder) in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)". It requires the civilization to have at least five s (five s in Vanilla) in cities it controls. The treasury begins earning 5% interest every turn with a maximum cap of 50 gold per turn.
Play the World changes.
The requirement was changed from 5 Banks to 5 Stock Exchanges, making it more difficult to build.
