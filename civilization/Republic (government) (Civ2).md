# Republic (government) (Civ2)

 Republic is a type in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)".
A Republic is an assembly of autonomous city-states under the control of a central government. Although the central government has the ultimate say in matters that affect the society as a whole, the city-states are given a certain amount of latitude in the governing of local affairs. Decisions are made by the ruler, but are subject to review by a group of officials known as the Senate.
Game Effects.
Switching to a Republic can give an astounding boost to your Science and Luxury revenues, although you will probably be forced to shift some Trade to Luxuries in order to prevent [Attitude%20%28Civ2%29](unhappiness). However, Republics make it difficult and expensive to keep a sizable army in the field, but building certain [Buildings%20%28Civ2%29](Improvements) and [Wonders%20%28Civ2%29](Wonders) can help to alleviate this problem.
