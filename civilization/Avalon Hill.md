# Avalon Hill

Avalon Hill is a game design and publishing company founded in Baltimore, Maryland in 1952. Although the company specializes in wargames and strategic [board%20game](board game)s, it has also published miniature wargaming rules, role-playing games, and sports simulations.
Avalon Hill's games are credited with introducing concepts such as [hex](hex)-based grids, the [zone%20of%20control](zone of control), [unit](unit) stacking, and [terrain](terrain) effects on [movement](movement) to wargames. Since 2004, the company has been a subsidiary of Wizards of the Coast.
