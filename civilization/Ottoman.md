# Ottoman

The Ottoman people represent the Ottoman Empire, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. Their [capital](capital) is [Istanbul](Istanbul), and they can be led by [Abdul%20Hamid%20II](Abdul Hamid II), [Mehmed%20II](Mehmed II), [Osman%20I](Osman I), or [Suleiman](Suleiman).
