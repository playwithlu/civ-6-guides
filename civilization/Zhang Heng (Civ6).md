# Zhang Heng (Civ6)

 is an [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ6%29](Great Scientist) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". He was added in the [Babylon%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Babylon Pack).
Unique ability.
Triggers the for [Celestial%20Navigation%20%28Civ6%29](Celestial Navigation), [Mathematics%20%28Civ6%29](Mathematics), and [Engineering%20%28Civ6%29](Engineering). If they are already triggered, instead completes the [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology).
