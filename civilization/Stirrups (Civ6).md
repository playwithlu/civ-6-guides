# Stirrups (Civ6)

Stirrups is a [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by developing the [Feudalism%20%28Civ6%29](Feudalism) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic).
In "", [Apprenticeship%20%28Civ6%29](Apprenticeship) is also a prerequisite technology of Stirrups.
Strategy.
Although Stirrups doesn't unlock anything else, the [Knight%20%28Civ6%29](Knight) makes this tech well worth rushing if your [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) has militaristic inclinations. For defensive purposes, you can use other Medieval Era [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) like [Pikeman%20%28Civ6%29](Pikemen) and [Crossbowman%20%28Civ6%29](Crossbowmen), which are easier to unlock with [Military%20Tactics%20%28Civ6%29](Military Tactics) and [Machinery%20%28Civ6%29](Machinery) (respectively).
