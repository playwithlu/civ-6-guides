# Portuguese (Civ4)

The Portuguese people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)".
Strategy.
As a naval empire, the Portuguese shine when placed near the sea. The [Feitoria%20%28Civ4%29](Feitoria) [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ4](building), the Portuguese replacement for the [Customs%20House%20%28Civ4%29](Customs House), must border water to be built but the sheer amount of commerce it creates will make any restriction worth the while of its builder. And with the combined strength of [Joao%20II%20%28Civ4%29](Joao's) "[Expansive%20%28Civ4%29](Expansive)" and "[Imperialistic%20%28Civ4%29](Imperialistic)" [Leader%20trait%20%28Civ4%29](traits), allowing a healthy empire to be developed at breakneck speeds, and the Portuguese [Carrack%20%28Civ4%29](Carrack), a [Caravel%20%28Civ4%29](Caravel) replacement that allows the Portuguese to begin exploring the seas far before any other, no other civilization in the game can compare to the sheer colony constructing power of the Portuguese.
