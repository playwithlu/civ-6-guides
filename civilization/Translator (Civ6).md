# Translator (Civ6)

Translator is a {{Promotion6}} for the [Apostle%20%28Civ6%29](Apostle) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" and the [Monk%20%28Civ6%29](Monk) from the [Path%20to%20Nirvana%20%28Civ6%29](Path to Nirvana) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario).
Effects.
Can spread its {{Link6|Religion}} to other [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations') [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) (and [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states)) at triple strength.
