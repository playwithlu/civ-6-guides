# Inundation Calendar (Civ6)

Inundation Calendar is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Inundation Calendar increases the output of each [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) adjacent to a water [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tile) by 1.
