# Revolution (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Revolution causes a city to break free from their empire and form a completely new nation. Any city near a newly revolted city also has a chance of Revolution.
A city will revolt under several circumstances. Either drops to a low enough point that people rise up to rule themselves, or a foreign or incites a Revolution. s can aid a slave revolt in a city that has slaves as well.
