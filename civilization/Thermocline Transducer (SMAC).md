# Thermocline Transducer (SMAC)

 is a [base%20facility%20%28SMAC%29](base facility) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)". It was added in the "[Alien%20Crossfire](Alien Crossfire)" expansion.
Strategy.
It is impossible to get more than two energy per tile until you have Environmental Economics even if you are the faction Morgan Industries running Free Market and Wealth with the Merchant Exchange.
Sea bases would benefit greatly from this as they can have kelp and tidal harnesses built on each water square. See sea bases for more information on how to best manage them, and how to get the minerals you need there for building.
