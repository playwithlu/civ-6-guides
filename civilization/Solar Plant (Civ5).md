# Solar Plant (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced power plant. Requires a [Factory%20%28Civ5%29](Factory); [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) must be on or next to a [Desert%20%28Civ5%29](desert) and not contain a [Nuclear%20Plant%20%28Civ5%29](Nuclear Plant).
Strategy.
A Solar Plant is a modern building which increases a city's , and it's the ecological alternative to the Nuclear Plant as a top -boosting building. Unlike the Nuclear Plant, it doesn't require to work, making it a better choice. However, it can only be constructed in a city which is on or adjacent to a desert tile.
