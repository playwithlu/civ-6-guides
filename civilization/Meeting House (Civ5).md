# Meeting House (Civ5)

Game Info.
Religious building in the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World Deluxe) [List%20of%20Civilization%20V%20scenarios](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". Can only be built in cities following a [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religion) with the Meeting Houses belief. Can only be purchased with .
Strategy.
The Meeting House provides for each [Trade%20route%20%28Civ5%29](Trade Route) within your civilization that connects to a [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](City-State). The Trade Routes can come from any combination of cities, even cities without the Meeting House.
