# Writing (Civ5)

Game Info.
One of the cornerstones of civilization, Writing is the ability to transfer information to durable materials (stone, paper), and thus preserve it for the generations. It allows the beginning of the scientific development of your empire, by the construction of the [Library%20%28Civ5%29](Library), the first science building in the game.
This is an excellent technology for scientific civs such as [Chinese%20%28Civ5%29](China) and [Babylonian%20%28Civ5%29](Babylon) to beeline, especially considering that the latter's unique ability provides a free [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ5%29](Great Scientist). As Population is the main source of , a 50% bonus is quite hefty, and progression towards the [National%20College%20%28Civ5%29](National College) can extend that bonus further.
Writing also allows the establishment of [Diplomacy%20%28Civ5%29%23Embassies](embassies) in other civilizations' capitals in "[Gods%20%26amp%3B%20Kings](Gods &amp; Kings)" and "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)".
