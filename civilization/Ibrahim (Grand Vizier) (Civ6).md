# Ibrahim (Grand Vizier) (Civ6)

Ibrahim, otherwise known as The Grand Vizier, is a unique [Governor%20%28Civ6%29](Governor) of the [Ottoman%20%28Civ6%29](Ottoman) [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) (when led by [Suleiman%20%28Civ6%29](Suleiman (Kanuni))) in "". He is the Ottomans' key to success in both military conquest and diplomacy.
Strategy.
Suleiman's vizier Ibrahim, is a Military and Diplomacy focused governor. In addition to having a unique promotion tree, Ibrahim is the only Governor that can be assigned to another civilization's [Capital%20%28Civ6%29](capital) as well as any domestic city. He is well suited for influencing military operations but is also a skilled diplomat, strengthening relationships with friendly civilizations and easing tensions with rivals.
Like [Victor%20%28Castellan%29%20%28Civ6%29](Victor), Ibrahim needs only 3 turns to establish himself in a city.
