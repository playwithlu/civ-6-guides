# Hill (Civ5)

A Hill is a type of [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29%23Terrain%20Features](terrain feature) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Terrain feature found everywhere.
Strategy.
Hills are mainly useful for building [Mine%20%28Civ5%29](Mines), which allow you to explore their vast potential. However, if they are adjacent to [Fresh%20water%20%28Civ5%29](fresh water), you can also build a [Farm%20%28Civ5%29](Farm) and combine and potential - consider this if there are many Hills close to a [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) and you're short of arable land. The [Incan%20%28Civ5%29](Incan) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) can construct their special [Terrace%20Farm%20%28Civ5%29](Terrace Farms) on Hills, which are more effective than normal Farms for , especially if there are [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](Mountains) nearby.
Hills are also natural defense centers - anything on a Hill receives a bonus. You can also build a [Fort%20%28Civ5%29](Fort) to increase the defensive bonus and create a stronghold in the region.
Hills are excellent places to settle due to early bonuses. A city gets Combat Strength and free , plus the lack of on an unimproved Hill is ignored and automatically changed to 2 after settling for free. A city founded on a Hill won't be able to build a [Windmill%20%28Civ5%29](Windmill), but that [Building%20%28Civ5%29](building) doesn't become available until the [Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Renaissance Era) and early is usually better.
