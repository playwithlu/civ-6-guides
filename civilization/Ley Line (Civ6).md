# Ley Line (Civ6)

A Ley Line is a special [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)", exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode), introduced in the [Ethiopia%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Ethiopia Pack). It appears on any [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tile) except [Snow%20%28Civ6%29](Snow), and only members of the [Hermetic%20Order%20%28Civ6%29](Hermetic Order) secret society can see it.
Strategy.
 Ley Lines are one of the two major bonuses from the Hermetic Order, the other being the [Alchemical%20Society%20%28Civ6%29](Alchemical Society). When revealed, they grant major [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonuses) to all s except [Korean%20%28Civ6%29](Korea's) [Seowon%20%28Civ6%29](Seowon), so plan your s around them for maximum benefit. When players reach the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era), Ley Lines also grant bonus yields, so it will be beneficial to settle [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) near them as often as possible. Notably, [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) like [Scottish%20%28Civ6%29](Scotland), [Brazilian%20%28Civ6%29](Brazil), and [Swedish%20%28Civ6%29](Sweden) who have unique bonuses towards can use Ley Lines to even greater effect, as Ley Line yields increase whenever one is earned.
Similar to [List%20of%20resources%20in%20Civ6%23Strategic](Strategic Resources), where Ley Lines can spawn is decided during [Starting%20a%20new%20game%20%28Civ6%29](game setup), and is revealed to you when you join the Hermetic Order. They are coded to only spawn on any open [Terrain%20%28Civ6%29](terrain) tiles (except Snow) without any features.
