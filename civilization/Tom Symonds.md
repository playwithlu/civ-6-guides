# Tom Symonds

Tom Symonds is a video game designer and artist who worked for [Firaxis%20Games](Firaxis Games) from 2003 to 2012. He served as an artist for "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)" and its ' expansion, "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)", and ', and the unit lead for "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". He has also worked for Zynga, Stardock, and Mohawk Games.
