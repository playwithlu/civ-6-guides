# Treasure Fleet (Civ5)

Treasure Fleet is a special technology in the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World) [Scenario%20%28Civ5%29](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Grants the Defensive Embarkation [Promotions%20%28Civ5%29](promotion) to all land units, enabling them to defend against naval attacks.
