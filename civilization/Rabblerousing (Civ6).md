# Rabblerousing (Civ6)

Rabblerousing is a Wildcard [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "".
Strategy.
This policy will help you thwart your opponents' efforts to win a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Diplomacy](Diplomatic Victory). The bonus to output and the refund you get for reducing other players' Diplomatic Victory points will buy you the time you need to push to victory while they recoup their losses.
