# Zhangzong (Civ5)

Zhangzong is a non-playable [Leaders%20%28Civ5%29](leader) from the [Rise%20of%20the%20Mongols%20%28Civ5%29](Rise of the Mongols) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". He leads the [Jin%20%28Civ5%29](Jin). He is a reused [Ramkhamhaeng%20%28Civ5%29](Ramkhamhaeng).
Unique Ability.
Father Governs Children - {{Food5}} and {{Culture5}} from friendly City-States increased by 50%.
