# Simón Bolívar (Civ6)

Simón Bolívar (24 July 1783 – 17 December 1830), nicknamed el Libertador, was a general and statesman who liberated much of [South%20America](South America) from [Spanish](Spanish) control during the , and became the first . He leads the [Gran%20Colombian%20%28Civ6%29](Gran Colombians) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Simón Bolívar's [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) are a nimble force to be reckoned with, whether on offense or defense. With clever use of his many [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante Generals), his army becomes nigh unstoppable.
Intro.
 You could have followed the whims of your colleagues, Simón Bolívar, but instead you chose to become the Liberator. Hardships never dissuaded you, so hold fast to your ideals and lead your republic into a new age of prosperity.
In-Game.
Simón Bolívar's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Carabobo. He respects other leaders with many [Promotion%20%28Civ6%29](promoted) units and focuses on that himself by building [Encampment%20%28Civ6%29](Encampments). He dislikes those who do not take care to build an elite army like he does.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Campaña Admirable. He earns a Comandante General, a unique type of , when the game enters a new [Era%20%28Civ6%29](era).
Detailed Approach.
Additional movement for [Military%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](military units) makes long marches across the [Plains%20%28Civ6%29](plains) or [Hills%20%28Civ6%29](hills) a breeze for Gran Colombia, whether patrolling their own lands or liberating hostile lands. Field promotions do not end a unit's turn, letting them get right back into the fray. With the addition of Comandante Generals, which join with each new era, Simón Bolívar's army is further bolstered with additional Combat Strength and . The [Llanero%20%28Civ6%29](Llaneros) are best near these Comandante Generals and other Llaneros, and serve as a powerful backbone for a mid-game military force. The [Hacienda%20%28Civ6%29](Hacienda) will provide sustenance and to continue the war path. Simón Bolívar is best leading Gran Colombia towards a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination Victory), though such might can divert resources to other paths as well.
Lines.
Simón Bolívar is voiced by [Iv%C3%A1n%20Ram%C3%B3n%20Orihuela%20Raschery](Iván Ramón Orihuela Raschery). He speaks with a , using the archaic "vosotros" and "os" pronoun and verb forms to refer to the player. Realistically, he would have used the more polite "usted" pronoun to refer to another head of state. "Os" was also mostly unused in Latin America during Bolívar's time.
