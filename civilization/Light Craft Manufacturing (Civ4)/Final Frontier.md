# Light Craft Manufacturing (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Light Craft Manufacturing allows the construction of the indirect attack Alpha Bomber Squadron and the Building necessary to create them, the Squadron Factory. The Squadron Defense Network, designed to defend against Bomber and Fighter Squadrons, is also unlocked, and the Monarchy Policy, granting additional and in the capital, becomes available as well.
