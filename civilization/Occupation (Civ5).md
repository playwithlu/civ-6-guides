# Occupation (Civ5)

{{Main|Conquered cities (Civ5)}}
Occupation.
Trade [Caravan%20%28Civ5%29](Caravans) can be built in main (or annexed) cities and sent to puppeted ones, with the ability to improve their {{Population5}} via food delivery (which requires a [Granary%20%28Civ5%29](Granary)) or improve their {{Production5}} capabilities (which requires a [Workshop%20%28Civ5%29](Workshop)). Also, occupied cities can join the [Trade%20route%20%28Civ5%29](road/railroad) network of the empire and [Worker%20%28Civ5%29](Workers) can improve tiles in that city's territory under the direction of the occupying civilization.
