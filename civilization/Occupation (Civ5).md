# Occupation (Civ5)


 Occupation is a feature of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Occupation.
 Occupation is the process of occupying a conquered enemy [City%20%28Civ5%29](city). When a [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) weakens a city and sends a melee attacker in to capture it, three options (or four, if the city formerly belonged to another civilization) are displayed on-screen:
Trade [Caravan%20%28Civ5%29](Caravans) can be built in main (or annexed) cities and sent to puppeted ones, with the ability to improve their via food delivery (which requires a [Granary%20%28Civ5%29](Granary)) or improve their capabilities (which requires a [Workshop%20%28Civ5%29](Workshop)). Also, occupied cities can join the [Trade%20route%20%28Civ5%29](road/railroad) network of the empire and [Worker%20%28Civ5%29](Workers) can improve tiles in that city's territory under the direction of the occupying civilization.
