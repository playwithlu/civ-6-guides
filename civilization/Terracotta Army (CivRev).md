# Terracotta Army (CivRev)

The Terracotta Army is an [Artifacts%20%28CivRev%29](artifact) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)", available for download with the Iconic Wonder &amp; Artifact Pack.
A player who finds the Terracotta Army receives the [Scout%20%28CivRev%29](Scout) [Abilities%20%28CivRev%29](ability) for all of his or her [List%20of%20units%20in%20CivRev](units).
