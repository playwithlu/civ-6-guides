Scenarios and modding.
Villages can instantly be erased from the map through the [Cheating%20%28Civ2%29](cheat menu) by the "Wipe all goody boxes" command under "Scenario parameters". Likewise, they can be replaced by "Restore all goody boxes", including villages already explored.
