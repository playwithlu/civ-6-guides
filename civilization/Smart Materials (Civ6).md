# Smart Materials (Civ6)

Smart Materials is a [Future%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Future Era) [Technologies%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "".
Strategy.
Researching Smart Materials allows the [Exoplanet%20Expedition%20%28Civ6%29](Exoplanet Expedition) [Project%20%28Civ6%29](project), the fourth step toward winning a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory), to be undertaken. It also unlocks [Reinforced%20Armor%20Plating%20%28Civ6%29](Reinforced Armor Plating), an upgrade for [Giant%20Death%20Robot%20%28Civ6%29](Giant Death Robots) that increases their Combat Strength when defending against land and naval attacks.
