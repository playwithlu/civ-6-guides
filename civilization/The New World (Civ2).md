## Overview

"The New World" is set on a 100-by-95 [tile](tile) [Map%20%28Civ2%29](map) of [North%20America](North America) ranging from roughly to the . The scenario is premised on the [Native%20American](indigenous Americans) being spared from [Europe](Europe)an interference and the devastating precipitated by the Columbian Exchange. It is essentially a standard game of "Civilization II" with a Native American flavour.
The scenario features minor adjustments to early game [/Units](units) and [/Advances](advances). Notably, [Horseback%20Riding%20%28Civ2%29](Horseback Riding) and its derivatives are deferred until after [Magnetism%20%28Civ2%29](Magnetism) is inferred to enable transoceanic trade.
