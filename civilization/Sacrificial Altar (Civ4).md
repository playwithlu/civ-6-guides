# Sacrificial Altar (Civ4)

The Sacrificial Altar is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [Aztec%20%28Civ4%29](Aztecs).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Courthouse%20%28Civ4%29](Courthouse) which it replaces, the Altar costs 30 fewer hammers and cuts in half the duration of anger caused by [Whipping%20%28Civ4%29](whipping), which encourages usage of the [Slavery%20%28Civ4%29](Slavery) Labor Civic.
