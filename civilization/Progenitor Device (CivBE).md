# Progenitor Device (CivBE)

Progenitor Devices are a type of [Expedition%20%28CivBE%29](expedition) site introduced in Rising Tide. These are not to be confused with [Progenitor%20Ruin%20%28CivBE%29](Progenitor Ruins).
Possible Rewards.
50% chance of giving affinity progress, 50% chance to give free affinity level
