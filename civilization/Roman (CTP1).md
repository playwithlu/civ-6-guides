# Roman (CTP1)

The Roman people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP1](civilization) in "". Their default leader names are [Julius%20Caesar](Julius Caesar) for a male and [Livia%20Drusilla](Livia) for a female.
Scenarios.
The Romans are in the M2M [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20CTP1](scenario). They represent modern [Italian](Italian)s and their space and sea colonies, and are player 2 of 24.
