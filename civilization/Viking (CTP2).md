# Viking (CTP2)

The Viking people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Their default leader names are [Sweyn%20Forkbeard](Sweyn Forkbeard) for a male and Thyra Danebod for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Trondheim](Trondheim).
