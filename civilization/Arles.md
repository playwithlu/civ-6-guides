# Arles

Arles is a [French](French) and [Holy%20Roman](Holy Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a coastal city and commune in the south of present-day France.
