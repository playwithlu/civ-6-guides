# Chariot Clan (Civ6)

The Chariot Clan is a type of [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarian) Clan in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It appears only in the [Barbarian%20Clans%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarian Clans) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode).
Profile.
Chariot clans require a nearby [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resource) and prefer to place their [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29%23Outposts](Outposts) in the [Desert%20%28Civ6%29](desert). They prefer mounted vehicles and [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Light%20cavalry](light cavalry) in all roles, rarely using [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Ranged](ranged units) at all.
