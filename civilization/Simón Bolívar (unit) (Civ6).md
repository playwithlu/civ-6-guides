# Simón Bolívar (unit) (Civ6)

Simón Bolívar is an {{Link6|Industrial Era}} [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 {{Strength6}} Combat Strength and +1 {{Movement6}} to Industrial and Modern era land units within 2 [Tiles%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Simón Bolívar can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to gain 2 {{Envoy6}}s. In "", Bolívar can instead be retired on a {{Link6|City Center}} to grant that {{Link6|city}} +4 {{Link6|Loyalty}} per turn.
In the [Maya%20%26amp%3B%20Gran%20Colombia%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Maya &amp; Gran Colombia Pack), Simón Bolívar becomes the [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) of the [Gran%20Colombian%20%28Civ6%29](Gran Colombian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) and is replaced as a Great General by [Jos%C3%A9%20de%20San%20Mart%C3%ADn%20%28Civ6%29](José de San Martín) under all rulesets.
