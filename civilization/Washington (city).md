# Washington (city)

 is the [American](American) [capital](capital) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and elsewhere. Its name is a shortened form of Washington, D.C., the federal capital of the present-day United States of America. In real life, it is home to the [Pentagon](Pentagon) (which is actually in Arlington, Virginia in the D.C. metro area) and the [World%20Bank](World Bank).
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the [Union](Union) capital in [American%20Civil%20War](American Civil War).
