# Wittenberg

Wittenberg, also known as Witenberg, is a [German](German) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a town in present-day Germany that is known for its close connections with [Martin%20Luther](Martin Luther) and [Protestantism](Protestantism).
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as an [Anglo-Saxon](Anglo-Saxon) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
