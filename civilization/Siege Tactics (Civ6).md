# Siege Tactics (Civ6)

Siege Tactics is a [Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Renaissance Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by owning 2 [Trebuchet%20%28Civ6%29](Trebuchets).
Strategy.
Military innovation is usually a series of measures and counter-measures. You invent good swords, I invent more powerful armor. You invent walls, I invent catapults. You invent castles, I invent explosives to bring them down. And so, you will be forced to elevate your city defenses to a whole new level yet again.
The development of Siege Tactics leads to the development of [Renaissance%20Walls%20%28Civ6%29](Renaissance Walls), the most powerful defensive structures available until the [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era). Also, a new field defense system - the [Fort%20%28Civ6%29](Fort) - is invented to provide strongholds outside of [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities).
