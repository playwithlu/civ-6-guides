# Paul Murphy

Paul Murphy is a computer programmer, game designer, producer, and writer who has worked for [Firaxis%20Games](Firaxis Games) and [MicroProse](MicroProse). He is known for designing the [Next%20War%20%28Civ4%29](Next War) and [Charlemagne%20%28scenario%29%20%28Civ4%29](Charlemagne) [scenario](scenarios) for "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)" and assisting with the design and writing of "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)" and its ' expansion, "Civilization IV" and its ' and ' expansions, "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)", "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)", and "[Civilization%20Revolution%202](Civilization Revolution 2)". He was also an editor and playtester for "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)", a designer and writer for ', a writer and manual editor for "[Railroads%21](Sid Meier's Railroads!)", and a production consultant for "". Since February 2018, he has worked for Oxide Games.
