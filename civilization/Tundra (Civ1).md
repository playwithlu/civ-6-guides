# Tundra (Civ1)

These sparse lands of permafrost produce only a small amount of [Food%20%28Civ1%29](food) from grazing animals. They cannot be [Irrigation%20%28Civ1%29](irrigated). These areas cannot be converted to other terrain and make very poor [City%20%28Civ1%29](city) sites. They do not produce any [Production%20%28Civ1%29](production) or [Trade%20%28Civ1%29](trade), even with improvements such as [Roads%20%28Civ1%29](roads).
Tundra produce 1 food. [Game%20%28Civ1%29](Game) can be found on tundra which generate an extra 2 food.
It costs 1 Movement Point to travel over a Tundra region.
