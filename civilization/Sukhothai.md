# Sukhothai

Sukhothai is the [Siamese](Siamese) [capital](capital) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" and a [Khmer](Khmer) [city](city) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it was a city near Sukhothai Thani in present-day [Thai](Thai)land.
