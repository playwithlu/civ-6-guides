# Victoria (Civ6)/Age of Steam

Victoria (24 May 1819 – 22 January 1901) was from 1837 until her death, as well as since 1876. Her 63-year reign, one of the longest in history, saw the achieve the height of its expansion, as well as experience sweeping economic, cultural and political transformations, and became known as the in her honor. She leads the [English%20%28Civ6%29](English) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
With Victoria in the Age of Steam you can lead England into the Industrial Revolution to manufacture your [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victory).
This is one of Victoria's two alternate [Persona%20%28Civ6%29](personas), the other being [Victoria%20%28Civ6%29](Age of Empire Victoria).
Intro.
 Your Majesty the Queen Victoria of England, extend your reach beyond your borders and across the face of the globe. Worry not over the possibility of defeat for your loyal redcoats and overwhelming navy will surely carry the day. With your calm and steady touch you can bring all lands under England's sway, establishing a true Pax Britannica.
In-Game.
Victoria's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Industrial Revolution. She likes [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) with high output and dislikes those with low output.
Her [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Age of Steam. Her [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) gain extra 10% for every [Industrial%20Zone%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Zone) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in them, and all [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Strategic](strategic resources) inside her empire receive a +2 bonus.
Detailed Approach.
England with Victoria is a powerhouse. Her initial research should be headed for [Bronze%20Working%20%28Civ6%29](Bronze Working) to unlock , which receives +2 from her leader ability and accumulates faster from her civilization ability. Then, steam towards [Apprenticeship%20%28Civ6%29](Apprenticeship), which will unlock the Industrial Zone and [Workshop%20%28Civ6%29](Workshop). With her abilities, the Workshop will start the engine of her machine. With all the Strategic Resources she will be accumulating, the [Royal%20Navy%20Dockyard%20%28Civ6%29](Royal Naval Dockyard) is a good place to store them. As she moves towards victory, bonuses gear her towards either creating a large army for a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination) win or [District%20%28Civ6%29](districts) and buildings to propel England into [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](space).
