# Nervana Hesham Ali Hafez

Nervana Hesham Ali Hafez (born July 26, 1990) is a voice actress who speaks Arabic and Middle Egyptian. She provided the voice of [Arshia%20Kishk%20%28CivBE%29](Arshia Kishk) in '. She also provided the voice of [Cleopatra%20%28Civ6%29](Cleopatra) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)", for which she was credited as Nirvana Hisham.
