The is not available in the scenario and is replaced with Teutonic Knights, a special German mounted unit, which can move quickly and will withdraw from combat if they are losing (unless fighting another fast unit).
A German city must have horses and iron in its [List%20of%20resources%20in%20Civ3%23Strategic](Strategic Resource) box to build a Teutonic Knight. This unit will start a [Golden%20Age%20%28Civ3%29](Golden age) if victorious in battle.
