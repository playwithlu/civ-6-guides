# Wheat (CivRev)

Wheat is a [List%20of%20resources%20in%20CivRev](resource) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". It becomes available after researching [Irrigation%20%28CivRev%29](Irrigation). It is found on [Grassland%20%28CivRev%29](grasslands) or [Plains%20%28CivRev%29](plains) and adds 2 [Food%20%28CivRev%29](food).
