# Walled Quarter (Civ6)

The Walled Quarter is a specialty , used to quarantine the victims of the Plague in [The%20Black%20Death%20%28Civ6%29](The Black Death) [Scenario%20%28Civ6%29](scenario) in "". It requires the [Castles%20%28Civ6%29](Castles) technology.
Strategy.
When playing the Black Death scenario, this district is an absolute must. Not only does it keep your people alive from the plague, it also gives a significant boost to Housing that will keep your cities growing. Try to surround it with districts for maximum adjacency bonuses. Build a Walled Quarter in all of your plague-stricken cities in order to keep them alive until the game ends.
