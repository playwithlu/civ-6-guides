# Te Hokianga-nui-a-kupe

Te Hokianga-nui-a-kupe is the [M%C4%81ori](Māori) [capital](capital) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it is the local Māori name for the area surrounding the Hokianga Harbour in [New%20Zealand](New Zealand), and translates to "the place of [Kupe](Kupe)'s great return."
