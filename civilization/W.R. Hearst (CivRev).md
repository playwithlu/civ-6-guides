# W.R. Hearst (CivRev)

W.R. Hearst is a [Great%20Person%20%28CivRev%29](Great Person) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Gameplay.
Great Explorers/Industrialists provide your civilization with new sources of wealth. You may choose to either receive an immediate gold bonus or to settle your Explorer/Industrialist in a [City%20%28CivRev%29](city) to increase that city's [Gold%20%28CivRev%29](Gold) production.
