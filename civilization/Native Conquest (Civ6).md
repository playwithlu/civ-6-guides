# Native Conquest (Civ6)

Native Conquest is a Military [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Native Conquest is quite useful for warmongers, as it yields additional sums each time they do what they do best: kill enemies. It will remain useful for the remainder of the game, as long as the condition holds true. Note that the activation of the policy depends on the [Era%20%28Civ6%29](era) the attacking [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](unit) belongs to, as compared to the era the defending one belongs to, and "not" on the era each of their respective [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) is currently in!
