# Treasure (Civ5)

The Treasure is a special unit from the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Civilian unit. Automatically generated by [Cerro%20de%20Potosi%20%28Civ5%29](Cerro de Potosi) and [El%20Dorado%20%28Civ5%29](El Dorado) if they are within a civilization's cultural borders, and by razing (or capturing) [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) or {{City-State5}}s.
Strategy.
A civilian unit unique to this scenario that represents a land mule train (or when embarked a treasure fleet) containing gold or silver from the new world. Return it to your capital to earn gold and victory points.
