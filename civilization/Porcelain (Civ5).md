# Porcelain (Civ5)

Game Info.
Luxury resource added in "". Unique to mercantile [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](city-states).
Strategy.
 Porcelain, unlike most other resources, doesn't appear on the [Map%20%28Civ5%29](map), but is instead made through the exceptional skills of the artisans in a mercantile city-state. Thus, it is available only if you ally yourself with this city-state. You won't get this resource if you conquer or buy the city-state - the artisans will simply disperse and refuse to work for you!
Note that only one of the two special resources is available in each mercantile city-state: either Porcelain or . Note also that they are available "alongside" normal luxuries that the city-state will produce from the surrounding land.
