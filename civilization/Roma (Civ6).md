# Roma (Civ6)

Roma is a religious [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) from the [Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21%20%28Civ6%29](Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is able to build the [Monastery%20%28Civ6%29](Monastery) and is the founder of [Catholicism%20%28Civ6%29](Catholicism).
Strategy.
Because the player cannot obtain s in Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!, it is not possible to interact with Roma diplomatically. As such, the city-state does not provide any or [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerain) bonuses.
