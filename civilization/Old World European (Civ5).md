# Old World European (Civ5)

The Old World European people represent a non-playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) from the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". They are led by [Oda%20Nobunaga%20%28Civ5%29](Oda Nobunaga). They reused the [Japanese%20%28Civ5%29](Japanese).
This civilization is used only to restrict access to additional parts of Europe. It is not present in the Deluxe version of the scenario.
