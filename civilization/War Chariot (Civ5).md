# War Chariot (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](Unique) ranged mounted unit of the [Egyptian%20%28Civ5%29](Egyptian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Chariot%20Archer%20%28Civ5%29](Chariot Archer).
Strategy.
The War Chariot is even faster than its generic counterpart, capable of moving through great distances, but only on flat [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29](terrain), and hitting the enemy with strong ranged attacks. A great advantage over the Chariot Archer is the fact that the War Chariot doesn't require , allowing the Egyptians to field a large force of these early in the game.
