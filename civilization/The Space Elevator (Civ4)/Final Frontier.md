# The Space Elevator (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Space Elevator allows the construction of the more powerful indirect attack Bomber Squadron and Espionage Stealth Ship Units. It also allows the development of the Stardock Building, which increases Trade Route yield in the Star System in which it's built.
