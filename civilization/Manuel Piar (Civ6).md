# Manuel Piar (Civ6)

Manuel Piar is a [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to land units within 2 s.
Piar can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to grant a +7 Combat Strength bonus to a [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit).
