# Specialists

Specialists are a recurring element in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)", "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)", and "[Colonization%20%28game%29](Colonization)" games. In "Civilization" and "Call to Power", they are alternative [work](work) assignments for [citizen](citizen)s; in "Colonization", they are [unit](unit)s.
