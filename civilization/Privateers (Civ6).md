# Privateers (Civ6)

The Privateers are one of four pirate factions from the [Pirates%20%28Civ6%29](Pirates) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Intro.
The Royal Navy has goals on the seas - cities that need plundering, ships that need capturing. But the Royal Navy has rules, and laws, and treaties, and their officers wear wigs. So they give you a letter of marque, so you can do the things they cannot. Go, and put the enemies of the Crown to the torch!
