# Totem Pole (Civ4)

The Totem Pole is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) for the [Native%20American%20%28Civ4%29](Native American civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Monument%20%28Civ4%29](Monument) that it replaces, the Totem Pole gives three bonus [experience](experience) points to all [Archery%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](archery units) built in its city.
