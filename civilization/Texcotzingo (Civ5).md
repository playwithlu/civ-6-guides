# Texcotzingo (Civ5)

Game Info.
World wonder in the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World Deluxe) [Scenario%20%28Civ5%29](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". May only be constructed by Native Americans in a city next to a [Rivers%20%28Civ5%29](river) or [Lakes%20%28Civ5%29](lake).
Strategy.
Texcotzingo increases a city's production of and , and also provides it with a [Garden%20%28Civ5%29](Garden) that expedites [Great%20people%20%28Civ5%29](Great Person) generation. These bonuses will allow the city to grow rapidly and become a cornerstone of a Native American empire, but they will be lost if the city is captured. Be sure to protect it well.
