# Scout (Civ3)

Scouts are unarmed, non-combat units with two movement points. They are good at exploring the countryside. Only [Expansionist%20%28Civ3%29](Expansionist) [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilizations) can build Scouts.
Variants.
The [Incan%20%28Civ3%29](Inca) instead build their [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ3%29](unique) [Chasqui%20Scout%20%28Civ3%29](Chasqui Scout) which, unlike the Scout, can defend and attack in addition to suffering no penalties when moving through hills and mountains.
