# River Goddess (Civ6)

River Goddess is a [Pantheons%20%28Civ6%29](Pantheon) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It provides +1 to [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) if they have a [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Site) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) adjacent to a [River%20%28Civ6%29](River). In "[Gathering%20Storm](Gathering Storm)", it was altered to provide +2 and +2 to cities instead.
Strategy.
Obviously, the more rivers your empire contains, the more useful this pantheon becomes, especially for militaristic or religious players. River Goddess is useful for both the [Dutch%20%28Civ6%29](Dutch) and the [Hungarian%20%28Civ6%29](Hungarians), whose unique abilities encourage them to found and develop riverside cities, as well as the [Khmer%20%28Civ6%29](Khmer), since [Jayavarman%20VII%20%28Civ6%29](Jayavarman VII's) leader ability already grants riverside Holy Sites bonus and . The extra and from the pantheon will make these cities more productive and allow them to support a larger Population. The [Egyptian%20%28Civ6%29](Egyptians), with their [Starting%20bias%20%28Civ6%29](starting bias) for rivers and ability to build on [Floodplains%20%28Civ6%29](Floodplains), can also easily use this pantheon.
