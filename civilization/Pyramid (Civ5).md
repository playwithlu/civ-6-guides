# Pyramid (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20building%20%28Civ5%29](Unique building) of the [Mayan%20%28Civ5%29](Mayan) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Shrine%20%28Civ5%29](Shrine). 
Strategy.
The Pyramid produces twice as much as the building it replaces and additionally produces 2 , which is a great thing early on in the game. Mayan players should research [Pottery%20%28Civ5%29](Pottery) as soon as possible and build these buildings first thing in every city, which will allow them to get ahead of the technological race in the early game.
A quick early progression is essential for the Maya, because it allows them to research [Theology%20%28Civ5%29](Theology) faster and start making use of their [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ5%29](unique ability).
