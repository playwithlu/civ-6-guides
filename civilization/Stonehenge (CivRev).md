# Stonehenge (CivRev)

Stonehenge is a [Wonder%20%28CivRev%29](wonder) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Stonehenge increases the effectiveness of [Temple%20%28CivRev%29](Temples) by 50%, bolstering the speed at which your borders will expand, the rate at which [Great%20Person%20%28CivRev%29](Great People) will appear in your [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CivRev](civilization) and the effect your cultural influence will have in enemy [City%20%28CivRev%29](cities).
Stonehenge is the first available wonder in the game and costs 50 resources, so even in the [Ancient%20Era%20%28CivRev%29](Ancient Era) it will take about 20 turns to complete. It becomes [obsolete](obsolete) once any civilization researches [Literacy%20%28CivRev%29](Literacy).
