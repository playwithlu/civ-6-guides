# Rock 'n' Roll (Civ4)

Strategy.
Rock 'n' Roll provides your [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) with five [Hit%20Singles%20%28Civ4%29](Hit Singles) [Resource%20%28Civ4%29](resources) (which provide [Happiness%20%28Civ4%29](Happiness)). It increases the odds that its [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) will create a [Great%20Artist%20%28Civ4%29](Great Artist).
