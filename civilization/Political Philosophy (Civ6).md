# Political Philosophy (Civ6)

Political Philosophy is a [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by meeting 3 [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states). 
Strategy.
Early states are usually built instinctively, by capable rulers with foresight, which know what is best for their people. They are governed mostly by popular consent. But when states start turning into empires, when classes start arising and it becomes increasingly difficult to govern an expanding population, it becomes essential to organize politics and establish some ideological concept on what should government look like.
Adopting political philosophy unlocks the first three types of early Governments: [Autocracy%20%28Civ6%29](Autocracy), [Oligarchy%20%28Civ6%29](Oligarchy) and [Classical%20Republic%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Republic). It also unlocks the first Diplomatic Policies in the game.
