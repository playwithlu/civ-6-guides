# Silver (Col)

Silver is one of the most valuable metals found in the New World. Silver can be mined from mountain squares, and some native civilizations have it in great quantity. Unlike other resources, silver will be depleted after a certain amount has been mined, and will not be as productive.
Silver has the highest selling price in [Europe](Europe), but the price drops rapidly after you begin selling it. The price does not recover, unlike other goods.
