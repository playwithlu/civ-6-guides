# Pavilion (Civ4)

The Pavilion is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [Chinese%20%28Civ4%29](Chinese civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Theatre%20%28Civ4%29](Theatre) that it replaces, the Pavilion gives +25% [Culture%20%28Civ4%29](culture) to the city it is constructed in.
