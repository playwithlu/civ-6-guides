# S-35 Tank (CTP2)

The Somua 1935 is a powerful tank. It is a very fast tank compared to the German ones and is the best tank that the French had when WWII broke out. Their other tank is the [R-35%20tank%20%28CTP2%29](R-35). This type of tank was not retired from service till the year 1945 - lasting till the end of WW2, which is roughly 10 years of duty helping out in the defense of France.
