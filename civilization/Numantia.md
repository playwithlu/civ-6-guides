# Numantia

Numantia is the [Celtic](Celtic) [capital](capital) in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games) and a Celtic [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it was a Celtiberian settlement whose ruins are located near Soria in present-day [Spain](Spain).
