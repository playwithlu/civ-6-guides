# Strength

Strength (sometimes called Combat Strength) is a rough measurement of the fighting prowess of a [unit](unit) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games.
Prior to "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)", Strength was divided into "Attack" and "[Defense](Defense)." "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" introduced different types of Strength for melee and ranged attacks, and "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" introduced three new types of Strength.
