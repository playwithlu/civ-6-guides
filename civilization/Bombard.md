# Bombard

Bombard is a [siege%20unit](siege unit) and unit action in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)". It refers to an attack with a [Siege%20unit](siege), [Naval%20unit](naval), or [air%20unit](air unit) that deals damage to units, [tile%20improvement](tile improvement)s, or the defenses of a [city](city) in the targeted [tile](tile).
