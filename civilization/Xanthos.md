# Xanthos

Xanthos, also known as Xanthus or Ksantos, is a [Greek](Greek) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and the [Lycian](Lycian) [capital](capital) and a [Minoan](Minoan) city in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it is is a ruined city in present-day [Turkish](Turkey) that was once the capital of Lycia.
