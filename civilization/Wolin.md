# Wolin

Wolin is a [city-state](city-state) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and a [Polish](Polish) [city](city) in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it is a town in northwestern present-day Poland, situated on the southern tip of the Wolin island off the Baltic coast of the historic region of Western Pomerania.
