# University (technology) (CivRev)

University is a [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technology) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". It allows players to build [University%20%28building%29%20%28CivRev%29](Universities) and [Oxford%20University%20%28CivRev%29](Oxford University), a [Wonder%20%28CivRev%29](wonder) that awards an advanced technology.
The first player to discover University will receive [Aristotle%20%28CivRev%29](Aristotle).
