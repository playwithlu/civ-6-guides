# Printing Press (Civ5)

Game Info.
A landmark invention for the contemporary world, the Printing Press allows the almost unrestricted copying and distribution of written material, such as books, newspapers, and texts. It also allows the construction of the next -providing building, the [Theatre%20%28Civ5%29](Theater) (replaced by the [Zoo%20%28Civ5%29](Zoo) in "").
In "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)" this technology is required to convene the first [World%20congress%20%28Civ5%29](World Congress), once someone has met all other civilizations.
