# The Great War (Civ6)

The Great War is a [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) from the [Outback%20Tycoon%20%28Civ6%29](Outback Tycoon) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The Great War is essentially a "turn wall" because of its insane cost. Once one progresses to Turn 45, an is applied, reducing its cost by 99%.
