# Winchester

Winchester, also known as Wintanceaster, is an [English](English) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state).
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as an [Anglo-Saxon](Anglo-Saxon) city.
