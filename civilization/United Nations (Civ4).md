# United Nations (Civ4)

Strategy.
Once a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civ) has entered into the [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ4%29](Modern Era) and researched [Mass%20Media%20%28Civ4%29](Mass Media), the United Nations can be used to trigger [Victory%20%28Civ4%29%23Diplomatic](Diplomatic Victory) [Elections%20%28Civ4%29](elections). It guarantees that you will be a candidate in those elections and increases its [City%20%28Civ4%29](city's) odds of generating a [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ4%29](Great Merchant). Rush to discover Mass Media then use a [Great%20Engineer%20%28Civ4%29](Great Engineer) to build it faster.
