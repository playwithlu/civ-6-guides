# United Nations (Civ5)

Game Info.
Vanilla and "Gods &amp; Kings".
The United Nations triggers voting for the [Diplomatic%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](Diplomatic Victory) when someone builds it. Because of this, there is an [Steam%20achievements%20in%20Civ5](achievement) related to building it.
"Brave New World".
In "", the United Nations is not an actual World Wonder anymore. Instead, the [World%20Congress%20%28Civ5%29](World Congress) will turn into the United Nations when any civilization reaches the [Information%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Information Era) or at least half the world's civilizations reach the [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Atomic Era).
