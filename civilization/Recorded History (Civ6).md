# Recorded History (Civ6)

Recorded History is a [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by constructing 2 [Campus%20%28Civ6%29](Campus) [Districts%20%28Civ6%29](districts).
Strategy.
In time a civilization feels the need to remember its own past. Great civilizations even need to remember the past of other civilizations, and this is how we get to know about the ancient world from multiple sources, which started recording events in their own, and their neighbors', history. This builds the foundations of the further development of a healthy state.
Developing written history immensely improves science, thanks to a new Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy). Now, we still need to learn how to ... learn from our past. Even today.
