# Capital Ship Focus (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Capital Ship Focus allows the construction of the Squadron-carrying Alpha Carrier and Star System-capturing Invasion Ship Units. It also enables the Capital Ship Doctrine Policy, which makes Battleships cheaper.
