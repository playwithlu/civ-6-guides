# Tundra (Freeciv)

Tundra is a type of [terrain](terrain) in [Freeciv](Freeciv). It can contain [Game](Game) or [Furs](Furs). [Engineer](Engineer)s can transform a [Glacier%20%28Freeciv%29](Glacier) into Tundra, and Tundra into a [Desert%20%28Freeciv%29](Desert) (and any [resource](resource)s on the tile will be carried over when it transforms).
