# Swashbucklers (Civ6)

The Swashbucklers are one of four pirate factions from the [Pirates%20%28Civ6%29](Pirates) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Intro.
The voyage may end in the gibbet, or at the execution dock, but for now, ye be free. Ye sail to drive the horizon back, and find and claim whatever lies there, whether it be treasure or peril. Aye, the wind be freshening and yer sails run taut!
