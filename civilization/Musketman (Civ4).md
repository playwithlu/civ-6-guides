# Musketman (Civ4)

The Musketman is a defensive gunpowder unit, used to defend [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities).
The Musketman is replaced by the following [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique units):
Strategy.
This early gunpowder unit requires no special [Resource%20%28Civ4%29](resources) and is good for defending cities and attacking [Archer%20%28Civ4%29](Archers) or any melee units. In groups, they are good for besieging rival [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilizations') cities.
