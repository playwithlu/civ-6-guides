# Radio (Civ4)

Strategy.
Radio enables the powerful [Bomber%20%28Civ4%29](Bomber) (with [Flight%20%28Civ4%29](Flight)) and [Guided%20Missile%20%28Civ4%29](Guided Missile) (with [Rocketry%20%28Civ4%29](Rocketry)) units, plus the [Attack%20Submarine%20%28Civ4%29](Attack Submarine) (with [Combustion%20%28Civ4%29](Combustion)) and [Submarine%20%28Civ4%29](Submarine) units. It also allows you to build the [Eiffel%20Tower%20%28Civ4%29](Eiffel Tower), [Cristo%20Redentor%20%28Civ4%29](Cristo Redentor) and [Rock%20%27n%27%20Roll%20%28Civ4%29](Rock 'n' Roll) wonders.
