# Spices (CTP2)

Spices are a common [Goods%20%28CTP2%29](good) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". They are found in [Plains%20%28CTP2%29](Plains).
Gameplay.
The is host to a diverse array of plants, some of which are endowed with fragrances and flavors that can be used to season and preserve food. Some spices are not available everywhere, and can fetch high prices on the open market.
