# Redcoat (Civ4)

The Redcoat is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [English%20%28Civ4%29](English) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Redcoats are a type of Musketeer Unit. Compared to the [Rifleman%20%28Civ4%29](Rifleman), which this unit replaces, the Redcoat has 25% more effectiveness against other [Gunpowder%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](Gunpowder Units).
Originally, the Redcoat had 16 strength, but it was lowered to 14 in a patch.
