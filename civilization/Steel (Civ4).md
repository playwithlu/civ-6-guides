# Steel (Civ4)

Strategy.
Steel enables you to build [Cannon%20%28Civ4%29](Cannon) and [Ironclad%20%28Civ4%29](Ironclads) (with [Steam%20Power%20%28Civ4%29](Steam Power)). It also enables construction of the [Drydock%20%28Civ4%29](Drydock) building and the [Ironworks%20%28Civ4%29](Ironworks) national wonder.
