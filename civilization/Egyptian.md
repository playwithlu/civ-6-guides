# Egyptian

The Egyptian people represent Egypt, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)". Their [capital](capital) might be [Alexandria](Alexandria), [Cairo](Cairo), [Memphis](Memphis), or [Thebes](Thebes), and they can be led by [Cleopatra](Cleopatra), [Hatshepsut](Hatshepsut), [Isma%27il%20Pasha](Isma'il Pasha), or [Ramesses%20II](Ramesses II).
The [Ayyubid](Ayyubid) and [Ptolemaic](Ptolemaic) people may also be represented by the Egyptians.
