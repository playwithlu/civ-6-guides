# Nuclear Fusion (Civ6)

Nuclear Fusion is an [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". In "", it can be hurried by building a [Nuclear%20Power%20Plant%20%28Civ6%29](Nuclear Power Plant). 
Strategy.
Nuclear fusion is the next step in high-grade energy manipulation. Potentially much safer than fission, fusion could also be much more deadly, under the right circumstances. And, since deadliness is all war is about, the military takes care these circumstances to come about. And thus appear nuclear devices even more deadlier than before. 
However, thus also appear the means to sustain a potential base on Mars.
