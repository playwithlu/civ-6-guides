# Frontier Fortresses (Civ6)

Frontier Fortresses is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Frontier Fortresses provides +4 {{Gold6}} from every {{TradeRoute6}}, much like [Triangular%20Trade%20%28Civ6%29](Triangular Trade) from the base game.
