# Tipi (Civ5)

The Tipi is a unique improvement from the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World Deluxe) [Scenario%20%28Civ5%29](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Unique improvement of the [Shoshone%20%28Civ5%29](Shoshone). Only a [Worker%20%28Civ5%29](Worker) may build it.
Strategy.
The Tipi allows the Shoshone to rapidly increase the of their [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) and expedite their religious development. It can only be built on a flat [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](plains) tile (even if it has a feature) not adjacent to another Tipi.
