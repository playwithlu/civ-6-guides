# Atoll (Civ5)

An Atoll is a type of [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29%23Terrain%20Features](terrain feature) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Terrain feature found in coastal regions.
Strategy.
[Coast%20%28Civ5%29](Coast) tiles containing Atolls benefit from the [Lighthouse%20%28Civ5%29](Lighthouse's) +1 bonus. Note, however, that they aren't considered a [Resources%20%28Civ5%29](resource), so they don't get additional benefits!
In "", the [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ5%29](unique ability) of the [Japanese%20%28Civ5%29](Japanese) gives them an additional bonus yield of +2 from Atolls.
