Choosing which Rainforest [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) to keep for adjacency and which to clear to slip in s is always a tricky decision for Brazil, but when set up properly, their [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) can be powerful generators of , , and : all useful commodities when pursuing Great People. Holding [Carnival%20%28Civ6%29](Carnivals) can also help them earn even more Great People. Carnival's emphasis on [Great%20Artist%20%28Civ6%29](Great Artists) gives them a slight edge toward the [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory), but Great Person generation can lead them effectively along any of the victory paths.
