# Space Era (CivWorld)

The Space Era is the twentieth [Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Era) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It comes after the [Digital%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Digital Era) and before the [Galactic%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Galactic Era). During this Era, players receive a 25% bonus to their Gold output.
