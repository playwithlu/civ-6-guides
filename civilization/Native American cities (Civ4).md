# Native American cities (Civ4)

Native American cities are [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities) that can be founded by the [Native%20American%20%28Civ4%29](Native American) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)" and its expansions. The first city founded is the [Capital%20%28Civ4%29](capital), and the names of future cities are chosen in the order they appear on the list below.
The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
