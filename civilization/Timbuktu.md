# Timbuktu

Timbuktu, also known as Timbuctu and Tombouctu, is the [Malian](Malian) [capital](capital) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)", and a Malian and [Songhai](Songhai) [city](city) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it is a city in present-day Mali.
