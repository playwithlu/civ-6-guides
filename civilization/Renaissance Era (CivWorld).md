# Renaissance Era (CivWorld)

The Renaissance Era is the eighth [Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Era) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It comes after the [Middle%20Ages%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Middle Ages Era) and before the [Baroque%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Baroque Era). During this Era, players receive a 50% bonus to their Science output.
