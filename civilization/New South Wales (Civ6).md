# New South Wales (Civ6)

New South Wales is a trade [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) from the [Outback%20Tycoon%20%28Civ6%29](Outback Tycoon) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
New South Wales's bonuses focus solely on {{Gold6}} income. The extra {{Gold6}} in all [Commercial%20Hub%20%28Civ6%29](Commercial Hubs) will give you invaluable advantages over your opponents in the scenario, so gaining control of this city-state is very important.
