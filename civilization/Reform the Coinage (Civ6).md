# Reform the Coinage (Civ6)

Reform the Coinage is a [Golden%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Golden Age) [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Dramatic%20Ages%20%28Civ6%29](Dramatic Ages) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is available from the [Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Renaissance Era) to the [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era) and uses a Wildcard slot.
Strategy.
Reform the Coinage is the safest bet for the midgame, available from the Renaissance all the way to the Modern Era. It provides a hefty profit - something which is useful in practically every situation - plus safety from pesky [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarians) looking for easy profits. The more developed the destination is, the more profit this card will bring you. This card is recommended for [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) who focus on building their trade network with many s, like [Malian%20%28Civ6%29](Mali), the [Cree%20%28Civ6%29](Cree), [Egyptian%20%28Civ6%29](Egypt), [Phoenician%20%28Civ6%29](Phoenicia), the [Dutch%20%28Civ6%29](Netherlands), [English%20%28Civ6%29](England), [Portuguese%20%28Civ6%29](Portugal) and [Spanish%20%28Civ6%29](Spain).
