# Pan-Spectral Observatory (CivBE)

The Pan-Spectral Observatory is a building in the "[Rising%20Tide](Rising Tide)" expansion pack. It can only be built by combining [Old%20Earth%20Relics%20%28CivBE%29](Old Earth Relics)
The city has +100% for Orbital Units, and it earns +2 city [Orbitals%20%28CivBE%29](orbital coverage) range
