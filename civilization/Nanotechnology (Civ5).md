# Nanotechnology (Civ5)

Game Info.
The advancements in the field of micro-machines - known as Nanotechnology, brings us the means to build the last part of the Spaceship - the [SS%20stasis%20chamber%20%28Civ5%29](Stasis Chamber).
In "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)" it also brings us a new unit - the futuristic [XCOM%20Squad%20%28Civ5%29](XCOM Squad), specialist at dropping in almost any hotpoint around the world.
