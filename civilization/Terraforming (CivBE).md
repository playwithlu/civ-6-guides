# Terraforming (CivBE)

Game Info.
Terraforming is a [Primary%20Tech%20%28CivBE%29](Primary Tech) in tier 2 of the [Tech_Web_%28CivBE%29](Tech Web). This technology allows construction of the [Gaian%20Well%20%28CivBE%29](Gaian Well), and allows workers to construct the [Floatstone%20Quarry%20%28CivBE%29](Floatstone Quarry) and the [Terrascape%20%28CivBE%29](Terrascape) Tile impovement.
There is no affinity linked to this technology.
