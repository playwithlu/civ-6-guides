# Plumbing (Civ2)

Plumbing was an [List%20of%20advances%20in%20Civ2](advance) that was slated to appear in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)", but was cut from the game before its release.
According to RULES.txt, Plumbing was supposed to be available to research after discovering [Construction%20%28Civ2%29](Construction) and [Pottery%20%28Civ2%29](Pottery). In the PlayStation version of the game, it appears under the variant name "Plumber" and shares a [Civilopedia](Civilopedia) entry with [Sanitation%20%28Civ2%29](Sanitation).
