# Theater Square (Civ6)

Strategy.
The Theater Square is your main tool for pursuing a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Cultural Victory). Although the mechanics in "Civilization VI" are a bit different when compared to the previous game, Great Works still provide most of your output. But, as before, without slots where you can place the Great Works, you cannot even produce them! That's where the Theater Square and its buildings come into play.
Of course, this district has another purpose: as the main booster of output of your [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities), it not only helps widen your borders more rapidly, but most importantly it helps you acquire [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](Civics) more quickly! This in turn allows you to tweak your [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) more often, which makes your civilization more versatile.
