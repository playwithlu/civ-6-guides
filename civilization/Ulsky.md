# Ulsky

Ulsky, also known as Ulski, is a [Scythian](Scythian) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it is a rural locality in Zarevskoye Rural Settlement of Shovgenovsky District, the Republic of Adygea in present-day [Russian](Russia).
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the Scythian [capital](capital) in [Rise%20of%20Rome](Rise of Rome).
