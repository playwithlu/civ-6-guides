# Hills Clan (Civ6)

The Hills Clan is a type of [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarian) Clan in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It appears only in the [Barbarian%20Clans%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarian Clans) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode).
Profile.
Hills clans prefer to place their [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29%23Outposts](Outposts) on open [Terrain%20%28Civ6%29](terrain) with [Hills%20%28Civ6%29](hills). They strongly prefer [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Melee](melee units), even to the point of excluding [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Ranged](ranged units) completely.
