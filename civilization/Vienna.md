# Vienna

Vienna, also known as Wien or by its historical name of Vindobona, is the [Austrian](Austrian) [capital](capital) and a [Celtic](Celtic), [German](German), and [Holy%20Roman](Holy Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the capital, largest city, and one of nine States in present-day Austria.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
