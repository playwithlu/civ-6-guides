# Mine (CTP2)

 is a [List%20of%20improvements%20in%20CTP2](tile improvement) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Mines extract ore and precious metals from the earth for use as raw materials. The net effect of Mining is a 5 to 15 (depending on terrain type) increase in on each tile with a Mine on it.
