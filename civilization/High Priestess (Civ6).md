# High Priestess (Civ6)

The High Priestess is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Civilian](civilian unit) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The High Priestess has a single build charge, which she can use to create her [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization's) unique [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](tile improvement): a Sphinx for [Egyptian%20%28Civ6%29](Egypt), a Nubian Pyramid for [Nubian%20%28Civ6%29](Nubia).
