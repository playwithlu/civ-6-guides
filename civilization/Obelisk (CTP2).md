# Obelisk (CTP2)

 is a [List%20of%20improvements%20in%20CTP2](tile improvement) in [Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II).
Gameplay.
Obelisks are one of three elements that make up the [Gaia%20Controller%20%28wonder%29%20%28CTP2%29](Gaia Controller) device. They distribute the energy beamed down from Gaia Satellites to the Controller Cores.
