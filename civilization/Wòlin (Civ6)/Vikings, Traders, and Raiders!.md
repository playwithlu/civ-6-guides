# Wòlin (Civ6)/Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!

Wòlin is a trade [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) from the [Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21%20%28Civ6%29](Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is able to build the [Monastery%20%28Civ6%29](Monastery).
Strategy.
Because the player cannot obtain s in Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!, it is not possible to interact with Wòlin diplomatically. As such, the city-state does not provide any or [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerain) bonuses.
Wòlin is one of the closest city-states to [Swedish%20%28Civ6%29/Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21](Sweden) and [Danish%20%28Civ6%29](Denmark), making them a particularity obvious target for invasion. When playing as Denmark, Wòlin should be one of the first targets for your army. When playing as Sweden, you should probably hold off on attacking Wòlin until you've settled the unclaimed land in Scandinavia and/or taken [Apuol%C4%97%20%28Civ6%29](Apuolė). When playing as [Norwegian%20%28Civ6%29/Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21](Norway), it's best to ignore Wòlin and focus more on the city-states in Great Britain or the [Frankish%20%28Civ6%29](Franks).
