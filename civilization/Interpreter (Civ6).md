# Interpreter (Civ6)

Interpreter is a for the [Missionary%20%28Civ6%29/Path%20to%20Nirvana](Missionary) from the [Path%20to%20Nirvana%20%28Civ6%29](Path to Nirvana) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It requires [Traveler%20%28Civ6%29](Traveler).
Effects.
Gain 75 if this [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit) converts a [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) to your [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](Religion) for the first time.
