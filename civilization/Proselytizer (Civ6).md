# Proselytizer (Civ6)

Proselytizer is a for the [Apostle%20%28Civ6%29](Apostle) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" and the [Monk%20%28Civ6%29](Monk) from the [Path%20to%20Nirvana%20%28Civ6%29](Path to Nirvana) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario).
Effects.
Eliminates 75% of the pressure of other s from the target when it uses Spread Religion.
