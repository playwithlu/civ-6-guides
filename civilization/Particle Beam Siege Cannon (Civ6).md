# Particle Beam Siege Cannon (Civ6)

Particle Beam Siege Cannon is a "" for the [Giant%20Death%20Robot%20%28Civ6%29](Giant Death Robot) in "". Since GDRs cannot gain XP or levels, it is automatically conferred upon every GDR a player owns or builds after researching [Advanced%20Power%20Cells%20%28Civ6%29](Advanced Power Cells).
