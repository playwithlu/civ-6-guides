# Mt. Sinai (Civ5)

Mt. Sinai is a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonder) in "". It spawns in [Desert%20%28Civ5%29](desert) and [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](mountain) tiles.
Strategy.
Mt. Sinai provides a massive bonus, making it invaluable to [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilizations) that focus on [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religion). If they discover it early on, they should make every effort to found a [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) nearby.
Mt. Sinai does not count as a [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](mountain) for the purpose of constructing [Neuschwanstein%20%28Civ5%29](Neuschwanstein) or [Machu%20Picchu%20%28Civ5%29](Machu Picchu).
