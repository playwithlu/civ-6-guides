# Wisselbanken (Civ6)

Wisselbanken is a Diplomatic [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "".
Strategy.
Wisselbanken provides you and your [Diplomacy%20%28Civ6%29%23Alliance](allies) with extra and if you send s to their [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities), and also speeds up the rate at which both of you earn Alliance Points. It's extremely valuable when playing as [Poundmaker%20%28Civ6%29](Poundmaker). With "", this bonus additionally applies to [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states) you are [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerain) of, increasing the versatility of this policy.
