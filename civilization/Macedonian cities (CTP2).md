# Macedonian cities (CTP2)

Macedonian cities are [City%20%28CTP2%29](cities) that can be founded by the [Macedonian%20%28CTP2%29](Macedonian) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)", introduced in [Apolyton%20Edition%20%28CTP2%29](Apolyton Edition). The first city founded is the [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital), and the names of future cities are chosen in the order they appear on the list below.
The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
