# Special Units (C-evo)

"Special Units" in the free-to-play game "[C-evo](C-evo)" are the eight predesigned units listed under that name in the Manual. They and the [Militia%20%28C-evo%29](Militia) are the only [List%20of%20units%20in%20C-evo%23Predesigned%20units](predesigned units). Nations design their own better units after researching the appropriate [Advance%20%28C-evo%29](technologies).
