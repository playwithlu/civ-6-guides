# Ritual (Civ6)

Ritual is the Level 1 title for the [Owls%20of%20Minerva%20%28Civ6%29](Owls of Minerva), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era).
Effects.
Allows you to construct the [Gilded%20Vault%20%28Civ6%29](Gilded Vault) building, a powerful replacement of the [Bank%20%28Civ6%29](Bank).
