# Mt. Kilimanjaro (Civ5)

Mt. Kilimanjaro is a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonder) in "". It spawns in [Grassland%20%28Civ5%29](Grassland), [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](Plains), and [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](Mountain) tiles.
Strategy.
Like the [Fountain%20of%20Youth%20%28Civ5%29](Fountain of Youth), Mt. Kilimanjaro offers a benefit to all [Military%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](military units) that move adjacent to it: a [Promotions%20%28Civ5%29](promotion) that allows double speed through [Hill%20%28Civ5%29](Hills) and a combat bonus on Hills for the rest of the game! When a [Scout%20%28Civ5%29](Scout) receives this promotion it will move faster on Hills than on regular tiles. [Worker%20%28Civ5%29](Workers) and [Great%20General%20%28Civ5%29](Great Generals) cannot get this promotion, since they aren't classified as military units.
Mt. Kilimanjaro counts as a Mountain for the purpose of building [Observatory%20%28Civ5%29](Observatories).
