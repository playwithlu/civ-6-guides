# Russian (Civ2)

The Russian people represent Russia, a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ2](default civilization) in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)".
Color.
The Russians are a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ2](white civilization). A game including the Russians will not involve the s, [Celtic%20%28Civ2%29](Celts), or [Inca%20%28Civ2%29](Incas).
Leaders.
The default male leader of the Russians is [Lenin](Lenin), the alias of Vladimir Ulyanov (1870–1924), the [Communism%20%28government%29%20%28Civ2%29](communist) revolutionary and politician who overthrew the [Democracy%20%28government%29%20%28Civ2%29](democratic) government that overthrew in 1917 during the chaos of the . As Russia was not remotely industrialized enough to be suitable for traditional Marxism, Lenin advocated that involved a vanguard communist party exerting dictatorial powers to force the country's further development. Experiments with were undertaken but abandoned in favor of full state planning.
The default female leader is [Catherine%20the%20Great](Catherine the Great) (1729–1796), a princess who overthrew and presided over a Russian golden age, as policies and rapid military conquests were undertaken by noble favorites including , , , and . Tblisi and Tiflis are both names for the same city, now spelled [Tbilisi](Tbilisi); Tiflis also appears as an [Arab%20%28Civ2%29](Arab) city when that civilization is [Modding%20%28Civ2%29](modded) into the game. Several cities retain their Soviet-era names (Sverdlovsk for Yekaterinburg, Kuibyshev for Samara, Kalinin for Tver, Stalingrad for Volgograd, Gorki for Nizhny Novgorod, Voroshilovgrad for Lugansk/Luhansk), even though Civ 1’s Leningrad is properly restored as St. Petersburg.
