# World War II (Civ6)

World War II is a [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) from the [Outback%20Tycoon%20%28Civ6%29](Outback Tycoon) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
World War II is essentially a "turn wall" because of its insane cost. Once one progresses to {{TurnIcon6}} Turn 53, an {{Inspiration6}} is applied, reducing its {{Culture6}} cost by 99%.
