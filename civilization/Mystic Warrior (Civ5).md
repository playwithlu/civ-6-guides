# Mystic Warrior (Civ5)

The Mystic Warrior is a special military unit from the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World Deluxe) [List%20of%20Civilization%20V%20scenarios](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Melee unit with special abilities like those of the [Impi%20%28Civ5%29](Impi) and the [Kris%20swordsman%20%28Civ5%29](Kris Swordsman). Available only through [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](City-States).
Strategy.
Before engaging in melee, the Mystic Warrior performs a spear throw attack that can damage or kill the enemy unit before making contact. And after one combat the Mystic Warrior will receive another promotion. The only way to obtain one is by allying with a Militaristic [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](City-State).
Note that an upgraded Mystic Warrior loses Spear Throw, but retains Mystic Blade or the special promotion that it received.
