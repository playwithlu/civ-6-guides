# Tbilisi

Tbilisi, also known as Tblisi or Tiflis, is the [Georgian](Georgian) [capital](capital) and an [Arabian](Arabian), [Mongol](Mongol)ian, and [Russian](Russian) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it is the capital of and the largest city in present-day Georgia.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Scythian](Scythian) city in [Rise%20of%20Rome](Rise of Rome).
