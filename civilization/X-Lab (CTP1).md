Great Library entry.
When the first alien artifacts were uncovered on Mars, most of the world's major governments began secret research in the fields of alien biology and communication. New installations were constructed to house specially secured laboratories. The locations of such installations are always a closely guarded secret, so secret that the scientists working inside are forbidden to leave for any reason. The cost of a Xenoform Laboratory is exorbitant, but the potential rewards are unbounded.
