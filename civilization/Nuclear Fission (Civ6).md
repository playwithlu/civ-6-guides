# Nuclear Fission (Civ6)

Nuclear Fission is an [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)." 
Strategy.
Breaking up the atom brings the next revolution - which, unfortunately, also could bring the end of the world. When the first atomic explosions are achieved in a lab, the scientists get both excited and scared to death...those that survive, that is. And thus the first weapons of mass destruction are born. Your [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) can now work to develop these weapons and to start producing them, to the terror of all your foes.
Discovering Nuclear Fission triggers the for [Cold%20War%20%28Civ6%29](Cold War).
