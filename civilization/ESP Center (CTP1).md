Great Library entry.
In the 2800's the work of alien archeologists came to fruition leading to an explosion of new research, particularly in ESP. By 2915 the first batch of engineered Adepts succeeded in identifying colors and smells in the minds of test subjects. By batch 3.01, Adepts successfully picked sentences from targeted thinkers. The race for ESP power was on.
