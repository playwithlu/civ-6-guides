# Renaissance (CivWorld)

 is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Wonder%20Events](Wonder Event) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It immediately upgrades one of each type of Small-sized [Building%20%28CivWorld%29](building) owned by each member of the [Civilizations%20%28CivWorld%29](Civilization) to Modest size.
