# Patriotic War (Civ6)

Patriotic War is a Military [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
In "", Patriotic War is removed from the game and its effects are transferred to [Defense%20of%20the%20Motherland%20%28Civ6%29](Defense of the Motherland).
Strategy.
In [vanilla](vanilla) "Civilization VI" and "", Patriotic War is the only policy that provides a bonus for [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Support](support units). It's helpful if you have immediate need of [Supply%20Convoy%20%28Civ6%29](Supply Convoys) and [Drone%20%28Civ6%29](Drones) to support your ground forces or [Anti-Air%20Gun%20%28Civ6%29](Anti-Air Guns) and [Mobile%20SAM%20%28Civ6%29](Mobile SAMs) to repel an enemy air assault.
