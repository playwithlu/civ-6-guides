# Paratrooper (Civ4)

Strategy.
The Paratrooper is a late-game [Gunpowder%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](gunpowder unit) that is able to Paradrop into any visible [Tile%20%28Civ4%29](tile) unoccupied by enemy units within its range. Watch for enemy [Air%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](aircraft), as Paratroopers on a Paradrop mission can be [Intercept%20%28Civ4%29](intercepted).
