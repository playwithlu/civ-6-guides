Strategy.
The Wheel allows your [Worker%20%28Civ4%29](Workers) to build [Roads%20and%20railroads%20%28Civ4%29](Roads), which enable trade routes and increase unit movement. Also, if you have [Horse%20%28Civ4%29](Horses), the Wheel enables you to build fast-moving [Chariot%20%28Civ4%29](Chariot) units.
