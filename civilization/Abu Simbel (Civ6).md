# Abu Simbel (Civ6)

Abu Simbel is a Diplomatic [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Abu Simbel doubles the output of [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Site) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](buildings), allowing for faster discovery of [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civics).
