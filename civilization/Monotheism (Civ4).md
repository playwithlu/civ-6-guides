# Monotheism (Civ4)

Strategy.
The first civilization to research Monotheism founds [Judaism%20%28Civ4%29](Judaism). It also enables the [Organized%20Religion%20%28Civ4%29](Organized Religion) [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic), which increases production of buildings in cities with your state religion and enables [Missionaries](Missionaries) to be built in any city with a religion.
