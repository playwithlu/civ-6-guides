# Temple of Solomon (Civ4)

Strategy.
The Temple of Solomon (Jewish [Religious%20shrine%20%28Civ4%29](Shrine)) produces one gold each turn for every city in the world that practices [Judaism%20%28Civ4%29](Judaism). It also spreads Judaism throughout the world and increases its city's chance of generating a [Great%20Prophet%20%28Civ4%29](Great Prophet).
