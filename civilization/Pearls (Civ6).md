# Pearls (Civ6)

 Pearls are a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are found in [Coast%20%28Civ6%29](Coast) and [Lake%20%28Civ6%29](Lake) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Strategy.
 Pearls are one of four water-based luxury resources (after the addition of and ), as well as one of a handful that provide when worked. With [Fishing%20Boats%20%28Civ6%29](Fishing Boats) and the appropriate [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technologies), they also provide some decent and bonuses. If [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](religion) is an important part of your strategy, don't hesitate to claim any Pearls you find.
