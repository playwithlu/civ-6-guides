# Ugarit

Ugarit is a [Hittite](Hittite) and [Phoenician](Phoenician) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and an [Assyrian](Assyrian) city in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games), and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it was an ancient port city on the outskirts of Latakia in northern present-day Syria.
