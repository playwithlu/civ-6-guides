# We Love the King Day (Civ1)

We Love the King Day is special event in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)". It can occur in any [City%20%28Civ1%29](city) of size 3 and greater with no unhappy citizens and an equal or greater number of citizens that are happy compared to content citizens. [Specialists%20%28Civ1%29](Specialists) count as content citizens.
