# Science (CivBE)

 Science is one of the tile yields in "". It represents the technological advancement of your [Colony%20%28CivBE%29](colony).
Sources.
 Science behaves as a yield and is also generated from [Building%20%28CivBE%29](buildings), [Tile%20improvement%20%28CivBE%29](tile improvements), [Trade%20routes%20%28CivBE%29](trade), [Expedition%20%28CivBE%29](expeditions), [Virtues%20%28CivBE%29](virtues), and [Quests%20%28CivBE%29](quests).
Uses.
 Science is used solely to research Technologies from the [Tech%20Web%20%28CivBE%29](Tech web). 
Unlike "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)", you can also trade raw Science with other players.
