# Zombie Defense (Civ6)

Zombie Defense is the eighth [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in [Civilization%20VI]("Civilization VI"), introduced in the [Portugal%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Portugal Pack). It adds a survival minigame in the form of a zombie apocalypse: dead units will not stay dead forever, they will all resurrect as Zombies and attack civilizations. Players are equipped with new tools, together with modified tile improvements and city projects, to fend off the zombie threats. 
Contents.
In this optional game mode, the dead don’t stay dead for long and present an ever-growing threat to the world’s civilizations.
