# Orbital Engineering (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Orbital Engineering allows the construction of the one-shot Alpha Anti-Ship Missile and the immobile weapons platform Starbase Units. The first player to discover this tech also founds the "Wealth" Value, giving additional income.
