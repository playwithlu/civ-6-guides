# Samoan (Civ5)

The Samoan people represent a playable civilization in [Paradise%20Found%20%28Civ5%29](Paradise Found). They are led by [Malietoa%20Savea%20%28Civ5%29](Malietoa Savea), a reused [Gandhi%20%28Civ5%29](Gandhi). Their symbol is a white spiral with a dark blue background.
Unique Ability.
Heart of Polynesia - from number of s reduced by 25%.
City Names.
Apia, Pago Pago, Tafuna, Nu Uuli, Salelologa, Vaitele, Faleasi U, Vailele, Le Auva A, Fale Ula, Malie, Siusega, Leone, Ili Ili, Pava Ia I, Aua
