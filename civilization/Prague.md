# Prague

Prague is a [Holy%20Roman](Holy Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and a [Slavic](Slavic) city in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games), and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the [capital](capital) of and largest city in the present-day Czech Republic.
