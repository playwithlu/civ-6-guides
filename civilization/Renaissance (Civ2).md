# Renaissance (Civ2)

The Renaissance is one of the [Era%20%28Civ2%29](eras) in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)" and its expansions. It is the second era in the game, being preceded by the [Ancient%20World%20%28Civ2%29](Ancient World) and followed by the [Industrial%20Revolution%20%28Civ2%29](Industrial Revolution).
The Renaissance also represents the High Middle Ages.
