# Stanisław Potocki (Civ6)

Stanisław Potocki is a playable [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) from the [Jadwiga%27s%20Legacy%20%28Civ6%29](Jadwiga's Legacy) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". He leads the [Polish%20%28Civ6%29/Jadwiga%27s%20Legacy](Poles).
In-Game.
Stanisław Potocki's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Big Stick Policy. He likes civilizations that have a city on his home [Continent%20%28Civ6%29](continent) and remain peaceful, while hates those that start wars against a city-state or civilization in his home continent.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Rotmistrz. Gives him a second [Knight%20%28Civ6%29](Knight) or [Winged%20Hussar%20%28Civ6%29](Winged Hussar) for each one he trains.
