# Satellite Broadcasts (Civ6)

Satellite Broadcasts is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Satellite Broadcasts triples the output of [Great%20Work%20%28Civ6%29%23Great%20Works%20of%20Music](Great Works of Music). All [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory) seekers will benefit from this policy, but the [American%20%28Civ6%29](Americans) should definitely reserve a slot in their [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) for it to fully leverage the bonuses of their [Film%20Studio%20%28Civ6%29](Film Studio).
