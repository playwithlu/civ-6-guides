# Nader Shah (Civ6)

Nader Shah (August 1688 – 19 June 1747) was the founder and first of the of . His strategic brilliance and extensive greatly expanded the Iranian empire, which earned him epithets such as "the second [Alexander%20the%20Great](Alexander)" and "the [Napoleon%20Bonaparte](Napoleon) of Persia." He leads the [Persian%20%28Civ6%29](Persians) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Fight opposing [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](Civilizations) with Nader Shah’s combat bonus against full [Hit%20points](Health) [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) and bask in the wealth from Persia’s Domestic s.
Intro.
 Shah of Shahs, who rose from bondage to become the wealthiest king in the world, we call to you! Sweep your forces across the land, and, with fire and steel, set the world alight once more, that we may better see the gold’s shine by the flames’ glow.
In-Game.
Nader Shah's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Jazayerchi. He likes civilizations with a high number of [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Land%20military](land units) and dislikes civilizations with a low number of land units.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Sword of Persia. His [Military%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](military units) receive +5 Combat Strength when attacking full health enemy units and conquered [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) yield +2 and +3 on domestic s sent from them.
Detailed Approach.
Nader Shah should go for [Iron%20Working%20%28Civ6%29](Iron Working) and [Political%20Philosophy%20%28Civ6%29](Political Philosophy) right out of the gate. Iron Working gives him access to his powerful [Immortal%20%28Civ6%29](Immortals). These Immortals can crush their opponents with their Ranged Attack and the +5 Combat Strength against full Health opponents. On the way towards Political Philosophy, you will unlock [Foreign%20Trade%20%28Civ6%29](Foreign Trade) and [Early%20Empire%20%28Civ6%29](Early Empire). The first of these, Foreign Trade, will give you access to s which receive benefits from both Nader Shah and Persia’s abilities. After Foreign Trade you will unlock the [Pairidaeza%20%28Civ6%29](Pairidaeza) [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](improvements) which are great for and . Once you unlock Political Philosophy, an extra is your reward. The two [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victories) Persia is best at are [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture) with their Pairidaezas or [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination) with their Immortals.
