# Opera House (Civ5)

Game Info.
Renaissance Era culture building. Requires [Amphitheater%20%28Civ5%29](Amphitheater).
Strategy.
The Opera House is a mid-level building of the Renaissance Era that increases a city's culture. It adds another Specialist slot for the culture-oriented empires to utilize (or 1 Great Work of Music slot in "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)"). 
You need an Opera House in each city in order to unlock the [Hermitage%20%28Civ5%29](Hermitage).
