# Tank (Civ4)

The Tank is replaced by the following [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique units):
Strategy.
The Tank is a magnificent offensive weapon. It has a movement of 2 and can attack twice a turn. Tanks can hunt down and destroy pre-modern-era units at will, but receive no [Terrain%20%28Civ4%29](terrain) bonuses when defending.
