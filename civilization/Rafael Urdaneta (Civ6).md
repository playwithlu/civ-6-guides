# Rafael Urdaneta (Civ6)

Rafael Urdaneta is a [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to land units within 2 s.
Urdaneta can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to reset the movement and attacks of each of the player's land combat [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) within 2 tiles.
