# Viking (Civ4)

The Viking people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)".
Strategy.
The Vikings are good at aggressively conquering rivals with the help of their [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ4%23Unique%20units](unique unit), the [Berserker%20%28Civ4%29](Berserker), when grouped and attacking [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities). A [Trading%20Post%20%28Civ4%29](Trading Post) in each [Coastal%20%28Civ4%29](coastal) city gets additional gold across. The Berserkers are further strengthened by already being promoted due to [Ragnar%20%28Civ4%29](Ragnar's) "[Aggressive%20%28Civ4%29](Aggressive)" [Leader%20trait%20%28Civ4%29](trait).
