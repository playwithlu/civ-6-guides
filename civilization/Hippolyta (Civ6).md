# Hippolyta (Civ6)

Hippolyta is a [Heroes%20%26amp%3B%20Legends%20%28Civ6%29%23Heroes](Hero) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". She is exclusive to the [Heroes%20%26amp%3B%20Legends%20%28Civ6%29](Heroes &amp; Legends) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode), introduced in the [Babylon%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Babylon Pack).
Strategy.
Like all Hero units, Hippolyta's Strength increases with the progression of the [Era%20%28Civ6%29](eras), as shown in the table below.
Among all the Heroes with active abilities, Hippolyta's Command is the only ability that does not cost any charges. This makes her an excellent frontline and support Hero with her healing ability.
