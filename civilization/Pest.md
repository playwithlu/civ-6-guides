# Pest

Pest is a [Hungarian](Hungarian) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it is the eastern, mostly flat part of [Budapest](Budapest) in present-day Hungary, comprising about two-thirds of the city's territory.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the Hungarian [capital](capital) in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
