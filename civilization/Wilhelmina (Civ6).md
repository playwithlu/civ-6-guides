# Wilhelmina (Civ6)

Wilhelmina (31 August 1880 – 28 November 1962) was from 1890 until her abdication in 1948, the longest reign of any Dutch monarch, and is best remembered for her staunch support of the during the . She leads the [Dutch%20%28Civ6%29](Dutch) in "".
Wilhelmina's Dutch empire is at home on the flatlands where [River%20%28Civ6%29](rivers) run into the sea, building polders to fuel their growth and trade routes to span the globe.
Intro.
 Queen Wilhelmina, you will defend the Netherlands to the last. Turn coastland to farmland with Dutch ingenuity. Your citizens will farm with great efficiency, knowing that they are under the protection of your mighty warships. Above all, trade with everyone you meet. A good deal can turn a foe into a friend—and if not, the money will fund your opposition.
In-Game.
Wilhelmina's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Billionaire. She tries to have as many s as possible, likes civilizations that send s to her [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) and dislikes civilizations that do not send s to her cities.
Her [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Radio Oranje. It provides +2 [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](Loyalty) per turn to cities that send domestic s and +2 to international s that are sent to and from the Netherlands.
Detailed Approach.
The Dutch will want to scout out the coastline near their start position to find rivers and natural bays within which to build their empire. Build a [Harbor%20%28Civ6%29](Harbor) in each [Coastal%20%28Civ6%29](coastal) city to grab extra [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) for your use and carefully plan which water tiles can be reclaimed by your [Polder%20%28Civ6%29](Polders). Then build along the shores of your great rivers to maximize your , and . Your plentiful s will then finish the job by maintaining Loyalty in your cities, by providing from foreign lands, and by amassing enough for you to win by any [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victory).
