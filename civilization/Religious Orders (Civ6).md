# Religious Orders (Civ6)

Religious Orders is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The window of opportunity for a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Religion](Religious Victory) is narrower than any of the other victory conditions, and Religious Orders will help players who are striving to achieve one before the window closes. In conjunction with [Theocracy%20%28Civ6%29](Theocracy), it provides all [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Religious](religious units) with +10 Religious Strength, which will give them a substantial advantage in [Theological%20Combat%20%28Civ6%29](Theological Combat).
