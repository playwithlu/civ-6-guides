# Zhou Daguan (Civ6)

Zhou Daguan is a [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchant) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". He was added in the [Babylon%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Babylon Pack).
Unique ability.
Grants 3 s at this [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](City-state).
Strategy.
Zhou Daguan is best utilized in the [Monopolies%20and%20Corporations%20%28Civ6%29](Monopolies and Corporations) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) to create a [Corporation%20%28Civ6%29](Corporation) without losing out on a high-impact ability. Outside of this mode, he should be used on city-states that are 3 or fewer s away from switching their [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerainty) to you.
