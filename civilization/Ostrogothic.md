# Ostrogothic

The Ostrogothic people, also known as the Ostrogoths, represent the Ostrogothic Kingdom, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. Their [capital](capital) is [Reidgotaland](Reidgotaland), and their [Leaders](leader) is [Theodoric%20the%20Great](Theodoric the Great).
