# Spaceship Fuel (CivRev)

The SS Fuel is a [Building%20%28CivRev%29](building) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Gameplay.
Spaceship Fuel is one of the four essential pieces needed to construct a [Spaceship%20%28CivRev%29](Spaceship) and win a [Victory%20%28CivRev%29](Technology Victory)! Building extra Fuel will make your Spaceship complete its journey faster!
Civilopedia entry.
A spaceship must create a massive amount of thrust to push several tons of equipment and personnel into the stars. Dense and highly-radioactive materials will be the fuel of choice for the first generation of colonizing vessels.
