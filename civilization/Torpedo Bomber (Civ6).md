# Torpedo Bomber (Civ6)

Torpedo Bomber is a Tier II for [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Air%20Bomber](air bomber units) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It requires [Box%20Formation%20%28Civ6%29](Box Formation) or [Evasive%20Maneuvers%20%28Civ6%29](Evasive Maneuvers).
Effects.
+17 Combat Strength against naval units.
