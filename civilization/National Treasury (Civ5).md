# National Treasury (Civ5)

Game Info.
Commercial national wonder. Requires [Market%20%28Civ5%29](Market) in all [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities).
Strategy.
The National Treasury was added to "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" as part of the [December%202010%20patch%20%28Civ5%29](December 2010 patch). Later, it was removed in the "" expansion, and replaced with the [East%20India%20Company%20%28Civ5%29](East India Company).
