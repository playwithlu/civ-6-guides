# Silk (Civ6)

 Silk is a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is found in [Woods%20%28Civ6%29](Woods).
Strategy.
 Silk is one of a handful of resources that provides when worked. It also provides and with a [Plantation%20%28Civ6%29](Plantation) and the proper [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ6](technological research).
