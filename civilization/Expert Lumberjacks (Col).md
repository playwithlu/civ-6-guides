# Expert Lumberjacks (Col)

The Expert Lumberjack increases [Lumber%20%28Col%29](Lumber) production on forest [Terrain%20%28Col%29](tiles). A [Free%20Colonist%20%28Col%29](Free Colonist) may become an Expert Lumberjack through work experience. Lumberjacks may also be trained in [Europe](Europe) for 700 [gold%20%28Col%29](gold).
Lumberjacks may teach their skills in a [Schoolhouse%20%28Col%29](Schoolhouse), [College%20%28Col%29](College) or [University%20%28Col%29](University).
