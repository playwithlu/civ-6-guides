# Optics (Civ5)

Game Info.
Optics, the development of tools that allow humans to see much farther away than normally possible, enables a number of technological advancements in seafaring. The most important of them is [Embarkation%20%28Civ5%29](embarkation), which allows land [Unit%20%28Civ5%29](units) to travel in the sea.
Optics also allows construction of the [Lighthouse%20%28Civ5%29](Lighthouse), an important [Building%20%28Civ5%29](building) for [Coastal%20%28Civ5%29](coastal) [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities). If you are playing as the [Venetian%20%28Civ5%29](Venetian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization), it spawns a [Merchant%20of%20Venice%20%28Civ5%29](Merchant of Venice) upon discovery.
