# War Chariot (Civ4)

The War Chariot is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [Egyptian%20%28Civ4%29](Egyptian) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the standard [Chariot%20%28Civ4%29](Chariot) it replaces, the War Chariot has +1 strength and is immune to [First%20strike%20%28Civ4%29](first strikes).
The War Chariot is the most effective pre-Medieval unit at taking down [Axeman%20%28Civ4%29](Axemen), and provides a fast, effective defense. Aside from that, due to their fast speed and immunity to first strikes, War Chariots are often used in early-game rushes, or even in fake rushes to force opponents into a defensive move.
