# Organized (Civ4)

"Organized" is one of the [Leader%20trait%20%28Civ4%29](leader traits). Organized leaders' [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic) upkeep is reduced by 50 percent. They also get double production speed for the [Lighthouse%20%28Civ4%29](Lighthouse), [Courthouse%20%28Civ4%29](Courthouse), and the [Factory%20%28Civ4%29](Factory).
