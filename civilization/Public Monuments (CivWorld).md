# Public Monuments (CivWorld)

 is a [Civic%20%28CivWorld%29](Civic) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 50% bonus to Culture output and a 25% penalty to Food output. It can be adopted after discovering [Construction%20%28CivWorld%29](Construction) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Democracy%20%28CivWorld%29](Democracy).
