# Oxford University (Civ5)

Game Info.
Scientific national wonder. Requires [University%20%28Civ5%29](University) in all [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities).
Strategy.
Oxford University is the next step for your national scientific development. As with other buildings that provide free [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ5](techs), the bonus might become really useful, or not that much (depending on your timing). Also, use the opportunity to get some more and by filling the [Great%20Work%20%28Civ5%29](Great Work) slots.
