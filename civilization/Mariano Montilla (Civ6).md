# Mariano Montilla (Civ6)

Mariano Montilla is a [Comandante%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Comandante General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to land units within 2 s.
Montilla can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to grant a bonus +4 Combat Strength when fighting [Districts%20%28Civ6%29](district) defenses to all units within 2 tiles.
