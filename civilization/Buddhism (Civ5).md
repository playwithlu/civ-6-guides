# Buddhism (Civ5)

Buddhism is one of thirteen eligible [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religions) featured in the ' and ' expansions for "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Unlike [Buddhism%20%28Civ4%29](the religion) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)", this religion has no technological requirements to be unlocked and neither unique buildings nor a unique missionary unit. However, the [Pagoda%20%28Civ5%29](Pagoda) religious building may be said to be close in spirit to Buddhism, although there is no gameplay relation of any kind between the two. Like all other religions, Buddhism can be founded directly after using a [Great%20Prophet%20%28Civ5%29](Great Prophet's) Found Religion ability and only one [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) can be the founder of this religion.
