# Han (CTP2)

The Han people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)", introduced in [Apolyton%20Edition%20%28CTP2%29](Apolyton Edition). Their default leader names are Liu Bang for a male and Feng for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Chang%27an](Chang'an).
