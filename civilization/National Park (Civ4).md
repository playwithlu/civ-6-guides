# National Park (Civ4)

Strategy.
Build the National Park to eliminate all [Health%20%28Civ4%29](unhealthiness) from [Population%20%28Civ4%29](population) in your [City%20%28Civ4%29](city). It also provides a free [Specialists%20%28Civ4%29](specialist) for each [Forest%20Preserve%20%28Civ4%29](Forest Preserve) in its city radius and increases the chances that a [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ4%29](Great Scientist) might appear in your city.
