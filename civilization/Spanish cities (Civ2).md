# Spanish cities (Civ2)

The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
List of cities.
The [Roman%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Roman city names) Hispalis and Caesaraugusta are equivalent to Seville and Saragossa. The [Carthaginian%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Carthaginian) Gades, Malaca and Carthago Nova are equivalent to Cadiz, Malaga and Cartagena. Cordoba is also repeated in the [Arab%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Arab list).
