# Mycenaean

The {{PAGENAME}} people, also known as the Mycenians, represent Mycenaean Greece, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. Their [capital](capital) is [Mycenae%20%28city%29](Mycenae), and their [Leaders](leader) is [Agamemnon](Agamemnon). To date, they have appeared only in [Scenario](scenarios).
