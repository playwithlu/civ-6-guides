# Napoleon Bonaparte (Civ6)

Napoleon Bonaparte is an [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Industrial and Modern era land units within 2 s.
Napoleon Bonaparte can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to form an [Unit%20%28Civ6%29%23Combining%20units](Army) out of a military land [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit).
