Strategy.
The Water Park serves the same purpose as an Entertainment Complex, with three key differences: it is built on the water instead of the land, the bonuses of its buildings extend farther, and its buildings provide additional bonuses to and (prior to the , in which Entertainment Complex buildings receive similar bonuses). It's particularly useful for seafaring civilizations like [English%20%28Civ6%29](England) and [Indonesian%20%28Civ6%29](Indonesia), who tend to settle on foreign [Continent%20%28Civ6%29](continents) and remote islands and may have difficulty maintaining the Loyalty of their cities as they grow. Water Parks are also a great way to get usage out of small lakes within your borders.
