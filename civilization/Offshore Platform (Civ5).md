# Offshore Platform (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced sea improvement. Requires [Refrigeration%20%28Civ5%29](Refrigeration).
Strategy.
Like the land-based [Oil%20Well%20%28Civ5%29](Oil Well), the Offshore Platform grants access to water-based sources of and gives a large bonus to nearby [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities). Notably, it requires [Refrigeration%20%28Civ5%29](Refrigeration) on the edge of the [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Modern Era) instead of [Biology%20%28Civ5%29](Biology), meaning these sources of are connected slightly later than those on land. Constructing it requires one [Work%20Boat%20%28Civ5%29](Work Boat).
