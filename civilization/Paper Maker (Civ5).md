# Paper Maker (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20building%20%28Civ5%29](Unique building) of the [Chinese%20%28Civ5%29](Chinese) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Library%20%28Civ5%29](Library).
Strategy.
The Paper Maker is extremely valuable for the Chinese at the start of the game. It costs nothing to maintain, unlike the Library it replaces; in fact it produces 2 instead, allowing the Chinese to boost their output early on. Research [Writing%20%28Civ5%29](Writing) ASAP, then build Paper Makers ASAP in every [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) you found to jump-start your and production.
