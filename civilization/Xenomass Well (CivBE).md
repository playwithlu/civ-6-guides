# Xenomass Well (CivBE)

Xenomass Well is a [Tile%20improvement%20%28CivBE%29](Tile Improvement) that requires the research of Alien Sciences.  It is buildable by [Worker%20%28CivBE%29](workers) on deposits of [Xenomass](Xenomass) and provides the underlying quantity of the Xenomass [Strategic%20resource%20%28CivBE%29](strategic resource), as well as yields +2  [Food%20%28CivBE%29](Food).  An interesting note about Xenomass Wells is that building them does not require removing [Forest%20%28CivBE%29](forest) from the tile.
Technology: [Alien%20Sciences%20%28CivBE%29](Alien Sciences)
Yields: +2  [Food%20%28CivBE%29](Food)
Maintenance: 2  [Energy%20%28CivBE%29](Energy)
