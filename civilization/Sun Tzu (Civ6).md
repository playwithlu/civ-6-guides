# Sun Tzu (Civ6)

Sun Tzu is a [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Classical and Medieval era land units within 2 s.
Sun Tzu can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) at a or with an available slot to create "The Art of War", a Great Work of Writing that provides +4 and +4 .
