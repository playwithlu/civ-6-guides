# Michael VIII (Civ5)

Michael VIII is a non-playable [Leaders%20%28Civ5%29](leader) from the [Rise%20of%20the%20Mongols%20%28Civ5%29](Rise of the Mongols) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". He leads the [Byzantine%20%28Civ5%29/Rise%20of%20the%20Mongols](Byzantines). He is a reused [Alexander%20%28Civ5%29](Alexander).
Unique Ability.
Hellenic League - City-State degrades at half and recovers at twice the normal rate.
