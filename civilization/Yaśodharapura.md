# Yaśodharapura

Yaśodharapura, also known as Angkor, is the [Khmer](Khmer) [capital](capital) in "" and the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games), and a Khmer [city](city) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". In real life, it was a city established by King Yasovarman I late in the 9th century, and is home to [Angkor%20Wat](Angkor Wat).
