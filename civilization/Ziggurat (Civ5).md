# Ziggurat (Civ5)

Game Info.
Unique building of the Sumerian civilization in the [Wonders%20of%20the%20Ancient%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Wonders of the Ancient World) [List%20of%20Civilization%20V%20scenarios](scenario). Replaces the [Temple%20%28Civ5%29](Temple). Requires [Monument%20%28Civ5%29](Monument).
Strategy.
The Ziggurat is the Sumerian unique building, replacing the [Temple%20%28Civ5%29](Temple). The Ziggurat increases the {{Culture5}} of a city, speeding the growth of its borders and the civilization's acquisition of [Social%20policies%20%28Civ5%29](Social Policies). The Ziggurat also provides a {{Science5}} bonus.
