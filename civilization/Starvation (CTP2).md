# Starvation (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
The more people there are in a , the more the city must produce to feed them. For each turn a city fails to produce enough food, the city starves. Each city can endure a state of starvation for up to three turns. After three turns, the decreases by one.
