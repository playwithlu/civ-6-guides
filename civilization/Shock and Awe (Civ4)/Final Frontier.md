# Shock and Awe (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Shock and Awe allows the construction of the very powerful but expensive Alpha Battleship Unit, and also enables the Police State Policy, which benefits civs which engage in war or Espionage.
