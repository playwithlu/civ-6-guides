# Soren Johnson

Soren Johnson (born May 23, 1976) is a video game designer and programmer who worked for [Firaxis%20Games](Firaxis Games) from 2000 to 2007. He served as the lead designer for "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)" and helped design several of the company's other popular games, as well as "". In 2013, he co-founded Mohawk Games.
