# Philadelphia

Philadelphia is an [American](American) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and a [New%20England%20%28Civ4Col%29](New English) city in "". In real life, it is a former U.S. [capital](capital), the largest city in present-day Pennsylvania, and home to the [Liberty%20Bell](Liberty Bell).
