# Reef (Civ6)

A Reef is a [Terrain%20%28Civ6%29%23Features](terrain feature) in "". It is found exclusively on [Coast%20%28Civ6%29](Coast) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Strategy.
Reefs are an aquatic terrain feature found along the coastline. They block the construction of [Harbor%20%28Civ6%29](Harbors), [Water%20Park%20%28Civ6%29](Water Parks), and [Wonder%20%28Civ6%29](wonders), but are otherwise very valuable on their own. They increase the and potential of their tile and provide a Combat Strength bonus to [Naval%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) defending there. They can also contain and and thus accept the [Fishing%20Boats%20%28Civ6%29](Fishing Boats) [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](improvement), which makes them even more valuable.
After fully developing the Harbor of a nearby [City%20%28Civ6%29](city), a Reef tile will yield +3 , +1 , and +5 , as well as +1 if you also have a Water Park with an Aquarium.
Reefs provide a +2 [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonus) for [Campus%20%28Civ6%29](Campuses).
