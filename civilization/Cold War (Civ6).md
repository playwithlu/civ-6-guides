# Cold War (Civ6)

Cold War is an [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era) [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be [Boost%20%28Civ6%29](boosted) by researching [Nuclear%20Fission%20%28Civ6%29](Nuclear Fission).
Strategy.
Discovering Cold War provides several benefits. It allows an empire to support an additional [Spy%20%28Civ6%29](Spy) and unlocks [Cryptography%20%28Civ6%29](Cryptography), a Diplomatic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) that enables more efficient (protection from) [Espionage%20%28Civ6%29](espionage). It also unlocks three other useful Policy Cards: [Containment%20%28Civ6%29](Containment), which helps one displace the [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerains) of [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states) who have a different form of [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government); [International%20Waters%20%28Civ6%29](International Waters), which gives bonuses toward late-game [Naval%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](naval units); and [Second%20Strike%20Capability%20%28Civ6%29](Second Strike Capability), which reduces the maintenance cost of [Nuclear%20weapons%20%28Civ6%29](nuclear weapons).
