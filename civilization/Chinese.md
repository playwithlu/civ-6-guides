# Chinese

The Chinese people represent China, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)". Their [capital](capital) might be [Beijing](Beijing), [Luoyang](Luoyang), or [Xi%27an](Xi'an), and they can be led by [Kublai%20Khan](Kublai Khan), [Mao%20Zedong](Mao Zedong) , [Qin%20Shi%20Huang](Qin Shi Huang), [Sun%20Tzu](Sun Tzu), [Taizong%20of%20Tang](Taizong of Tang), [Wu%20Zetian](Wu Zetian), or [Yongle](Yongle).
The [Han](Han), [Jin](Jin), [Manchurian](Manchurian), and [Shang](Shang) people may also be represented by the Chinese.
The Chinese people can also represent the People's Republic of China and the Republic of China.
