# Gdańsk

Gdańsk is a [Polish](Polish) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the capital and largest city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship, on the Baltic coast in northern present-day Poland.
