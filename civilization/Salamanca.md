# Salamanca

Salamanca is the [Iroquois](Iroquois) [capital](capital) in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)" and a Iroquois and [Spanish](Spanish) [city](city) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. It refers to several places in real life:
Salamanca, New York it's a city in Cattaraugus County, New York, United States, inside the Allegany Indian Reservation, one of two governed by the Seneca Nation of New York (which is one of the six tribes of the Iroquois Confederacy).
Salamanca, Spain is the capital of the Province of Salamanca in the autonomous community of Castile and León in present-day Spain.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Castilian](Castilian) city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
