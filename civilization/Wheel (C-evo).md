# Wheel (C-evo)

The [Wheel](Wheel) is often the first [Advance%20%28C-evo%29](advance) a [C-evo](C-evo) nation will seek, because it gives an immediate increase in the speed at which advances are discovered as soon as you have built any on a , , , , or even tile that is being worked. 
It can usually be partly obtained in a technology exchange with the first nation you befriend if you have researched something else such as .
The effect is doubled when you build a [Superhighways%20%28C-evo%29](Superhighways) system, but that will provide no benefit under your initial , [Despotism%20%28C-evo%29](Despotism).
The Wheel also has military and movement significance in the advances that it leads to.
