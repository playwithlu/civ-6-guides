# Submarine (CivRev)

The Submarine is a [Naval%20unit%20%28CivRev%29](naval unit) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". It is similar to land-based artillery units in that its attack is much greater than its [Defense%20%28CivRev%29](defense). It resembles a German U-Boat from [World%20War%20II](World War II).
The Submarine does not upgrade and cannot reach [Veteran%20%28CivRev%29](Veteran) status by winning battles unless it is built in a [City%20%28CivRev%29](city) with a [Barracks%20%28CivRev%29](Barracks).
