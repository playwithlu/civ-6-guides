# Thetford

Thetford is an [Anglo-Saxon](Anglo-Saxon) and [English](English) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). To date, it appeared only in [Scenario](scenarios). In real life, is a market town and civil parish in the Breckland District of Norfolk in present-day England.
