# Grassland (Freeciv)

Grassland is a type of [terrain](terrain) in [Freeciv](Freeciv). It can contain [resource](resource)s. [Engineer](Engineer)s can transform a [Forest%20%28Freeciv%29](Forest) or [Plains%20%28Freeciv%29](Plains) into Grassland, and Grassland into [Hills%20%28Freeciv%29](Hills) (and any resources on the tile will be carried over when it transforms).
