# Operation Ivy (Civ6)

Operation Ivy is a [Project%20%28Civ6%29](project) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It becomes available after researching [Nuclear%20Fusion%20%28Civ6%29](Nuclear Fusion) and completing the [Manhattan%20Project%20%28Civ6%29](Manhattan Project).
Operation Ivy applies the theoretical knowledge of Nuclear Fusion to the practical task of building even more deadly doomsday weapons. Once completed, it allows the player to undertake the [Build%20Thermonuclear%20Device%20%28Civ6%29](Build Thermonuclear Device) project.
