# WorldBuilder (Civ6)

WorldBuilder is a tool to create or edit [Map%20%28Civ6%29](maps) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Features.

Additionally in advanced mode:
On the right side of the screen you have either single plot editing (Plot button) or a placing tool.
In the placing tool you can select brush size, clear or scatter resources and select what exactly do you want to place on the map.
There is also a player editor where you can select what civs and leaders will be available on the map, what techs/civics they will have already unlocked and what cities they will have.
