# Wu Zetian (Civ6)

Wu Zetian (17 February 624 – 16 December 705), personal name Wu Zhao, was to of and, after his death in 683, the sole ruler of China for over two decades. She first was regent to and, unprecedently, empress regnant (referred to as "emperor") of her own from 690 until her death, the only legitimate female sovereign in the history of China. She leads the [Chinese%20%28Civ6%29](Chinese) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
There are the [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leaders) who will fight you head-on, and then there is Wu Zetian. Her skills are launching offensive [Espionage%20%28Civ6%29%23Espionage%20Missions](spy missions).
Intro.
 From humble beginnings to the center of the world you rose, Wu Zetian, Empress of China. Do not let the machinations of your court halt you, for you outwit them all. Whether by the painter’s brush, your skill with words, or the poisoned blade you will rise above your foes, and cause the world to bow to you – out of love or fear, there is little difference.
In-Game.
Wu Zetian's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Court Intrigue. She likes [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) which pose no threat and dislikes ones with a strong military or nearby [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities).
Her [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Manual of Entrapment. She receives a free [Spy%20%28Civ6%29](Spy) with [Defensive%20Tactics%20%28Civ6%29](Defensive Tactics) and all of her offensive Spies operate at 1 level higher. Detailed Approach.
China under Wu Zetian focuses on building her empire up while tearing down her opponent's civilization from the inside. Her bonuses towards [Espionage%20%28Civ6%29](Espionage) give her an extra Spy and also help her offensive spy operations. When she is successfully spying on her enemies, she receives and to advance in the [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](Technology) and [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](Civic) trees. This and is even more useful when progressing, as [Boost%20%28Civ6%29](Eurekas and Inspirations) go even farther with China's Dynastic Cycle ability.
