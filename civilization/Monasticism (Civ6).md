Monasticism is a [Dark%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Dark Age) [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "". It is available from the [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) to the [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era) and uses a {{WildcardSlotIcon6}} Wildcard slot.
Strategy.
Monasticism increases {{Science6}} output at the expense of {{Culture6}}. It's useful if you've built many [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Sites) and are trying to get caught up in the tech race.
