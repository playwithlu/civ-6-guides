# Seaside Resort (Civ6)

The Seaside Resort is a standard [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](tile improvement) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can only be built on flat [Coastal%20%28Civ6%29](coastal) [Grassland%20%28Civ6%29](Grassland), [Plains%20%28Civ6%29](Plains), and [Desert%20%28Civ6%29](Desert) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) with a minimum [Appeal%20%28Civ6%29](Appeal) rating of Breathtaking (4+). In "", it can also be built on any coastal [Volcanic%20Soil%20%28Civ6%29](Volcanic Soil) tile that has Breathtaking Appeal, regardless of its [Terrain%20%28Civ6%29](terrain) or relief.
Strategy.
For the player pursuing a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Cultural Victory), Seaside Resorts provide an alternative source of which may help immensely! Unfortunately, their placement requirements are quite specific (like the [National%20Park%20%28Civ6%29](National Park)), so you should plan in advance. Found [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) close to or on the [Coast%20%28Civ6%29](Coast) with lowlands nearby, and avoid building stuff which diminishes Appeal in the tiles immediately next to water. The [Eiffel%20Tower%20%28Civ6%29](Eiffel Tower) and [Golden%20Gate%20Bridge%20%28Civ6%29](Golden Gate Bridge) will help you immensely with placement opportunities. Seaside Resorts can also be made more effective with [Cristo%20Redentor%20%28Civ6%29](Cristo Redentor), which doubles their yields.
A Seaside Resort generates as long as it's within a city's workable radius (i.e., 1-3 [hex](hex)es away from the [City%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](City Center)), even if its tile isn't worked.
