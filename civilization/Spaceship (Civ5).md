# Spaceship (Civ5)

The spaceship is a game concept in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" and its expansions.
## Overview

By building and launching a spaceshipÂ meant to colonize the nearby star system of [Alpha%20Centauri%20%28star%20system%29](Alpha Centauri), the player achieves a [Science%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](science victory) in "Civilization V".
Instruction.
Players must complete the [Apollo%20Program%20%28Civ5%29](Apollo Program) [Project%20%28Civ5%29](project) before they can start building their spaceship. They also have to research several end-game [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ5](technologies) to unlock each part.
A spaceship consists of six parts that must all be built in [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) and then transported to the for assembly and launch. Note that the parts will be destroyed if a hostile [Military%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](military unit) enters their tile during the transportation phase, so they should be protected well. The specific parts are as follows:
The [Hubble%20Space%20Telescope%20%28Civ5%29](Hubble Space Telescope), [Spaceship%20Factory%20%28Civ5%29](Spaceship Factory), and [Forge%20%28Civ5%29](Forge) (because spaceship parts are considered land units) greatly help by boosting city for spaceship parts. In addition, players who choose the [Ideology%20%28Civ5%29%23Freedom](Freedom) ideology can adopt [Space%20Procurements%20%28Civ5%29](Space Procurements) to buy parts with , while those who choose the [Ideology%20%28Civ5%29%23Order](Order) ideology can adopt [Spaceflight%20Pioneers%20%28Civ5%29](Spaceflight Pioneers) to build parts with [Great%20Engineer%20%28Civ5%29](Great Engineers).
