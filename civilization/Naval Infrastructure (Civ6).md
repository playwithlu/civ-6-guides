# Naval Infrastructure (Civ6)

Naval Infrastructure is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Another Economic Policy which depends on adjacency bonuses, Naval Infrastructure is not that important unless you're building a trade empire and have many well-situated [Harbor%20%28Civ6%29](Harbors).
Don't forget that the adjacency bonus for the Harbor district is ! So, this policy will potentially increase the size of your treasury. Again, it is only worth using an Economic Slot on it if you are either focusing on and will use it to purchase lots of stuff, or are so desperate for that you need every extra bit. However, keep in mind that another Economic Policy, [Caravansaries%20%28Civ6%29](Caravansaries), has the potential to yield more ! As a rule of thumb, you should go with Naval Infrastructure if you have well-situated Harbors (meaning they have good adjacency bonuses) and Caravansaries if you have many s. 
Another use of this policy is with the [Age%20%28Civ6%29](Golden Age) dedication "Free Inquiry", providing the same boost to your output.
