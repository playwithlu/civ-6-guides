# Ocean

Ocean is a common component of world [map](map)s in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)", "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)", and "[Colonization%20%28game%29](Colonization)" games, in [C-evo](C-evo) a player can choose to have none. [Lake](Lake)s are usually treated the same as oceans, but in "Colonization" they are not navigable.
