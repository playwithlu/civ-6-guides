# Oracle of Delphi (CivRev)

The Oracle of Delphi is a [Wonder%20%28CivRev%29](wonder) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
This wonder can be built when the player discovers [Alphabet%20%28CivRev%29](Alphabet) and gives the player the advantage that they will know if they will win or lose a battle in advance. This wonder's effect is cancelled once any [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CivRev](civilization) discovers [Religion%20%28CivRev%29](Religion).
