# Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri Planetary Pack

Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri Planetary Pack (called Alpha Centauri Planetary Pack for short) is a compilation pack originally released for Windows on February 20, 2000. It includes "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)" and its "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alien%20Crossfire](Alien Crossfire)" expansion.
