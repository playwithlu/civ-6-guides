# War weariness (Civ4)

War weariness is how much your [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) dislikes a war. Increased war weariness causes a civilization to suffer a Happiness penalty. War weariness occurs with all civs when engaged in warfare, except with the [Barbarian%20%28Civ4%29](Barbarian State).
