# Civil Quarantine (Civ6)

Civil Quarantine is a Wildcard [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from [The%20Black%20Death%20%28Civ6%29](The Black Death), a [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "".
Strategy.
Civil Quarantine increases the yield of [Campus%20%28Civ6%29](Campuses) and gives [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) a 10% chance to avoid losing Population from the plague.
