# Plains (Civ5)

Plains is a type of [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29%23Base%20Terrain](base terrain) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Base terrain, found in the central parts of the world.
Strategy.
Plains tiles enable [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) to produce [Building%20%28Civ5%29](buildings) and [Unit%20%28Civ5%29](units) more quickly now, but also as their grows. They can also contain a wide variety of [Resources%20%28Civ5%29%23Strategic%20Resources](strategic) and [Resources%20%28Civ5%29%23Luxury%20Resources](luxury resources). Note that the natural feature of a [Forest%20%28Civ5%29](Forest) has the same original yield, but Plains favor [Farm%20%28Civ5%29](Farms), a growth-enhancing [List%20of%20improvements%20in%20Civ5](improvement), while Forests count as rough terrain for [Combat%20%28Civ5%29](combat) and favor the [Lumber%20Mill%20%28Civ5%29](Lumber Mill) for .
