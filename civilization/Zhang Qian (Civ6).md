# Zhang Qian (Civ6)

Zhang Qian is a [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchant) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Unique ability.
Increases capacity by 1. Foreign s to this [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) provide +2 to both cities.
Strategy.
Before activating Zhang Qian, think about which of your cities is likely to receive the most foreign trade. In most cases, this is going to be either your or your most populous city, but you can confirm this by checking your trade screen. [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](Civilizations) with perks to foreign trade, such as [Egyptian%20%28Civ6%29](Egypt), [Malian%20%28Civ6%29](Mali), and [Polish%20%28Civ6%29](Poland) with its [Sukiennice%20%28Civ6%29](Sukiennice), should try to recruit Zhang Qian for further boosts.
