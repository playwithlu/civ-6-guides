# Spy (CivRev)

The Spy is a [Non-combat%20unit%20%28CivRev%29](non-combat unit) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Strategy.
A Spy can only engage in combat against another Spy. It can be captured without declaring war and has many abilities. It can disrupt the fortifications of a [City%20%28CivRev%29](city), demolish a [Building%20%28CivRev%29](building), steal a [Great%20Person%20%28CivRev%29](Great Person), steal [Gold%20%28currency%29%20%28CivRev%29](gold), or disrupt production. Spy movement is not detected by other [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CivRev](civilizations).
