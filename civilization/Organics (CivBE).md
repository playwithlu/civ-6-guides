# Organics (CivBE)

Game Info.
Organics is a {{LinkBE|Primary Tech}} within Tier 2 of the [Tech_Web_%28CivBE%29](Tech Web). In addition to making your generators produce more energy it allows you to build the [Mass%20Digester%20%28CivBE%29](Mass Digester) and the [Biofactory%20%28CivBE%29](Biofactory).
There is no affinity linked to this technology
