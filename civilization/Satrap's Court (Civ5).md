# Satrap's Court (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20building%20%28Civ5%29](Unique building) of the [Persian%20%28Civ5%29](Persian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Requires [Market%20%28Civ5%29](Market). Replaces the [Bank%20%28Civ5%29](Bank).
Strategy.
The Satrap's Court is slightly more effective at increasing the city's output of wealth than a normal Bank, and it also increases the civilization's happiness. This bonus happiness helps you reach s faster which, as the Persians, is extra important. The city must have a [Market%20%28Civ5%29](Market) before the Satrap's Court may be constructed.
