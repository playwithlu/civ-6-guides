# Mysticism (Civ6)

Mysticism is an [Ancient%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Ancient Era) [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by founding a [Pantheons%20%28Civ6%29](Pantheon).
Strategy.
To see the unseen has always been the definition of religion. After all, all prophets see things that mere mortals do not. But how do you use these unseen things? By interpreting them. To interpret mystic things becomes a staple of early civilizations, because it gives them both a veneer of sophistication, and allows them to impress the masses and pursue their agendas easily. It is also the stepping stone to a true religion.
Mysticism unlocks the first {{WildcardPolicy6}} Wildcard Policies in the game, one of which ([Revelation%20%28Civ6%29](Revelation)) helps immensely with earning a [Great%20Prophet%20%28Civ6%29](Great Prophet) and thus establishing a [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](Religion). It also impresses smaller nations and grants you 1 {{Envoy6}}. If you are playing with the [Vietnam%20%26amp%3B%20Kublai%20Khan%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Vietnam &amp; Kublai Khan Pack), it also unlocks the [Preserve%20%28Civ6%29](Preserve), a special district dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of your civilization.
