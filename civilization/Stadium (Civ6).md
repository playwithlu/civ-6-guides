# Stadium (Civ6)

The Stadium is an advanced [Amenities%20%28Civ6%29%23Entertainment](entertainment) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is built in the and requires a (or one of its replacements).
Strategy.
This late game building can do miracles for your empire's happiness! Its effect is even more powerful than that of the Zoo, but of course comes with greater maintenance and a higher cost.
The Stadium also becomes a source of in big cities, which makes it even more important for [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory) pursuers.
Remember that the area effect of the Stadium works similarly to that of the [Factory%20%28Civ6%29](Factory): it doesn't stack if you have multiple copies of this building within 6 tiles of a [City%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](City Center).
