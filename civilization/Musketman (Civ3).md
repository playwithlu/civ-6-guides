# Musketman (Civ3)

The first gunpowder unit, Musketmen are powerful defenders.
A city must have saltpeter in its Strategic Resource box to build a musketman.
Variants.
The [French%20%28Civ3%29](French), cannot build the Musketman they instead have a superior unique unit - [Musketeer%20%28Civ3%29](Musketeer) which is a more powerful city defender with defensive bombard.
