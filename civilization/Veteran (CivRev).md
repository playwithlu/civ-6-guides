# Veteran (CivRev)

Veteran [List%20of%20units%20in%20CivRev](units) are those that have won three victories in battle or were produced in a [City%20%28CivRev%29](city) with a [Barracks%20%28CivRev%29](Barracks). A veteran unit gains +50% attack and [Defense%20%28CivRev%29](defense) and becomes an [Elite%20%28CivRev%29](elite) unit after winning three more battles.
All units except [Settlers%20%28CivRev%29](Settlers) and [Militia%20%28CivRev%29](Militia) can achieve veteran status.
