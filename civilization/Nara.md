# Nara

Nara is a [Japanese](Japanese) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and the [Yamato](Yamato) [capital](capital) in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it is the capital of Nara Prefecture and served as the capital of Japan until the Emperor moved the capital to [Kyoto](Kyoto).
