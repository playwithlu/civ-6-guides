# Veii

Veii is the [Etruscan](Etruscan) [capital](capital) and a [Roman](Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it is a ruined city near [Rome%20%28city%29](Rome) that was once the richest city in the Etruscan League.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
