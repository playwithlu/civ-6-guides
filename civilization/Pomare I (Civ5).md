# Pomare I (Civ5)

Pomare I is a playable leader in [Paradise%20Found%20%28Civ5%29](Paradise Found). He is a reused [Hiawatha%20%28Civ5%29](Hiawatha). He is the leader of [Tahitian%20%28Civ5%29](Tahiti) and lived from 1742 to 1803 AD. Therefore, Pomare I is the most recent and the most historically accurate of the leaders in the scenario.
Unique Ability.
Tupaia's Navigation - All land and naval military units have +1 sight.
