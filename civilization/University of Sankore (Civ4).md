# University of Sankore (Civ4)

Strategy.
The University of Sankore increases the [Science%20%28Civ4%29](science) output of all [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ4](buildings) associated with the state [Religion%20%28Civ4%29](religion) . It also increases its [City%20%28Civ4%29](city's) chances of generating a [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ4%29](Great Scientist).
