# Zoo (Civ6)

The Zoo is an advanced [Amenities%20%28Civ6%29%23Entertainment](entertainment) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is built in the [Entertainment%20Complex%20%28Civ6%29](Entertainment Complex) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) and requires an [Arena%20%28Civ6%29](Arena) (or one of its replacements).
Strategy.
It is common for medium-sized and large empires to feel a happiness crunch in the middle game. There are just so many [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities), and so many people in those cities! This is when well-placed Entertainment Complexes with Zoos in them really come in handy. A single one of these may provide to 3-4 cities if situated correctly. Use the same logic when placing them as when you plan the placement of your [Industrial%20Zone%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Zones).
In "", the Zoo also adds yield to [Rainforest%20%28Civ6%29](Rainforests) and [Marsh%20%28Civ6%29](Marshes) within its city's [Territory%20%28Civ6%29](territory). Build Entertainment Complexes in jungle cities to get a solid scientific boost come the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era). This is especially useful for [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) such as [Brazilian%20%28Civ6%29](Brazil) and [Kongolese%20%28Civ6%29](Kongo), whose scientific side may suffer due to their [Starting%20bias%20%28Civ6%29](starting bias).
