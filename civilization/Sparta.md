# Sparta

Sparta is the [Greek](Greek) [capital](capital) (under [Gorgo%20%28Civ6%29](Gorgo)) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" and a Greek [city](city) in other and the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it was a [city-state](city-state) in and a city and municipality in Laconia, Peloponnese in present-day Greece.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Macedonian](Macedonian) city in [Rise%20of%20Rome](Rise of Rome).
