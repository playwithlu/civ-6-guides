# Uluru (Civ5)

Uluru is a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonder) in "". It spawns in [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](Plains) and [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](Mountain) tiles.
Strategy.
Uluru, when worked, provides a large amount of (equal to that provided by [Mt.%20Kailash%20%28Civ5%29](Mt. Kailash)) and a small amount of . A [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) that settles near it will have an easier time founding a [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religion).
Uluru counts as a Mountain when building an [Observatory%20%28Civ5%29](Observatory), but not when building [Neuschwanstein%20%28Civ5%29](Neuschwanstein) or [Machu%20Pichu%20%28Civ5%29](Machu Pichu). No [Unit%20%28Civ5%29](units) except for [Helicopter%20Gunship%20%28Civ5%29](Helicopter Gunships) can pass through it, and they sustain damage if they end their turn on it.
