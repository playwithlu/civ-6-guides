# Tikal

Tikal, also known as Yax Mutal, is the [Mayan](Mayan) [capital](capital) in "" and a Mayan [city](city) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. In real life, it is a ruined city in present-day Guatemala that was once the capital of a powerful Mayan kingdom.
