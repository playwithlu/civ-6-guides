# Slave (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Slaves are who do not enjoy the benefit of citizenship. They are prisoners, in a sense, forced to work in a foreign for no pay. Slaves only eat half of the of standard .
An empire can only acquire slaves by sending on slave raids in neighboring empires. The act of enslaving others is seen as a grave offense and may enrage other nations.
