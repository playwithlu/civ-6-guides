# Mt. Kailash (Civ5)

Mt. Kailash is a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonder) in "". It spawns in [Grassland%20%28Civ5%29](grassland), [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](plains), and [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](mountain) tiles.
Strategy.
Reflecting its real-life status as a sacred mountain, Mt. Kailash provides a small bonus to and a large bonus to . Empires that focus on [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religion) should try to found a [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) near Mt. Kailash and incorporate it into their [Territory%20%28Civ5%29](territory) as early as possible, and the [Persian%20%28Civ5%29](Persians) and [Brazilian%20%28Civ5%29](Brazilians) will particularly enjoy the bonus it provides.
