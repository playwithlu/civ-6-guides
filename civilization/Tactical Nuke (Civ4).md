# Tactical Nuke (Civ4)

The Tactical Nuke is the smaller and less powerful weapon of mass destruction weapon available to any [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization). Compared to the [ICBM%20%28Civ4%29](ICBM), which has no limit to where it can strike, the Tactical Nuke has a range of about 50 squares.
Strategy.
The amount of damage done to any [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) or object on the [Map%20%28Civ4%29](map) by a Tactical Nuke varies. If used over a city, it will mostly damage important [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ4](buildings) and cause less damage to [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ4](units). Important structures, such as [List%20of%20wonders%20in%20Civ4](wonders) or [Bank%20%28Civ4%29](Banks), may be destroyed.
Unlike the ICBM, the Tactical Nuke has a 50% chance to evade interception attempts.
