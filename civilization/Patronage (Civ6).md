# Patronage (Civ6)

Patronage is an Economic [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from [The%20Black%20Death%20%28Civ6%29](The Black Death), a [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "".
Strategy.
Similar to the [Aesthetics%20%28Civ6%29](Aesthetics) policy card in the base game, the most glaring problem with Patronage is that it is very difficult to gain a high starting [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonus) for the [Theater%20Square%20%28Civ6%29](Theater Square). However, if you have a Theater Square with a good starting bonus, this card can help you tremendously race towards the end.
