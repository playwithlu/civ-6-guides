# Florence

Florence, also known as Firenze or Florentia, is a [Holy%20Roman](Holy Roman), [Italian](Italian), and [Roman](Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in present-day Italy.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Burgundian](Burgundian) city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
