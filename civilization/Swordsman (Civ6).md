# Swordsman (Civ6)

The Swordsman is a [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Melee](melee unit) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It upgrades from the [Warrior%20%28Civ6%29](Warrior) (or its replacements, except the [Gaesatae%20%28Civ6%29](Gaesatae)) or the [Sabum%20Kibittum%20%28Civ6%29](Sabum Kibittum) and requires .
In the "" expansion, the Swordsman requires 20 to train.
Strategy.
The Swordsman is covered in iron plating, armed with sword and shield and ready to defend your empire against the more advanced enemies from the [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical) and [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era). Its strength is superior or equal to anything before [Horseman%20%28Civ6%29](Horsemen) (which have only 1 Combat Strength more), turning it into the bulwark of your army for quite some time. Know, however, that Medieval Era [Knight%20%28Civ6%29](Knights) will destroy the Swordsman!
