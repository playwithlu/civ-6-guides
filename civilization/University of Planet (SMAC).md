# University of Planet (SMAC)

The University of Planet is a [Factions%20%28SMAC%29](faction) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)". It is a led by Prokhor Zakharov, often referred to by the titles of Academician or Provost.
Faction characteristics.
Although Zakharov himself is not restricted from using the Power or Wealth values choices, an [AI](AI)-controlled Zakharov will take offense to a player's choices of such.
Leader's defining quote.
"The substructure of the universe regresses infinitely towards smaller and smaller components. Behind atoms we find electrons, and behind electrons quarks. Each layer unraveled reveals new secrets, but also new mysteries."
-- Prokhor Zakharov, "For I Have Tasted The Fruit"
