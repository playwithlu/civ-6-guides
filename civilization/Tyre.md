# Tyre

Tyre, also known as Tyrus, is the [Phoenician](Phoenician) [capital](capital) in some of the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and a [Hebrew](Hebrew), [Persian](Persian), [Roman](Roman), and Phoenician [city](city) in others and the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games), and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in present-day Lebanon.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as an [Abbasid](Abbasid), [Sasanian](Sasanian), and [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city.
