Effect.
The SDI, the Strategic Defense Initiative, protects cities from nuclear attack within the city's radius. Using a combination of missiles and lasers, the SDI creates an umbrella of safety over the worried citizens of the nuclear age.
Great Library entry.
During the 1980s, the American president Ronald Reagan instituted the Strategic Defense Initiative, a multi-billion dollar project whose mission was to protect the U.S. against intercontinental ballistic missiles. Fifty years later, developments in advanced computer tracking and high-energy lasers created a working interception system. Operational for the first time in the middle of the 21st century, SDI arrived just in time to protect the world from nuclear war.
