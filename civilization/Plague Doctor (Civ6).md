# Plague Doctor (Civ6)

The Plague Doctor is [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Civilian](civilian unit) from [The%20Black%20Death%20%28Civ6%29](The Black Death), a [Scenario%20%28Civ6%29](scenario) in "". It can shorten the plague's duration on plague-stricken [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) and restore [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](Loyalty) to [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities).
Strategy.
In The Black Death, the Plague Doctor is an extremely important [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](unit) for your [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization). Minister to the Sick is best used on your [City%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](City Center) and [District%20%28Civ6%29](districts) to allow you to build up your cities during an outbreak. Pacify City is less needed than Minister to the Sick, but can be useful if you have a city with decreasing Loyalty. When playing as the [Imperial%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Roman Empire), [Castilian%20%28Civ6%29](Castile), or [French%20%28Civ6%29/The%20Black%20Death](France) make usage of Plague Doctors one of your top priorities. When playing as [English%20%28Civ6%29/The%20Black%20Death](England) Plague Doctors, while still useful, are less impervious thanks to their unique Coerce command.
