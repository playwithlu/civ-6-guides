# Sasanian

The Sasanian people, also known as the Sassanids, represent the Sasanian Empire, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. Their [capital](capital) is [Ctesiphon](Ctesiphon), and they can be led by [Bahram%20V](Bahram V) or [Shapur%20II](Shapur II). To date, they have appeared only in [scenario](scenario)s.
