# Plains (CivRev)

Plains are a type of [Terrain%20%28CivRev%29](terrain) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". They resemble [Grassland%20%28CivRev%29](grasslands), but are more yellow in color.
A plain provides 1 [Food%20%28CivRev%29](food) (+1 with [Russian%20%28CivRev%29](Russia's) Ancient Era bonus or [America%20%28CivRev%29](America's) Industrial Era bonus) to nearby [City%20%28CivRev%29](cities). A [Granary%20%28CivRev%29](Granary) can increase the food power of plains tiles by 2.
