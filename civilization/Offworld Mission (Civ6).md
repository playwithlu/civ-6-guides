# Offworld Mission (Civ6)

Offworld Mission is a [Future%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Future Era) [Technologies%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "".
Strategy.
The two [Project%20%28Civ6%29](projects) Offworld Mission unlocks, [Terrestrial%20Laser%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](Terrestrial Laser Station) and [Lagrange%20Laser%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](Lagrange Laser Station), are projects meant to speed up a civ's progression to the [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory). It may prove useful to if possible have the [Exodus%20Imperative%20%28Civ6%29](Exodus Imperative) Civic already enabled upon or just before the discovery of this tech, along with one of that civics' associated policy wildcards, [Aerospace%20Contractors%20%28Civ6%29](Aerospace Contractors).
The [International%20Space%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](International Space Station) can possibly give an additional boost to the projects enabled by this tech, as well as to your Space Race projects in general.
