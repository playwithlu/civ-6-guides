# Farmer (Civ6)

The Farmer is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Civilian](civilian unit) in the [Outback%20Tycoon%20%28Civ6%29](Outback Tycoon) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The Farmer can build [Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Farms), [Plantation%20%28Civ6%29](Plantations), and [Lumber%20Mill%20%28Civ6%29](Lumber Mills) to provide a [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) with [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resources) and strengthen its economy.
