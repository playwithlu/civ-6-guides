# Aiwass (Civ6)

Aiwass is the Level 3 title for the [Hermetic%20Order%20%28Civ6%29](Hermetic Order), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era).
Effects.
Unlocks [Occult%20Research%20%28Civ6%29](Occult Research), a city [Project%20%28Civ6%29](project) that provides {{Gold6}} while active. When completed, it grants {{GreatPeople6}} points, and {{Science6}} for every {{LeyLine6}} in the city.
