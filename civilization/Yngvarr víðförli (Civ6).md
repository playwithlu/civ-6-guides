# Yngvarr víðförli (Civ6)

Yngvarr víðförli is a {{Link6|Great Admiral}} from the [Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21%20%28Civ6%29](Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Unique Ability.
When adjacent to [Vinland%20%28Civ6%29](Vinland) earn +500 {{Gold6}} and Victory Points based on order of activation.
+5 {{Strength6}} Combat Strength and +1 {{Movement6}} to naval units within 2 {{Link6|tile}}s.
