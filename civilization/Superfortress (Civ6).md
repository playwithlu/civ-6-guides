# Superfortress (Civ6)

Superfortress is a Tier IV for [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Air%20Bomber](air bomber units) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It requires [Tactical%20Maintenance%20%28Civ6%29](Tactical Maintenance) or [Long%20Range%20%28Civ6%29](Long Range).
Effects.
No minimum health requirement to air pillage.
