# Seaport (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced sea building. Requires [Harbor%20%28Civ5%29](Harbor).
Strategy.
A Seaport is the pinnacle of maritime technology. Besides further boosting and output for sea [Resources%20%28Civ5%29](resources), the Seaport gives a bonus toward naval vessels, which can be priceless. Make sure to build training buildings in [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) with Seaports, and build up your navy there!
