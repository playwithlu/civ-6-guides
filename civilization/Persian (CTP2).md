# Persian (CTP2)

The Persian people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Their default leader names are [Cyrus](Cyrus the Great) for a male and [Scheherazade](Scheherazade) for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Hyrcania](Hyrcania) (or [Persepolis](Persepolis) in [Apolyton%20Edition%20%28CTP2%29](Apolyton Edition)).
Scenarios.
In the [Classical%20Age%20%28CTP2%29](Classical Age) [Mods%20%28CTP2%29](mod), the Persian leader is [Darius%20%28CTP2%29](Darius).
