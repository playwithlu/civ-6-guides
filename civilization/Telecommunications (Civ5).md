# Telecommunications (Civ5)

Game Info.
The development of modern Telecommunications (via satellites, for example) brings your civilization various advancements. It allows you to build the [Nuclear%20Submarine%20%28Civ5%29](Nuclear Submarine), an advanced submarine capable of carrying two missiles; and also allows the construction of the [Bomb%20Shelter%20%28Civ5%29](Bomb Shelter), which significantly reduces population loss from a nuclear attack.
