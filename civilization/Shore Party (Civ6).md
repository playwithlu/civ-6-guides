# Shore Party (Civ6)

The Shore Party is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Melee](melee unit) from the [Pirates%20%28Civ6%29](Pirates) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)", added in the [Civilization%20VI%20October%202020%20Update](October 2020 Update).
Strategy.
As the only land unit available in the game mode, its main use is to plunder Buccaneer camps and bury treasure for Treasure points. It is also the only unit capable of directly attacking [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) on land, which is useful as the colonial civilizations are often more focused on their navy. It has the best chances on a city that has already been damaged by naval attacks.
