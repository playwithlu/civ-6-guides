# Nanohospital (SMAC)

 is a [base%20facility%20%28SMAC%29](base facility) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)".
The Nanohospital is available for construction in a base after one has built a [Research%20Hospital%20%28SMAC%29](Research Hospital) in that base and one has also discovered the [Homo%20Superior%20%28SMAC%29](Homo Superior) technology. Its effects are cumulative with the Research Hospital.
