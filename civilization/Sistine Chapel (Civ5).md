# Sistine Chapel (Civ5)

Strategy.
The Sistine Chapel is one of the must-have Wonders for pursuers of [Cultural%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](cultural victory). Not only does it enhance your empire's overall output, but it also has the first two slots for Great Works of Art, which appear much earlier than the standard [Building%20%28Civ5%29](building) containing such slots: the [Museum%20%28Civ5%29](Museum).
