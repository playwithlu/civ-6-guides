# National Epic (Civ5)

Game Info.
Cultural national wonder. Requires a [Monument%20%28Civ5%29](Monument) in all [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities).
Strategy.
The National Epic increases a city's Great People generation by 25 percent, which is especially useful for a player pursuing a [Cultural%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](cultural victory). Build your [Artists%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Artists'), [Musicians%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Musicians'), and [Writers%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Writers' Guilds) in this city as well to ensure the maximum boost to Great People generation!
