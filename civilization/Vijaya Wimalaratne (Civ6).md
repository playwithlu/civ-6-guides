# Vijaya Wimalaratne (Civ6)

Vijaya Wimalaratne is an [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Information era land units within 2 s.
Vijaya Wimalaratne can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to grant a level and +100% combat experience to a military land unit.
