# Scipio (CTP2)

Scipio is a special land [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP2](unit) from the [Classical%20era%20%28CTP2%29](Classical age) [Mods%20%28CTP2%29](modpack) of "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". His full name is Cn Cornelus Scipio (with the "Cn" representing the rank of "Consul").
History.
Scipio is a general that lived from 236-183 BC. He is credited with defeating the [Carthaginian%20%28CTP2%29](Carthaginians) in the Battle of Zama in 202 BC. Scipio failed at killing [Hannibal%20Barca](Hannibal Barca) but he still gets the credit for having defeated the Carthaginians. Most other facts about his personal life are not referenced in this game but he is considered a very effective general with strong attack abilities.
