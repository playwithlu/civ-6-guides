# Research Institute (Civ4)

The Research Institute is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [Russian%20%28Civ4%29](Russian civilization).
Strategy.
Research Institutes, the Russian replacements for [Laboratory%20%28Civ4%29](Laboratories), provide two free [Scientist%20%28Civ4%29](scientists) to their city in addition to increasing scientific research and spaceship production.
