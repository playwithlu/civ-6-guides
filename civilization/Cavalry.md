# Cavalry

The Cavalry is a [Mobile%20unit](mounted unit) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)", "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)", and "[Colonization%20%28game%29](Colonization)" games. The term "cavalry" can also refer broadly to any mounted unit (i.e., mobile unit available from the early game to the midgame).
