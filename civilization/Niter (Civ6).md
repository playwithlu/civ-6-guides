# Niter (Civ6)

 Niter is a [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era) [Resources%20%28Civ6%29%23Strategic%20Resources](Strategic Resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is revealed with [Military%20Engineering%20%28Civ6%29](Military Engineering).
 Niter is found on a variety of terrain, always on flatland.
Strategy.
 Niter is a mid-game resource that is often found in rather cumbersome locations (such as [Tundra%20%28Civ6%29](Tundra) regions), but it is essential for furthering military progress in the game. It is a requirement for producing the major siege and melee units of its era, and since its influence can reach even into the [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era), it can be considered highly valuable for warfare.
 Niter, being such a useful ingredient in gunpowder production and fertilizers, additionally offers the same bonuses to and that do, so it is generally recommended to take advantage of its appearance.
This resource has become even more valuable in "", with a number of previously resource-independent [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique units) such as the [Redcoat%20%28Civ6%29](Redcoat) now requiring Niter.
