# Synthetic Technocracy (Civ6)

Synthetic Technocracy is a form of Tier 4 [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) in "". It is unlocked by the [Optimization%20Imperative%20%28Civ6%29](Optimization Imperative) [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](civic).
Strategy.
Synthetic Technocracy is an ideal form of government for players seeking a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory), as it will allow them to finish vital [Project%20%28Civ6%29](projects) such as [Launch%20Mars%20Colony%20%28Civ6%29](Launch Mars Colony) and [Exoplanet%20Expedition%20%28Civ6%29](Exoplanet Expedition) more quickly and provide their [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) with needed to maintain advanced [Buildings%20%28Civ6%29](buildings) and [Terrestrial%20Laser%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](Terrestrial Laser Stations). It can also assist with a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Diplomacy](Diplomatic Victory) by speeding up [Carbon%20Recapture%20%28Civ6%29](Carbon Recapture) projects, which will allow players to earn and win [Climate%20Accords%20%28Civ6%29](Climate Accords) competitions for Diplomatic Victory points.
