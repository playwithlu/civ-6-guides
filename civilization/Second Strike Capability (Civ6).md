# Second Strike Capability (Civ6)

Second Strike Capability is a Military [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "".
Strategy.
If you like to maintain a massive stockpile of [Nuclear%20weapons%20%28Civ6%29](nuclear weapons), you'll love this policy. It cuts their maintenance cost in half, allowing you to wait for the right moment to unleash nuclear hell on your enemies without having to worry as much about your empire's economy.
