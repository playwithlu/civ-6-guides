# Spaceship (Civ3)

The spaceship is a game concept in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)" and its expansions.
Space Race.
One of the peaceful methods of [Victory%20%28Civ3%29](victory) in "Civilization III" is to be the first to colonize [Alpha%20Centauri%20%28star%20system%29](Alpha Centauri), the nearest star system. In order to achieve this victory, the player must be the first to launch a completed spaceship to Alpha Centauri.
Building the Ship.
Each [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) can build an "Alpha Centauri-worthy" spacecraft, and construction can begin when the civilization completes the [Apollo%20Program%20%28Civ3%29](Apollo Program) (Small Wonder). To win the space race victory, you have to be the first to build, then launch, the spaceship.
