# Victory (CTP2)

There are four ways to achieve victory in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Conquest.
Capture or convert all opponents' [City%20%28CTP2%29](cities).
Score.
Survive until the year [2300%20AD](2300 AD) and have the highest score.
Diplomatic.
Have an [Alliance%20%28CTP2%29](alliance) with every [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in the game.
Science.
Build at least 10 [Gaia%20Controller%20Core%20%28CTP2%29](Gaia Controller Cores), 10 [Gaia%20Satellite%20%28CTP2%29](Gaia Satellites), and enough [Obelisk%20%28CTP2%29](Obelisks) to cover 60% of the [Map%20%28CTP2%29](map), and initialize the [Gaia%20Controller%20%28wonder%29%20%28CTP2%29](Gaia Controller).
