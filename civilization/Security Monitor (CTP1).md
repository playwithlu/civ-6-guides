# Security Monitor (CTP1)

Security Monitor is a [List%20of%20buildings%20%28CTP1%29](city improvement) in "Civilization: Call to Power".
Effect.
The Security Monitor reduces crime by 50% and pollution by 100%, and increases production by 25%. Through the observation and recording of all public life.
Great Library entry.
By the end of the 20th century the third world faced a crisis in personal safety. Criminals used cheap, powerful assault weapons, explosives, and tear gas to overwhelm private security and municipal police. No one was safe until governments turned to the Security Monitor. Capable of recognizing any citizen at several hundred yards, the Monitor ended the concept of privacy in the name of public safety.
