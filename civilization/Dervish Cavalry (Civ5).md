# Dervish Cavalry (Civ5)

The Derwish Cavalry is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](unique) mounted unit of the [Egyptian%20%28Civ5%29](Egyptian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) from the [Scramble%20for%20Africa%20%28Civ5%29](Scramble for Africa) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "".
Game Info.
Cavalry that spawns Great Generals faster and fights better in open terrain. Only the Egyptians can build it.
Strategy.
Egyptian Cavalry replacement. Its promotions allow it to spawn Great Generals faster and fight better in open terrain.
