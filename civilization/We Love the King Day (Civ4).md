# We Love the King Day (Civ4)

We Love the King Day is special event in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)". It can occur in any [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) with a [Population%20%28Civ4%29](population) of 8 or more if it has not lost any [Food%20%28Civ4%29](food) to [Health%20%28Civ4%29](unhealthiness) and has no angry [Citizen%20%28Civ4%29](citizens). During a We Love the King Day celebration, the city pays no [Maintenance%20%28Civ4%29](maintenance) costs for that turn.
