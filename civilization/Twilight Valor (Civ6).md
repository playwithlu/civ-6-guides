# Twilight Valor (Civ6)

Twilight Valor is a [Dark%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Dark Age) [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "". It is available from the [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Era) to the [Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Renaissance Era) and uses a Wildcard slot. Twilight Valor is removed from game when playing with the game mode.
Strategy.
If you find yourself fighting for survival at the beginning of a , adopt Twilight Valor to give your forces an edge. Just remember to switch it out before launching a counteroffensive of your own.
The Combat Strength bonus applies to [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Recon](recon units) (including upgraded ones with ranged attacks), [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Naval%20melee](naval melee units), [Nihang%20%28Civ6%29](Nihang), and [Vampire%20%28Civ6%29](Vampires), but not to [Warrior%20Monk%20%28Civ6%29](Warrior Monks).
