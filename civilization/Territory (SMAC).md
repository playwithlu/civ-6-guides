# Territory (SMAC)

Your territory consists of all land nearer to one of your land s on the same continent than to anyone else's. Your territorial waters consist of all sea areas nearer to one of your sea bases (in the same ocean) than to anyone else's. Territory may move and shift during the course of the game as bases are built, conquered, and destroyed.
