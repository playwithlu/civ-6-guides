# The United Nations (CivWorld)

 is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It allows all members of the [Civilizations%20%28CivWorld%29](Civilization) to contribute more than one [Great%20Person%20%28CivWorld%29](Great Person) to [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29](Wonder) construction and is available after discovering [Globalization%20%28CivWorld%29](Globalization).
