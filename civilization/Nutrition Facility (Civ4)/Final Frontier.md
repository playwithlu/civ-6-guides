Civilopedia entry.
The development of advanced hydroponics remains one of the most crucial discoveries in the expansion of interstellar settlement. The oxygen produced by photosynthetic life nurtured through hydroponic systems has been used to sustain settlers and even terraform alien atmospheres. Such facilities have become the backbone of sustaining human life on new worlds.
