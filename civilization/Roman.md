# Roman

The Roman people represent the Roman Empire, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)". Their [capital](capital) is [Rome%20%28city%29](Rome), and they can be led by [Augustus%20Caesar](Augustus Caesar), [Julius%20Caesar](Julius Caesar), [Livia%20Drusilla](Livia Drusilla), or [Trajan](Trajan).
The [Byzantine](Byzantine) and [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) people may also be represented by the Romans.
