Great Library entry.
In 1974, Project FAMOUS, the French-American Mid-Ocean Undersea Study launched forty-four dives to study the structure of the rift valley in the mid-Atlantic ridge. The project confirmed volcanoes, earthquakes, geysers, and wildlife were prevalent in the underwater mountain ranges where divergent tectonic plates meet. In the century to come, the Rifts would be settled and developed along with the rest of the ocean floor.
