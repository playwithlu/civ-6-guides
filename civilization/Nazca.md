# Nazca

Nazca or (Nasca) is an [Incan](Incan) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in present-day Peru.
In "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)", Nazca is the default name for a female [Aztec](Aztec) [Leaders](leader).
