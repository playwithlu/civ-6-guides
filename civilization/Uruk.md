# Uruk

Uruk, also known as Erech, is the [Sumerian](Sumerian) [capital](capital) in some of the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and an [Assyrian](Assyrian), [Babylonian](Babylonian), and Sumerian [city](city) in others and the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). In real life, it was an ancient city located east of the bed of the river in present-day Iraq.
