# Spy Plane (CTP1)

The Spy Plane is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
The Spy Plane wafts high over the [Map%20%28CTP1%29](world) peeking into enemy armies and enemy [City%20%28CTP1%29](cities). With the widest vision range of any unit and the ability to move quickly over long distances, the Spy Plane can investigate the largest area in the least amount of time. It needs speed and endurance to evade its enemies, however, for it has virtually no protection.
Great Library entry.
Developed using the latest Robotic technology of the 22nd century, the Spy Plane was the 22nd century eye-in-the-sky. Designed to monitor battles even when space was inaccessible or even hostile, the Spy Plane used a single wing saucer-like shape to evade radar and to increase range.
