# Staatsmuseum (Civ5)

Game Info.
Building in the [Scramble%20for%20Africa%20%28Civ5%29](Scramble for Africa) [List%20of%20Civilization%20V%20scenarios](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". Can only be constructed by Boer civilization. Contains 2 slots for Great Works of Art or Artifacts.
Strategy.
The Staatsmuseum is a Boer-unique building which increases the city's output of (especially when housing Great Works). It comes pre-built with 1 Great Work. A European civs capturing a city with a Staatsmuseum will gain VP for capturing artifact(s). It replaces the Museum.
