# Support

Support is a [Society%20Effects%20%28SMAC%29](Society Effect) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)". In the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, it can be either a [combat](combat) bonus or another term for [maintenance](maintenance) (especially when used to pay for [unit](unit)s).
