# Musketeer (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](Unique) front-line unit of the [French%20%28Civ5%29](French) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Musketman%20%28Civ5%29](Musketman).
Strategy.
The Musketeers, elite fighters that were immortalized by Alexandre Dumas in his most famous novel, are the strongest infantry units of the [Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Renaissance Era). They are otherwise nondescript.
