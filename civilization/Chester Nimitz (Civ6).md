# Chester Nimitz (Civ6)

Chester Nimitz is an [Great%20Admiral%20%28Civ6%29](Great Admiral) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Atomic and Information era naval units within 2 s.
