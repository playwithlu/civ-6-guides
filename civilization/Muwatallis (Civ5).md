# Muwatallis (Civ5)

Muwatallis is a playable [Leaders%20%28Civ5%29](leader) from [Wonders%20of%20the%20Ancient%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Wonders of the Ancient World) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". He leads the [Hittite%20%28Civ5%29](Hittites). He is a reused [Suleiman%20%28Civ5%29](Suleiman).
Unique Ability.
First to Iron - [List%20of%20resources%20in%20Civ5%23Strategic%20resources](Strategic resources) provide +1 {{Production5}}. [Horses%20%28Civ5%29](Horses) and [Iron%20%28Civ5%29](Iron) resources provide double quantity.
