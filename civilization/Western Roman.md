# Western Roman

The Western Roman people represent the Western Roman Empire, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. Their [capital](capital) is [Rome%20%28city%29](Rome), and they can be led by [Constantine%20the%20Great](Constantine the Great) or [Honorius](Honorius). To date, they have appeared only in [scenario](scenario)s.
The [Roman](Roman) people may also be represented by the Western Romans.
