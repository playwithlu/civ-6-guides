# Lithuanian (Civ6)

The Lithuanian people represent the Lithuanian noble families, a playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) from the [Jadwiga%27s%20Legacy%20%28Civ6%29](Jadwiga's Legacy) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are led by [Miko%C5%82aj%20Radziwi%C5%82%C5%82%20%28Civ6%29](Mikołaj Radziwiłł), and their [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](civilization ability) is "Golden Liberty," which allows them to claim all surrounding [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) when they build an [Encampment%20%28Civ6%29](Encampment) or a [Fort%20%28Civ6%29](Fort) in their territory and changes one of their [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government's) Military [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) slots into a Wildcard slot.
