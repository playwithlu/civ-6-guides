# Palenque

Palenque, also known as Lakamha º, is the [Mayan](Mayan) [capital](capital) in "" and a Mayan [city](city) in other "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it was a Mayan city-state that flourished in the 7th century.
