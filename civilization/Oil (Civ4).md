# Oil (Civ4)

Strategy.
Oil appears in the game as a black puddle shooting out black circles. It can appear in any [Terrain%20%28Civ4%29](terrain), including water.
Oil provides production and commerce to the city that works it. It is required for the production of many of the late-game military units.
Civilizations with cities where the [Standard%20Ethanol%20%28Civ4%29](Standard Ethanol) [Corporation%20%28concept%29%20%28Civ4%29](corporation) is present automatically receive one of this resource.
