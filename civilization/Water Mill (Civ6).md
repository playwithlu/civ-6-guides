# Water Mill (Civ6)

The Water Mill is an early infrastructure [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is built in the [City%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](City Center).
Strategy.
As usual, the Water Mill is very useful for a general boost to a budding [City%20%28Civ6%29](city's) growth and functionality, and even more so if it has , , and nearby. Unfortunately, it can only be built in a city whose City Center is adjacent to a [River%20%28Civ6%29](River). Fortunately, with the present city growth rules, you have really good reasons to found cities on Rivers!
