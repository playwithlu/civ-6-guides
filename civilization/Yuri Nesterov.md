# Yuri Nesterov

Yuri Nesterov is a voice actor who speaks English. He provided the voice of Academician Prokhor Zakharov, the leader of the [University%20of%20Planet%20%28SMAC%29](University of Planet) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)", for which he was credited as Yuri Nesteroff.
