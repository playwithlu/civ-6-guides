War Machine is a 1-on-1 [multiplayer](multiplayer) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "". The scenario is set in [The%20Great%20War](the First World War), where one player plays with Germany and the other plays with France. There is no [single%20player](single player) mode in this scenario.
Intro.
In this scenario you take command of the armies of France or Germany during the first month of WWI. You have a massive army poised to strike, an industrial nation ready to back it up, and only 40 turns to capture or defend Paris. Can you triumph in the greatest clash of arms the world has ever seen? Or will warfare on this scale and speed overwhelm you?
Gameplay.
The German Empire is locked into war with France, Belgium, and Britain, which are locked into an alliance with each other. [Settler%20%28Civ6%29](Settlers) and [Builder%20%28Civ6%29](Builders) cannot be trained, and , [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civics), [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](religion), [Government%20%28Civ6%29](governments), [Espionage%20%28Civ6%29](espionage), [Diplomacy%20%28Civ6%29](diplomacy), and the [World%20Congress%20%28Civ6%29](World Congress) are all disabled - this is a purely military scenario.
The German Empire wins if it captures [Paris](Paris), the French , before 40 [turn](turn)s elapse; otherwise, France wins.
