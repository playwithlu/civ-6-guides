# Foreign Volunteer (Civ5)

The Foreign Volunteer is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](unique) gunpowder unit of the [Boer%20%28Civ5%29](Boer) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) from the [Scramble%20for%20Africa%20%28Civ5%29](Scramble for Africa) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "". It replaces the [Rifleman%20%28Civ5%29](Rifleman).
Game Info.
Rifleman that specializes in defense and heals whenever it kills its opponent. Only the Boer Republics can build it.
Strategy.
Boer Republics Rifleman replacement. It is weaker than a normal rifleman attacking, but stronger defending. It also partially heals after killing its opponents.
