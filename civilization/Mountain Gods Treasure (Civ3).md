# Mountain Gods Treasure (Civ3)

Description.
The Mountain Gods Treasure is used in this scenario to score victory points by presenting the unit to the Three Sisters volcanoes.
When the game starts, each civilization will have one of these units in their capital. After activating a military unit, use the hot keys Shift + S to pick up the unit, and then carry it to the volcano. The unit carrying the Treasure will be shown in red text any time you right click on that unit.
