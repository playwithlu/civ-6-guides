# Copter (SMAC)

 is a unit chassis type in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)".
Game info.
Copters, with their maneuverability and ability to land temporarily in rough terrain, make formidable combat units. Copters should return to a friendly base or airbase at the end of every turn for refueling. If it is unable to do so and must land temporarily in the field, it incurs 30% damage.
The 'Copter chassis is available upon the discovery of [Mind/Machine%20Interface%20%28SMAC%29](Mind-Machine Interface) technology. 
Units with this chassis are [Air%20unit%20%28SMAC%29](atmospheric corps units) that can attack multiple times per turn, but must return to a base or airbase within the same turn or it will crash-land and suffer damage equal to 30% of its maximum structural integrity in the process. A 'copter which has landed in such a fashion can be attacked by both air and ground units.
A 'copter with the [Air%20Superiority%20%28SMAC%29](Air Superiority) ability may be known as a "lifter".
