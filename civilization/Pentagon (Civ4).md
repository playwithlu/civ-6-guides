# Pentagon (Civ4)

Strategy.
Once the Pentagon is built, all new [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ4](units) built in your [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization) receive two experience points. It also increases the chance that its [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) will generate a [Great%20Spy%20%28Civ4%29](Great Spy).
