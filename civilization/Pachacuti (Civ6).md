# Pachacuti (Civ6)

Pachacuti (1410 AD – 1471 or 1472 AD), born Cusi Yupanqui, was the ninth of . Through a combination of military campaigns and skilled diplomacy, he conducted a swift and far-reaching expansion of his kingdom that led to the establishment of the . He leads the [Incan%20%28Civ6%29](Incas) in "".
Pachacuti builds [Terrace%20Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Terrace Farms) and [Qhapaq%20%C3%91an%20%28Civ6%29](Qhapaq Ñan) to grow sprawling [Mountain%20%28Civ6%29](mountain) [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities).
Intro.
 Pachacuti, son of the Sun, father to a prosperous people who dwell among lofty peaks. Let the mountains be roads to you, and mighty walls for your enemies, for you grow in strength where others would starve. O, Sapa Inca who shakes the earth, show strength and diligence, and your name will shine throughout all of history.
In-Game.
Pachacuti's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Sapa Inca. He will try to settle near Mountains whenever possible and dislikes other leaders who settle near Mountains.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Qhapaq Ñan. His domestic s provide +1 for every Mountain [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tile) in the origin city's [Territory%20%28Civ6%29](territory), and his [Builder%20%28Civ6%29](Builders) can build the [Qhapaq%20%C3%91an%20%28Civ6%29](Qhapaq Ñan) [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](improvement) when he discovers [Foreign%20Trade%20%28Civ6%29](Foreign Trade).
Detailed Approach.
The Inca's civilization home is mountain terrain. Their [Citizen%20%28Civ6%29](citizens) can work these tiles and gain additional yields for doing so. Terrace Farms help make their mountain cities grow even larger. Pachacuti as a leader of Inca allows them to construct Qhapaq Ñan. This improvement allows the Inca to traverse the mountains much sooner than other civilizations. Pachacuti also gives their mountain cities greater trade income.
