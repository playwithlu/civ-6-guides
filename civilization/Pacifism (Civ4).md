# Pacifism (Civ4)

Pacifism is a religion [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)".
Strategy.
Pacifism increases [Great%20Person%20%28Civ4%29](Great People) birth rate in [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities) that follow the [Religion%20%28Civ4%29](state religion), but also increases support costs for military [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ4](units).
