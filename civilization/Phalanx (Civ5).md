# Phalanx (Civ5)

The Phalanx is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](unique unit) from the [Wonders%20of%20the%20Ancient%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Wonders of the Ancient World) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Unique melee unit of the [Sumerian%20%28Civ5%29](Sumerian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Spearman%20%28Civ5%29](Spearman).
Strategy.
The Phalanx is the Sumerian unique unit, replacing the Spearman. It is available on an earlier [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ5](technology) ([Mining%20%28Civ5%29](Mining) instead of [Bronze%20Working%20%28Civ5%29](Bronze Working)) than the Spearman.
