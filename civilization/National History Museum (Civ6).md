# National History Museum (Civ6)

Awards +1 {{GovernorIcon6}} Governor Title.
The National History Museum is a Tier 3 [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) [Buildings%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "". It is built in the [Government%20Plaza%20%28Civ6%29](Government Plaza) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) and requires a Tier 3 government ([Communism%20%28Civ6%29](Communism), [Democracy%20%28Civ6%29](Democracy), or [Fascism%20%28Civ6%29](Fascism)) and a Tier 2 government building ([Foreign%20Ministry%20%28Civ6%29](Foreign Ministry), [Grand%20Master%27s%20Chapel%20%28Civ6%29](Grand Master's Chapel), [Intelligence%20Agency%20%28Civ6%29](Intelligence Agency), or [Queen%27s%20Bibliotheque%20%28Civ6%29](Queen's Bibliotheque)).
Strategy.
The National History Museum is quite useful if you're trying to win a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Cultural Victory). Any [Great%20Writer%20%28Civ6%29](Great Writers), [Great%20Artist%20%28Civ6%29](Great Artists), or [Great%20Musician%20%28Civ6%29](Great Musicians) you have on hand late in the game can fill its slots with their Great Works, providing you with extra {{Culture6}} and {{Tourism6}}. This is a must-build for [Sweden%20%28Civ6%29](Sweden) under [Kristina%20%28Civ6%29](Kristina), as this building is now theme-able once it is filled.
