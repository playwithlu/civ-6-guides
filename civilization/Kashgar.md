# Kashgar

Kashgar, also known as Kashi, is a [Mongol](Mongol)ian [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in Xinjiang in present-day [China](China).
