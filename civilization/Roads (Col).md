# Roads (Col)

Roads are built by [Pioneers%20%28Col%29](Pioneers) at the cost of 20 [Tools%20%28Col%29](Tools) per tile. They reduce movement costs by 1/3, as do rivers. Roads add 1 production to [Ore%20%28Col%29](Ore) mining and [Silver%20%28Col%29](Silver) mining and 2 production to [Lumber%20%28Col%29](Lumber) and [Furs%20%28Col%29](Furs).
