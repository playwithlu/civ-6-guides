# Space Bomber (CTP1)

The Space Bomber is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
Peering down from orbit, the Space Bomber dispenses destruction to the helpless [Earth](Earth)-bound enemy by bombarding them.
Great Library entry.
Where the 23rd century Star Cruiser used asteroid mining to produce thicker armor, the Space Bomber used it to create weaponry. Taking hunks of asteroid and coating them in ceramic heat shielding the Space Bomber unleashed a hailstorm of iron meteors. The destructive force of the combined impact approximated the strike of a tactical nuke.
