# Warlord (Civ3)/Civilopedia

The warlord is the fourth and highest level of barbarian in Fall of Rome. They represent the pinnacle of barbarian power as symbolized by Attila's army of Huns that terrorized Europe at the time of the battle of Chalons in 451 AD. Warlords strike terror into the hearts of citizens and soldiers alike and do not hesitate to burn and sack anything in their path.
