# Poland Civilization &amp; Scenario Pack (Civ6)

The Poland Civilization &amp; Scenario Pack is [DLC%20%28Civ6%29](DLC) for "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" released on December 20, 2016. It is now available to purchase on various digital game distributors including [Steam](Steam).
Steam Description.
"[Jadwiga%20%28Civ6%29](Jadwiga) rules [Polish%20%28Civ6%29](Poland) in Sid Meier's Civilization VI! Religious and expansionist, Poland can gain control of other civ's [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) when they fortify their borders, and Jadwiga makes Relics more valuable and [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Sites) more effective. A new [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) is also included: stand at the crossroads of Europe protecting the fertile Polish homelands from those who would seize it to empower themselves. Can you stand as a bulwark against this threat?" 
"Includes the complete Polish civilization with Jadwiga, [Winged%20Hussar%20%28Civ6%29](Winged Hussar) unique [Units%20%28Civ6%29](Unit), and [Sukiennice%20%28Civ6%29](Sukiennice) unique [Buildings%20%28Civ6%29](Building):"
