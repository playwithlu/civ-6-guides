# Terrain (CTP2)

Terrain is the features of the [Map%20%28CTP2%29](world map) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Terrain is affected by [Pollution%20%28CTP2%29](pollution) and the productivity of nearby [City%20%28CTP2%29](cities), and is sometimes a source of [Goods%20%28CTP2%29](goods) that can be traded.
