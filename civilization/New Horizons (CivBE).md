# New Horizons (CivBE)

New Horizons is a total conversion [Mods%20%28CivBE%29](mod) for "". It was created by [CivFanatics](CivFanatics) user Machiavelli24 (who also created [Echoes%20of%20Earth%20%28CivBE%29](Echoes of Earth)) and first released on January 25, 2015. It revamps the game's [Virtues%20%28CivBE%29](Virtues) system and replaces all the pre-game choices ([Colonists%20%28CivBE%29](Colonists), [Cargo%20%28CivBE%29](Cargo), [Spacecraft%20%28CivBE%29](Spacecraft)), early to mid-game [Tech%20Web%20%28CivBE%29](techs) and [Building%20%28CivBE%29](buildings), and [Units%20%28CivBE%29](military units) while also making changes to [Tile%20improvement%20%28CivBE%29](tile improvements), [Terrain%20%28CivBE%29](terrain) [Yield%20%28CivBE%29](yields), [Expedition%20%28CivBE%29](expeditions), the [Orbitals%20%28CivBE%29](orbital layer and units), and [City%20%28CivBE%29](city) strength and scaling.
New Horizons was featured in both a video posted on the Sid Meier's Civilization [YouTube](YouTube) channel and a Mod of the Week article published on Civilization.com. The mod does not work with "".
