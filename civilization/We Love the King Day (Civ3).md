# We Love the King Day (Civ3)

We Love the King Day is special event in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)". It can occur in any [City%20%28Civ3%29](city) with no unhappy [Citizen%20%28Civ3%29](citizens) and an equal or greater number of citizens that are happy compared to content citizens. A city's population must be at least 6. The benefits of the celebration begin the first full turn after the celebration is announced.
