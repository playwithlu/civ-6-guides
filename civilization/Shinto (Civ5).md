# Shinto (Civ5)

Shinto is one of thirteen eligible [Religion%20%28Civ5%29](religions) featured in the ' and ' expansions for "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Unlike the [Religion%20%28Civ4%29](religions) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)", this religion has no technological requirements to be unlocked and neither unique buildings nor a unique missionary unit. Like all other religions, Shinto can be founded directly after using a [Great%20Prophet%20%28Civ5%29](Great Prophet's) Found Religion ability and only one [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) can be the founder of this religion.
