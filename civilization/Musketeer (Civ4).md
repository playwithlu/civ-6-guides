# Musketeer (Civ4)

The Musketeer is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [French%20%28Civ4%29](French) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Musketman%20%28Civ4%29](Musketman) it replaces, the Musketeer has one more movement point. The Musketeer makes for a good stack defender, since it can actually keep pace with [List%20of%20mounted%20units%20in%20Civ4](cavalry).
