# Psi Attack (SMAC)

 is a unit weapon type in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)".
Game info.
The Psi Attack weapon allows a conventional unit to make Psi attacks. Weapons and armor are ignored in , and the attacker receives a 3 to 2 advantage on land (1 to 1 at sea).
