# Outback Station (Civ6)

The Outback Station is a unique of the [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It must be built on flat [Grassland%20%28Civ6%29](Grassland), [Plains%20%28Civ6%29](Plains), [Desert%20%28Civ6%29](Desert), or [Desert%20%28Hills%29%20%28Civ6%29](Desert Hills) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Strategy.
With the Outback Station, the Australians can change even barren deserts into rich and productive land. While its and bonuses are initially small, the adjacency bonuses it earns from Pastures, Steam Power, and Rapid Deployment can eventually make its yields quite impressive. To maximize these yields, found [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) near , , and . After improving these [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resources) with Pastures, encircle them with Outback Stations and watch your cities grow into sprawling metropolises.
The late game rewards building Outback Stations in clusters, similar to [Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Farm) clusters. Each Outback Station in a cluster of three will eventually receive +1 and bonuses, which only grow larger as the cluster expands.
