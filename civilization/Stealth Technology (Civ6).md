# Stealth Technology (Civ6)

Stealth Technology is an [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". 
Strategy.
Invisible bombers are the dream of every general. And modern technology is finally close to making it come true.
Although stealth camouflage is not true invisibility, the combination of shape, color, and special materials can make an airplane nigh impossible to detect on a radar. Thus the ultimate military plane - the [Jet%20Bomber%20%28Civ6%29](Jet Bomber) - becomes a reality. All but invisible to the enemy, it can appear out of nowhere, drop its deadly payload, and return from whence it came.
