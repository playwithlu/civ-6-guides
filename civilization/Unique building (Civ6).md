# Unique building (Civ6)

In "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)", a unique building is a special [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) that is only available to a specific [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) or [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader). Similar to unique districts, unique buildings will revert to their normal counterparts if their parent city changes ownership.
