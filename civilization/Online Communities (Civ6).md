# Online Communities (Civ6)

Online Communities is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Online Communities will help you leverage your s to make the final push toward a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Cultural Victory), causing each [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) to which you're sending a [Trader%20%28Civ6%29](Trader) to receive 50% more from you. If you manage to attract the [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchants) [Sarah%20Breedlove%20%28Civ6%29](Sarah Breedlove) and [Melitta%20Bentz%20%28Civ6%29](Melitta Bentz), the 100% bonus you get from their abilities and this policy will put you on the fast track to cultural dominance.
