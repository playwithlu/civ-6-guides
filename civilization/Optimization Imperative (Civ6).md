# Optimization Imperative (Civ6)

Optimization Imperative is an [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "". It can be hurried by researching [Robotics%20%28Civ6%29](Robotics). 
Strategy.
Discovering Optimization Imperative unlocks [Synthetic%20Technocracy%20%28Civ6%29](Synthetic Technocracy), a form of Tier 4 [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government). This government is suitable for any [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) that yearns to wrest control of the stars.
