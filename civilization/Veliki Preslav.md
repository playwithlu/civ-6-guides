# Veliki Preslav

Veliki Preslav, also known as Great Preslav, former Preslav, is a [city-state](city-state) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it is a city and the seat of government of the Veliki Preslav Municipality, which in turn is part of Shumen Province in present-day Bulgaria.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Bulgar](Bulgar) city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages) and a [Hunnic](Hunnic) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
