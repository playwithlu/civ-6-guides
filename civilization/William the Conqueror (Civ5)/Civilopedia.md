# William the Conqueror (Civ5)/Civilopedia

William the Conqueror (also known as William I) was the leader of the Norman Conquest of England, and would rule as England's first Norman king. Having defeated the army of Harold Godwinson at the Battle of Hastings in October of 1066, William would eventually quash the English resistance and rule for over 20 years until his death in 1087.
