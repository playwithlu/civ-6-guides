# Religion (CivRev)

Religion is a [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technology) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Strategy.
Religion is a tier 5 technology. A cultural technology, it can be used to build the [Cathedral%20%28CivRev%29](Cathedral), or be used to gain the [Fundamentalism%20%28CivRev%29](Fundamentalism) [Government%20%28CivRev%29](government) allowing for a more militaristic [Victory%20%28CivRev%29](victory). An early discoverer gets the bonus of having +1 [Culture%20%28CivRev%29](culture) added to all of their [City%20%28CivRev%29](cities).
