# Sanitation (Civ6)

Sanitation is an [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era) [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by building 2 [Neighborhood%20%28Civ6%29](Neighborhoods).
Strategy.
Sanitation may or may not be a high priority to research based on the [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victory) condition you're pursuing. The [Sewer%20%28Civ6%29](Sewer) is useful for situations where you need to add without dedicating the additional space to another [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) (i.e., an [Aqueduct%20%28Civ6%29](Aqueduct) or a Neighborhood) but adds only 2 , and Sanitation is often obtained after [Urbanization%20%28Civ6%29](Urbanization). Furthermore, Aqueducts and Neighborhoods provide [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonuses) to other districts, and Neighborhoods additionally allow the construction of [Shopping%20Mall%20%28Civ6%29](Shopping Malls) or [Food%20Market%20%28Civ6%29](Food Markets) in the expansions, while Sewers do neither.
The [Medic%20%28Civ6%29](Medic) is a somewhat useful [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](unit) for military campaigns, particularly for prolonged sieges where it can allow your units to heal more quickly and attack more often than they otherwise could. In the expansions, Medics become much more useful after researching [Combustion%20%28Civ6%29](Combustion), which allows them to upgrade to [Supply%20Convoy%20%28Civ6%29](Supply Convoys).
