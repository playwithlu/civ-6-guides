# Terraform (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Terraforming is the process of converting one terrain type into another. As empires expand all over the world, the need for good land becomes dire. Fortunately, players can use resources to Terraform undesirable terrain tiles into more useful, more productive land. As a player progresses through the technology tree, certain key enable the Terraforming of different terrain types.
