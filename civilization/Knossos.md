# Knossos

Knossos is the [Minoan](Minoan) [capital](capital) and a [Greek](Greek) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is an archaeological site on the island of Crete and major center of the Minoan [Civilizations](civilization).
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Macedonian](Macedonian) city in [Rise%20of%20Rome](Rise of Rome).
