# Oghuz

Oghuz, also known as Ghuzz, is a [Barbarian](Barbarian) [city](city) and tribe in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games. In real life, it were a western Turkic people.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the [Turkish](Turkish) [capital](capital) in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
