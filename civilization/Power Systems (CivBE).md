# Power Systems (CivBE)

Game Information.
Power Systems is a [Secondary%20Tech%20%28CivBE%29](Secondary Tech) that can be researched from [Engineering%20%28CivBe%29](Engineering). It improves the yield from [Mine%20%28CivBE%29](Mines) and [Geothermal%20Well%20%28CivBE%29](Geothermal Wells).
This technology provides 12 points towards the [Supremacy%20%28CivBE%29](Supremacy) affinity.
