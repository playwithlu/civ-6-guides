# Explorer (Civ5)

The Explorer is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](unique) recon [Unit%20%28Civ5%29](unit) of the [Belgian%20%28Civ5%29](Belgian), [English%20%28Civ5%29](English), [French%20%28Civ5%29](French), [German%20%28Civ5%29](German), [Italian%20%28Civ5%29](Italian), and [Portuguese%20%28Civ5%29](Portuguese) [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) from the [Scramble%20for%20Africa%20%28Civ5%29](Scramble for Africa) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "". It replaces the [Archaeologist%20%28Civ5%29](Archaeologist).
Game Info.
A special archaeologist that is not slowed by rough terrain and has a weak attack and defense. Only the Europeans can build it. Does not require a University. Like all units in this scenario it does not have the Explore command.
Strategy.
European archaeologist replacement. It can attack and defend unlike the archaeologist. It can also move through rough terrain as if the terrain was clear. It does not require a University.
