# Sailing (Civ4)

Strategy.
Sailing confers multiple benefits. It allows you to train the [Galley%20%28Civ4%29](Galley) unit, which is useful for early exploration, and enables [trade](trade) along coasts and rivers, increasing your commerce. In addition, Sailing allows the [Lighthouse%20%28Civ4%29](Lighthouse) and [Moai%20Statues%20%28Civ4%29](Moai Statues) [List%20of%20wonders%20in%20Civ4%23National%20Wonders](national wonder) to be built. These improve a city's growth and production respectively.
