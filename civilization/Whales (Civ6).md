# Whales (Civ6)

 Whales are a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are found exclusively in [Coast%20%28Civ6%29](Coast) [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles).
Strategy.
 Whales are one of four water-based luxury resources (after the addition of and ). The and bonuses from them can help [Coastal%20%28Civ6%29](coastal) cities develop rapidly.
