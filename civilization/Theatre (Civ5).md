# Theatre (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Renaissance%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Renaissance Era) entertainment building. Requires [Colosseum%20%28Civ5%29](Colosseum).
Strategy.
The Theatre is a mid-level provider, appearing at a time when you may start expanding your empire overseas. As such, it is very important.
The Theatre is removed in "", likely to avoid confusion with the similarly-named [Amphitheater%20%28Civ5%29](Amphitheater). It is replaced by the [Zoo%20%28Civ5%29](Zoo).
