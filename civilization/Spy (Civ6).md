# Spy (Civ6)

The Spy is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Civilian](civilian unit) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It manifests the game's [Espionage%20%28Civ6%29](espionage) system.
Strategy.
Each player can support only a limited number of Spies. A player's Spy capacity increases by 1 for each of these:
If the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) is enabled, being a member of the [Owls%20of%20Minerva%20%28Civ6%29](Owls of Minerva) and taking [Indoctrination%20%28Civ6%29](Indoctrination) increases a player's Spy capacity by 2.
