# Worker

The Worker is a special [unit](unit) in some "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "Civ"-style games. It does the same development work as [Settler](Settler)s (and some done by [Engineer](Engineer)s) but cannot found [City](cities).
Powers as for [Settlers](Settlers) but do not reduce city population and cannot add to city population or found city.
