# Republic (CivRev)

Republic is a form of [Government%20%28CivRev%29](government) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". It can be adopted after researching [Code%20of%20Laws%20%28CivRev%29](Code of Laws).
Republic lowers the [Population%20%28CivRev%29](population) cost of producing [Settlers%20%28CivRev%29](Settlers) from 2 to 1, allowing for earlier and more rapid expansion. [Julius%20Caesar%20%28CivRev%29](Julius Caesar), the [Roman%20%28CivRev%29](Roman) [Leaders%20%28CivRev%29](leader), begins the game with a Republic.
Leaders who adopt this form of government are given the title of "Emperor," and other leaders sometimes refer to it as an "inclusive" form of government.
