# Scottish (CTP2)

The Scottish people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Their default leader names are [William%20Wallace](William Wallace) for a male and [Mary%20Stuart](Mary Stuart) for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Glasgow](Glasgow).
