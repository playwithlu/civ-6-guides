# Oral Tradition (Civ6)

Oral Tradition was a [Pantheon%20%28Civ6%29](Pantheon) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It granted +1 to all [Plantation%20%28Civ6%29](Plantation) improvements built on [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) with , , , , , , or . This Pantheon was removed from "" in the [Civilization%20VI%20June%202019%20Update](June 2019 Update).
Strategy.
Like other [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource)-dependent pantheons, Oral Tradition is somewhat risky, as it requires the presence of these specific resources within your [Territory%20%28Civ6%29](territory). Oral Tradition is most useful for [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) that are interested in a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory), as these Plantations will provide a burst of additional once [Flight%20%28Civ6%29](Flight) is researched. Additionally, these resources will provide faster [Borders%20%28Civ6%29](border) growth for newly settled [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) without requiring the building of [Monument%20%28Civ6%29](Monuments).
