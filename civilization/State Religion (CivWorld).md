# State Religion (CivWorld)

 is a [Civic%20%28CivWorld%29](Civic) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 25% bonus to Food output and a 25% penalty to Science output. It can be adopted after discovering [Ceremonial%20Burial%20%28CivWorld%29](Ceremonial Burial) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Mass%20Media%20%28CivWorld%29](Mass Media).
