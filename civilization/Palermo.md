# Palermo

Palermo, also known by its historical names of Balarm, Panormus, Ziz, or Zis, is a [Carthaginian](Carthaginian), [Italian](Italian), [Phoenician](Phoenician), and [Roman](Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)", and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in present-day Italy.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the Italian [capital](capital) in [Scramble%20for%20Africa](Scramble for Africa) and a [Vandal](Vandal) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
