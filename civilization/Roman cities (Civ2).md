# Roman cities (Civ2)

The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
List of cities.
Hispalis and Caesaraugusta are the equivalent of the contemporary Seville and Saragossa in the [Spanish%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Spanish city list). Lugdunum, Lutetia, and Aurelianorum are the equivalents of Lyons, Paris, and Orleans in [French%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](French). Londinium and Eburacum are the equivalent of London and York in [English%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](English), and the [Viking%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Viking city) Jorvik. Agrippina is equivalent to the [German%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](German city) Cologne. Tyrus is equivalent to the [Persian%20cities%20%28Civ2%29](Persian city) Tyre.
Neapolis, Pisae and Mediolanum are repeated as their contemporary Italian names (Naples, Pisa, and Milan) in the extra name list available to all civs.
