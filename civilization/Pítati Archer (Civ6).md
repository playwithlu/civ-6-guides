# Pítati Archer (Civ6)

The Pítati Archer is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Ranged](ranged unit) of the [Nubian%20%28Civ6%29](Nubian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It replaces the [Archer%20%28Civ6%29](Archer).
Strategy.
The Pítati Archer is a superb replacement for the Archer: it's stronger, quicker, and earns s 50% faster thanks to the Nubians' [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](civilization ability). Its one drawback is its higher cost, though the Nubians' bonuses toward training ranged units help offset this.
All things considered, the Pítati Archer is hands down the best ranged unit until the [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era):
The Nubians can gain a military advantage early on by training several Pítati Archers, which they can use to launch lightning raids on [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarian) outposts and unsuspecting neighbors.
Pítati Archers become [obsolete](obsolete) with [Ballistics%20%28Civ6%29](Ballistics), though they'll be replaced by [Crossbowman%20%28Civ6%29](Crossbowmen) long before this [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technology) is discovered.
