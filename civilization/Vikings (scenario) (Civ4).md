# Vikings (scenario) (Civ4)

Vikings is a [scenario%20%28Civ4%29](scenario) in "" that puts you in control of the [Viking%20%28Civ4%29](Viking) empire.
Gameplay.
In addition to the Vikings, the [English%20%28Civ4%29](English), the [French%20%28Civ4%29](French), the [Irish](Irish), and the [Holy%20Roman%20%28Civ4%29](Holy Romans) appear in this scenario as minor [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilizations).
Aside from the normal means of collecting [Gold%20%28currency%29%20%28Civ4%29](gold), this scenario gives you the option of ransoming captured [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities), or returning them to their original owners in exchange for a one-time gold payment. These payments start at 200 gold, but can be upwards of 1000 gold for the largest cities. Be warned that if you recapture a city that you previously ransomed, the other civilizations will know that you cannot be trusted and you will no longer have the option to ransom cities.
Victory.
Your goal is to collect 30,000 gold, though the target number increases on higher [Difficulty%20level%20%28Civ4%29](difficulty levels).
