# Nuclear Program (Civ6)

Nuclear Program is a [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by building a [Research%20Lab%20%28Civ6%29](Research Lab).
Strategy.
Developing a nuclear program is seen as one of the pinnacles of modern society. Why exactly the capability of a nation to utterly destroy another nation, or the entire planet, would be an achievement for civilization remains a little unclear. Still, it is undeniable that a nuclear program concentrates the greatest scientific minds of a nation, and spurs new development.
In , Nuclear Program unlocks one of the most powerful Wildcard [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policies) in the game: Science Foundations, for attracting [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ6%29](Great Scientists) and [Great%20Engineer%20%28Civ6%29](Great Engineers). The other Policy unlocked is Diplomatic, and helps acquire [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technologies) even faster. All this makes Nuclear Program very important for [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) striving for [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Scientific Victory).
The civic also awards 3 {{Envoy6}} - nothing commands the respect of smaller nations like a nuclear threat.
