# Special unit (CivRev)

A special unit is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CivRev](unit) that does not fall into any of the other unit classes and cannot be produced or obtained in the same manner as other units. There are four special units in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)": [Great%20Person%20%28CivRev%29](Great People) (which can provide bonuses to your [City%20%28CivRev%29](cities) or, in the case of the [Great%20General%20%28CivRev%29](Great General), boost the attack and [Defense%20%28CivRev%29](defense) of the unit in its [Tile%20%28CivRev%29](tile)), the [ICBM%20%28CivRev%29](ICBM) (which is awarded for building the [Manhattan%20Project%20%28CivRev%29](Manhattan Project) and can be used to launch a single devastating attack), the [Militia%20%28CivRev%29](Militia) (which appears when you found your first city next to a [Barbarian%20%28CivRev%29](Barbarian) village), and the [Ranger%20Militia%20%28CivRev%29](Ranger Militia) (which appears when you train a [Galley%20%28CivRev%29](Galley) or [Galleon%20%28CivRev%29](Galleon)).
