# Writing (CivRev)

Writing is a tier two [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technology) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)". It requires [Alphabet%20%28CivRev%29](Alphabet) to be researched and once done so, it unlocks the [Spy%20%28CivRev%29](Spy) and the [Great%20Library%20of%20Alexandria%20%28CivRev%29](Great Library of Alexandria) [Wonder%20%28CivRev%29](wonder). It also unlocks the tech [Literacy%20%28CivRev%29](Literacy).
The first player to discover Writing will receive [Aesop%20%28CivRev%29](Aesop).
