# Veterans (CTP1)

Military Units can become veterans by winning battles. When a unit achieves veteran status, it receives a 50% bonus to its assault statistic when attacking. 
Great Library entry.
Veteran units are battle-hardened troops whose combat experience gives them added strength and cunning. From the Auxillarium legion of ancient Rome to the SS Panzer divisions of WWII, veteran units have determined the outcome of many major battles.
