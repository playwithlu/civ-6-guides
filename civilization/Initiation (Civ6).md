# Initiation (Civ6)

Initiation is the starting title for the [Owls%20of%20Minerva%20%28Civ6%29](Owls of Minerva), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked by sending an {{Envoy6}} to a [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state).
Effects.
+1 {{EconomicPolicy6}} Economic Policy slot. Each {{TradeRoute6}} sent to a city-state grants 1 {{Envoy6}} there.
