# Indian (Civ1)

The Indians are a playable [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ1](civilization) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)". Their [Leaders%20%28Civ1%29](leader) is [Mohandas%20Gandhi%20%28Civ1%29](Mohandas Gandhi), their [Capital%20%28Civ1%29](capital) is [Delhi](Delhi), and their color is gray (which they share with the [Mongol%20%28Civ1%29](Mongols)).
