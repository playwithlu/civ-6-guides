# Plow (Col)

Plowing increases the crop yield of terrain tiles by 1: [food%20%28Col%29](food), [cotton%20%28Col%29](cotton), [tobacco%20%28Col%29](tobacco), [sugar%20%28Col%29](sugar).
Plowing is performed by [Pioneers%20%28Col%29](Pioneers) at the cost of 20 [Tools%20%28Col%29](Tools) per tile.
A tile must be cleared of trees before it can be plowed.
Clearing is also performed by Pioneers at the cost of 20 Tools per tile.
