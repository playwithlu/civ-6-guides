# Sofia

Sofia, also known as Sofiya, is a [city-state](city-state) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games and a [Slavic](Slavic) [city](city) in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games), and also appears (under its historical names of Sardica, Serdica, or Serdika) as a [Byzantine](Byzantine) [city](city). In real life, it is the [capital](capital) of and largest city in present-day Bulgaria.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Bulgar](Bulgar) city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages) and a [Hunnic](Hunnic) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
