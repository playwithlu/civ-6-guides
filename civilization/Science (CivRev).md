# Science (CivRev)

Science is what allows you to research [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technologies). The more science you have the faster you can research new technologies.
The spots you may get science from are [Desert%20%28CivRev%29](desert) regions and the [Sea%20%28CivRev%29](sea). [Library%20%28CivRev%29](Libraries), [University%20%28building%29%20%28CivRev%29](Universities), and [Great%20Scientist%20%28CivRev%29](Great Scientists) increase the amount of science produced.
