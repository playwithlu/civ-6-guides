# Uranium (Civ4)

Strategy.
Uranium provides commerce to the city that works it. Among all tile resources, Uranium is exceptional in that it does not add any bonus yield to its tile, unless improved with a [Mine%20%28Civ4%29](Mine). It is required for the production of many of the late-game military units and [Nuclear%20weapons%20%28Civ4%29](nuclear weapons) and doubles the production speed of the [The%20Manhattan%20Project%20%28Civ4%29](Manhattan Project).
