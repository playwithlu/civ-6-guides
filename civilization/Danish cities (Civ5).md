 are [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) that can be founded by the [Danish%20%28Civ5%29](Danish) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" and its expansions. The first city founded is the , and the names of future cities are chosen in the order they appear on the list below.
The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
This list notably excludes [Swedish%20cities%20%28Civ5%29](Swedish cities), but also has [Norwegian%20cities](Norwegian cities), as well as one Icelandic, two ancient [North%20America](North America)n settlements, one Greenlandic and one literally named "Faroe Islands" in addition to the Danish ones.
