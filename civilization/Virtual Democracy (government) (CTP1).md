Virtual Democracy is a form of [Government%20%28CTP1%29](government) in "". Its effects are excellent on growth, bad on production, excellent on science, good on gold, and bad on military.
Summary.
For large empires, Virtual Democracy inspires the greatest progress in science. Virtual Democracy uses nanotechnology to feed and clothe, provides opportunity for all, and governs through virtual representation. 
Great Library entry.
Following economic crisis brought about by new Nanotechnologies and moral crisis brought about by centuries of depravity, intellectuals grabbed the reigns of virtual power and fought a successful struggle to create the Virtual Democracy. By giving a vote to each citizen and truly eliminating discrimination and corruption, the Virtual Democracy was a key to the healing of humanity.
