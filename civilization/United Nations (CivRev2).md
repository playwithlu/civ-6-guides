# United Nations (CivRev2)

The United Nations is a wonder in "[Civilization%20Revolution%202](Civilization Revolution 2)". It is unlocked when the player acquires a total of 20 [Great%20Person%20%28CivRev2%29](Great People), wonders, and culturally-flipped [City%20%28CivRev2%29](cities). Once it is built, the player wins a [Victory%20%28CivRev2%29](Cultural Victory).
