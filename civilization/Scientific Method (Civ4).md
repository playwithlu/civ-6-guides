# Scientific Method (Civ4)

Strategy.
Scientific Method reveals the [Oil%20%28Civ4%29](Oil) resource and allows construction of the [Forest%20Preserve%20%28Civ4%29](Forest Preserve) improvement. It makes the [Great%20Library%20%28Civ4%29](Great Library), the [Parthenon%20%28Civ4%29](Parthenon), the [Temple%20of%20Artemis%20%28Civ4%29](Temple of Artemis) and [Monastery%20%28Civ4%29](Monasteries) obsolete.
