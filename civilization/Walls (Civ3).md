# Walls (Civ3)

The is a [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ3](building) in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)".
Description.
Walls can be built only in town-size settlements and provide a [Bombard%20%28Civ3%29](land bombardment) defense of eight, and a 50% defensive bonus to [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ3](units) defending in the town. When a town becomes a , the walls no longer provide a defensive bonus.
