# Oil Well (Civ6)

The Oil Well is a standard [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](tile improvement) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is used to access land-based [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resources).
In the "" expansion, the Oil Well is unlocked with [Refining%20%28Civ6%29](Refining).
Strategy.
Oil Wells extract from land [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles), providing a resource and extra at the expense of the surrounding tiles' [Appeal%20%28Civ6%29](Appeal). The [Tank%20%28Civ6%29](Tanks) and [Aircraft%20Carrier%20%28Civ6%29](Aircraft Carriers) that can be built with , however, are a great boon to military-focused [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations).
