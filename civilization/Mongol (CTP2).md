# Mongol (CTP2)

The Mongol people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Their default leader names are [Genghis%20Khan](Genghis Khan) for a male and [Bortei](Bortei) for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Karakorum](Karakorum).
