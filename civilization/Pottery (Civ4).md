# Pottery (Civ4)

Strategy.
Pottery improves commerce and growth. It allows your [Worker%20%28Civ4%29](Workers) to build [Cottage%20%28Civ4%29](Cottage) improvements, which generate increasing amounts of commerce the longer they are worked. Pottery also enables the [Granary%20%28Civ4%29](Granary) building, which makes grow faster.
