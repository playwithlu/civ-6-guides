# Valley of the Kings (Civ6)

Valley of the Kings is a Wildcard [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
Valley of the Kings doubles the [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonuses) of all [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Sites), providing extra to purchase [Shrine%20%28Civ6%29](Shrines) and [Temple%20to%20Amun%20%28Civ6%29](Temples to Amun).
