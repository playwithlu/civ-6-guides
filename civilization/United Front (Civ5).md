# United Front (Civ5)

United Front is a [Social%20policies%20%28Civ5%29](social policy) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". It is part of the [Order%20%28Civ5%29](Order) tree. In "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)" it is a Level 1 [Ideology%20%28Civ5%29%23Autocracy](Autocracy) tenet with the same effect.
Game Info.
Militaristic [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](City-States) grant units twice as often when you are at war with a common foe.
