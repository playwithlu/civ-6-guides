# Totalitarianism (Civ6)

Totalitarianism is a [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by building 3 [Military%20Academy%20%28Civ6%29](Military Academies).
Strategy.
Developing Totalitarianism enables the [Fascism%20%28Civ6%29](Fascism Government), the second type of modern government available. It again makes many old Policies [obsolete](obsolete), and replaces them with new Economic, Military and Diplomatic ones.
