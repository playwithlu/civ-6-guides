# Plastics (Civ4)

Strategy.
Plastics enables [Work%20boat%20%28Civ4%29](Work Boats) to construct [Offshore%20platform%20%28Civ4%29](Offshore Platforms). It also lets you build [Hydro%20Plant%20%28Civ4%29](Hydro Plants) and the [Standard%20Ethanol](Standard Ethanol) and [Three%20Gorges%20Dam%20%28Civ4%29](Three Gorges Dam) wonders, and obsoletes [Furs%20%28Civ4%29](Furs).
