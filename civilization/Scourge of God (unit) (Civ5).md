# Scourge of God (unit) (Civ5)

The Scourge of God is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](unique) [Great%20People%20%28Civ5%29](Great Person) of the [Hunnic%20%28Civ5%29](Hunnic) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) from the [Fall%20of%20Rome%20%28Civ5%29](Fall of Rome) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "".
Game Info.
This Hun Great Person replaces the [Great%20General%20%28Civ5%29](Great General). While providing the same combat bonus as a Great General, the Scourge of God also moves at a faster rate and heals adjacent units.
Strategy.
The Scourge of God is a Hun Unique Great Person, replacing the standard Great General. The same combat bonuses apply, but the Scourge moves at a faster rate and can heal adjacent units for an additional 20 HP per turn. This enhanced General ensures that his cavalry units will always be in a battle-ready state.
