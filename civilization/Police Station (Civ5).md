# Police Station (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced counter-espionage building. Requires [Constabulary%20%28Civ5%29](Constabulary).
Strategy.
The Police Station increases the time it takes for an enemy [Espionage%20%28Civ5%29](spy) to steal your [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ5](technology) by 25% - the ideal deal for all conspiracy paranoids. It is necessary to have a Police Station in every [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) to build the [National%20Intelligence%20Agency%20%28Civ5%29](National Intelligence Agency).
