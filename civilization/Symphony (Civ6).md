# Symphony (Civ6)

Symphony is the Level 3 title for the [Voidsingers%20%28Civ6%29](Voidsingers), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era).
Effects.
Unlocks [Dark%20Summoning%20%28Civ6%29](Dark Summoning), a city [Project%20%28Civ6%29](project) that provides lots of while active, and upon completion, raises the amount of [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](Loyalty) damage done by [Cultist%20%28Civ6%29](Cultists).
