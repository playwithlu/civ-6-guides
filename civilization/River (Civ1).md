# River (Civ1)

Rivers are great sites for starting [City%20%28Civ1%29](cities) and civilizations dues to the richness of riverbank soils and natural trade routes for boats. Rivers are as good as Grasslands for producing [Food%20%28Civ1%29](food) and always generate [Trade%20%28Civ1%29](trade). River terrain may be [Irrigation%20%28Civ1%29](irrigated) to increase food production. It was no accident that the first civilizations sprang up along rivers. [Roads%20%28Civ1%29](Roads) can be built across rivers after [Bridge%20Building%20%28Civ1%29](Bridge Building) is discovered.
Rivers produce 2 food, either 0 or 1 [Production%20%28Civ1%29](resource) and 1 trade.
It costs 1 Movement Point to travel over a River region.
Rivers offer a +50% defense bonus.
