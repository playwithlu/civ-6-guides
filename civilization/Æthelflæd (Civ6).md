# Æthelflæd (Civ6)

Æthelflæd is a [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". Her unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Medieval and Renaissance era land units within 2 s.
