# Harappan (CTP2)

The Harappan people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)", introduced in [Apolyton%20Edition%20%28CTP2%29](Apolyton Edition). Their default leader names are Melukhkha for a male and Melukhkhet for a female, and their [Capital%20%28CTP2%29](capital) is [Harappa](Harappa).
