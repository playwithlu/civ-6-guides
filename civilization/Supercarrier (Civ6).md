# Supercarrier (Civ6)

Supercarrier is a Tier IV for [Units%20%28Civ6%29%23Naval%20Carrier](naval carrier units) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It requires [Deck%20Crews%20%28Civ6%29](Deck Crews) or [Folding%20Wings%20%28Civ6%29](Folding Wings).
Effects.
Can heal outside of friendly territory.
