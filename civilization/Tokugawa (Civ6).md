# Tokugawa (Civ6)

Tokugawa Ieyasu (31 January 1543 – 1 June 1616), born Matsudaira Takechiyo, is considered one of the three great unifiers of Japan alongside [Oda%20Nobunaga](Oda Nobunaga) and . After defeating the at the in 1600, he founded the , which would govern Japan for over two hundred years, and ruled as its first from 1603 until his death. He leads the [Japanese%20%28Civ6%29](Japanese) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Japan under Tokugawa likes to isolate itself by creating a lot of Domestic s and [District%20%28Civ6%29](districts).
Intro.
 Great Shōgun Tokugawa Ieyasu, you know well that the patient blade is the one that will strike home. Guard your homeland carefully from usurpers domestic and foreign, and trust in the industry and resourcefulness of your people, for they alone can be relied upon. The task of rule is one that is long, and its burdens are heavy, but there is no room for discontent, nor for despair, with your hand upon the sword.
In-Game.
Tokugawa's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Sakoku. He wants to stay safe from conquest, liking those who do the same and disliking those who have conquered other [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations') s.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Bakuhan. His international s receive 25% penalties to all yields and , but his domestic s provide +1 , +1 , and +2 for every specialty district at the destination. Additionally, cities within 6 [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) of his receive +4 and, after researching [Flight%20%28Civ6%29](Flight), receive +1 for every specialty district. From "" onward, cities within 6 tiles of his stay 100% [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](loyal) instead of receiving +4 .
Detailed Approach.
The Meiji Restoration ability gives large [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonuses) to Japan’s districts. Districts also receive +1 if they are in a city near the from Tokugawa’s ability. His ability also greatly increases Domestic s’ yields for every district. But be aware that International s are much weaker. The [Electronics%20Factory%20%28Civ6%29](Electronics Factory) will help him towards a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture) or [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory).
