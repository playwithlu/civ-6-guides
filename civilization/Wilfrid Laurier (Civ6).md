In-Game.
Wilfrid Laurier's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Canadian Expeditionary Force. He responds to [Emergency%20%28Civ6%29](Emergencies) whenever possible and dislikes leaders who refuse to participate in Emergencies.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is The Last Best West. He can build [Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Farms) on Tundra [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles) (and [Tundra%20%28Hills%29%20%28Civ6%29](Tundra Hills) tiles after discovering [Civil%20Engineering%20%28Civ6%29](Civil Engineering)) and receives a 50% discount on purchase costs of [Snow%20%28Civ6%29](Snow), [Snow%20%28Hills%29%20%28Civ6%29](Snow Hills), Tundra, and Tundra Hills tiles. Also, on those tiles, [Resource%20%28Civ6%29](resources) accumulate twice as fast, [Mine%20%28Civ6%29](Mines) and [Lumber%20Mill%20%28Civ6%29](Lumber Mills) receive 2 extra , and [Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Farms) and [Camp%20%28Civ6%29](Camps) 2 .
Detailed Approach.
Canada is a civilization that thrives in the Snow and Tundra. Canada can generate more using the [Ice%20Hockey%20Rink%20%28Civ6%29](Ice Hockey Rink) [Tile%20improvement%20%28Civ6%29](improvement) and [Mountie%20%28Civ6%29](Mountie) [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit). Their diplomatic skills allow them to influence [World%20Congress%20%28Civ6%29](World Congress). Laurier will always want to join Emergencies in order to accumulate the most in the World Congress.
