# Mycenaean (Civ3)

The {{CleanPageName}} people (or Mycenians) represent Mycenaean Greece, a playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) from the [Mesopotamia%20%28Civ3%29](Mesopotamia) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ3](scenario) in "". They are led by [Agamemnon%20%28Civ3%29](Agamemnon).
Description.
The Mycenaeans are [Scientific%20%28Civ3%29](scientific) and [Industrious%20%28Civ3%29](industrious). They start the game with [Warrior%20Code%20%28Civ3%29](Warrior Code) and [Stone%20Working%20%28Civ3%29](Stone Working), and build [Hoplite%20%28Civ3%29](Hoplites) instead of [Spearman%20%28Civ3%29](spearmen).
