# Russian (Civ1)

The Russians are a playable [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ1](civilization) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)". Their [Leaders%20%28Civ1%29](leader) is [Stalin%20%28Civ1%29](Stalin), their [Capital%20%28Civ1%29](capital) is [Moscow](Moscow), and their color is white (which they share with the [Roman%20%28Civ1%29](Romans)).
The Russians have a chance of starting the game with two [Settlers%20%28Civ1%29](Settlers) instead of one.
