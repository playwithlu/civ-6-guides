# Slavic

The {{PAGENAME}} people, also known as the Slavs, represent a [Civilizations](civilization) in the [Call%20to%20Power%20games]("Call to Power" games). Their [capital](capital) is [Beograd](Beograd), and their [Leaders](leader) is [Vlad%20the%20Impaler](Vlad the Impaler).
The [Kievan%20Rus%27](Kievan Rus'), [Polish](Polish), [Russian](Russian), and [Volhynian](Volhynian) people may also be represented by the Slavs.
