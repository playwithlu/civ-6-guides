# Obelisk (Civ4)

The Obelisk is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [Egyptian%20%28Civ4%29](Egyptian civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Monument%20%28Civ4%29](Monument) which it replaces, the Obelisk allows the city it is built in to convert 2 citizens into [Priest%20%28Civ4%29](Priests).
