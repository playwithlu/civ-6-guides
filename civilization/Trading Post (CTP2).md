# Trading Post (CTP2)

The {{CleanPageName}} is a [List%20of%20improvements%20in%20CTP2](tile improvement) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Trading Posts are the most primitive of commercial {{LinkCTP2|Tile Improvements}}. They attract traders from around the globe and provide a marketplace outside of the city, increasing {{LinkCTP2|Commerce}} by 10. Build them on any land tile.
