# Water (Civ5)

Water is very important to any [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) and can be found in many forms.
Fresh water.
Fresh water is very important for your growth and you should always try to secure access to some for your [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities). A city is considered to have access to fresh water if it is settled next to it. Mousing over a tile with tooltips enabled will tell you if it has fresh water.
