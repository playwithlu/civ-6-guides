# Themistocles (Civ6)

Themistocles is a [Great%20Admiral%20%28Civ6%29](Great Admiral) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Classical and Medieval era naval units within 2 s.
Themistocles can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to instantly create a unit in standard games, or to grant +2 to a city in "". In "[Gathering%20Storm](Gathering Storm)", he can be retired to create a Quadrireme and grant +20% towards Naval Ranged promotion class.
