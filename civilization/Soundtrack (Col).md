# Soundtrack (Col)

This is the soundtrack for "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Colonization](Sid Meier's Colonization)". It was originally in the MOD, XM, S3M, and IT formats. Its composers were Anthony Putson, Allister Brimble (Amiga), Ken Lagace (DOS), [Jeff%20Briggs](Jeff Briggs) (DOS), and [Roland%20Rizzo](Roland Rizzo) (DOS).
The music below is listed in the order found on the Pick Music menu of "Colonization".
