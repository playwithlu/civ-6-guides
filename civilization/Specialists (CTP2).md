# Specialists (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Specialists represent groups of citizens who are not primarily involved in the collection of resources. Rather, they can help boost certain specific resources in order to achieve a particular goal in the city. Each specialist is enabled by a specific and will be unavailable the advance is discovered.
