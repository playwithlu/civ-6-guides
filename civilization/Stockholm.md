# Stockholm

Stockholm is the [Swedish](Swedish) [capital](capital) and a [Viking](Viking) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the capital of and most populous city in present-day Sweden as well as the largest urban area in the Nordic countries.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a Swedish city.
