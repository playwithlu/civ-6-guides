# Tiger I Tank (CTP2)

The Tiger I is one of many tanks that are in the World War 2 global campaign for "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
History.
These tanks were used by the German Civilization. During [World%20War%20II%20era%20%28CTP2%29](World War II) they operated in all theatres of the war, serving with distinction in the battle of Villers-Bocage, in which a few Tiger tanks destroyed several tanks and anti-tank guns.
