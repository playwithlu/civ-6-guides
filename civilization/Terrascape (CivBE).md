A Terrascape is a [tile%20improvement%20%28CivBE%29](tile improvement) unlocked with [Terraforming](Terraforming) technology. Unlike other tile improvements, instead of adding to the tile's existing yield, a Terrascape replaces the yield with 2 , 2 , and 2 .

Strategy.
