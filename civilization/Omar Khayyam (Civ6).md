# Omar Khayyam (Civ6)

Omar Khayyam is a {{Link6|Medieval Era}} [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ6%29](Great Scientist) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Unique ability.
Triggers the {{Eureka6}} for 2 random [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technologies) and the {{Inspiration6}} for 1 random [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) from the Medieval or Renaissance era.
