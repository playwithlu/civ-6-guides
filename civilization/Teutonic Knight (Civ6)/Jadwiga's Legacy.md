# Teutonic Knight (Civ6)/Jadwiga's Legacy

The Teutonic Knight is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Heavy%20cavalry](heavy cavalry unit) of the [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarians) from the [Jadwiga%27s%20Legacy%20%28Civ6%29](Jadwiga's Legacy) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
In one of the updates, the Teutonic Knight was removed from the scenario and replaced by the standard [Knight%20%28Civ6%29](Knight).
Strategy.
The Teutonic Knight is identical to a standard Knight.
