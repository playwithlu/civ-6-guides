# Plasmatica (CTP1)

The Plasmatica is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
A futuristic infantry, the Plasmatica has plasma cannons cybernetically mounted on her arms, which she uses to combat enemy war units. She is an excellent defender.
