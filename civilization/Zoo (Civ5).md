# Zoo (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced [Happiness%20%28Civ5%29](happiness)-boosting [Building%20%28Civ5%29](building). Replaces the [Theatre%20%28Civ5%29](Theatre) in "". Requires [Colosseum%20%28Civ5%29](Colosseum).
Strategy.
The Zoo is a building included in the "Brave New World" expansion and replaces the Theatre, compared to which it generates +2 {{Happiness5}} instead of +3.
A Zoo increases the {{Happiness5}} of your empire, but it cannot provide more {{Happiness5}} than there are {{Citizen5}}s in the [City%20%28Civ5%29](city). The city must possess a Colosseum before the Zoo can be constructed.
