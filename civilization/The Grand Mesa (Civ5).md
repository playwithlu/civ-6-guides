# The Grand Mesa (Civ5)

The Grand Mesa is a [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonder) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". It spawns in [Plains%20%28Civ5%29](plains), [Desert%20%28Civ5%29](desert), [Tundra%20%28Civ5%29](tundra), and [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](mountain) tiles.
Strategy.
The Grand Mesa provides small but useful and bonuses to the [City%20%28Civ5%29](city) that works it.
