# Urbanization (Civ6)

Urbanization is an [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by growing a [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) to 15 Population.
Strategy.
Technological progress has another, quite unexpected result: the explosive growth of cities. With fewer jobs to be found in the countryside, thanks to mechanization of agriculture and mining, people have to move where the factories and company offices are: the cities. Which quickly become overcrowded.
To resolve this problem, it becomes necessary to develop a new : the [Neighborhood%20%28Civ6%29](Neighborhood). Your cities are now able to expand well beyond their natural limits.
