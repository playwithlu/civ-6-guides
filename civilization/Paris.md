# Paris

Paris is the [French](French) [capital](capital) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and elsewhere, and also appears (under its original name of Lutetia) as a [Celtic](Celtic), [Gallic](Gallic), and [Roman](Roman) [city](city). In real life, it is the capital of and most populous city in present-day France.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the [Frankish](Frankish) capital in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages) and a Frankish and [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in others.
