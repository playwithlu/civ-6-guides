# Shakespeare's Theater (CivWorld)

 is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 25% bonus to Culture output. It is available after discovering [Literacy%20%28CivWorld%29](Literacy) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Mass%20Media%20%28CivWorld%29](Mass Media).
