# Monotheism (Civ6)

Monotheism is a [Civics%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in the [Vikings%2C%20Traders%2C%20and%20Raiders%21%20%28Civ6%29](Vikings, Traders, and Raiders!) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". For all practical purposes, it is obtained by having a majority [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](religion) in one's [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities), which provides 100% of its {{Culture6}} cost.
