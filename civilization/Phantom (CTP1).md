# Phantom (CTP1)

The Phantom is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
The Phantom moves through [Space%20%28CTP1%29](space) truly unseen. Unlike other [Stealth%20units%20%28CTP1%29](stealth units), the Phantom doesn't hide so much as disappear. By folding most of its matter out of the four dimensions of space-time, the Phantom loses all materiality. However, if caught in its cloaked form, it is easily destroyed.
Great Library entry.
In the 25th century, with the discovery of the Wormhole, scientists embarked on a new quest to understand and control the folded dimensions of space-time. From this came first the [Wormhole%20Probe%20%28CTP1%29](Wormhole Probe), then soon after, the Phantom.
