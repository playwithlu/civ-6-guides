# Windmill (Civ5)

Game Info.
Production-boosting building of the Renaissance Era. City must not be on a [Hill%20%28Civ5%29](hill).
Strategy.
The Windmill is a mid-level Renaissance-era building which increases the output of a city, especially when constructing buildings. It also allows the second Engineer Specialist slot after the [Workshop%20%28Civ5%29](Workshop). Cities constructed on [Hill%20%28Civ5%29](hills) may not build Windmills.
