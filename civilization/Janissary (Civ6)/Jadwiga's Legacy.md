# Janissary (Civ6)/Jadwiga's Legacy

The Janissary is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Melee](melee unit) of the [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarians) from the [Jadwiga%27s%20Legacy%20%28Civ6%29](Jadwiga's Legacy) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The Janissary is a deadly Ottoman unit that recovers [HP](HP) with each kill. When fighting Janissaries, keep severely wounded units away from them and soften them up with ranged units before sending in melee units to finish the job.
