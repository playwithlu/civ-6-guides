# Samori Touré (Civ6)

Samori Touré is a [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". His unit provides +5 Combat Strength and +1 to Modern and Atomic era land units within 2 s.
In standard games, Samori Touré can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) to instantly create an [Units%20%28Civ6%29](unit) with 1 level. In "", retiring Samori Toure instead creates a unit with a free promotion level.
