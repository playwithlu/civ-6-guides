# Stealth Destroyer (Civ4)

Strategy.
In "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)", the Stealth Destroyer is an improved version of the [Destroyer%20%28Civ4%29](Destroyer). It is invisible to most units and has a chance to intercept aircraft. However, the Stealth Destroyer cannot see [Submarine%20%28Civ4%29](Submarines).
