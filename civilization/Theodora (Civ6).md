# Theodora (Civ6)

Theodora (c. 500 – 28 June 548) was the wife of [Justinian%20I](emperor Justinian), ruling as empress from 527 until her death. As her husband's closest and most trusted advisor, she held considerable influence over Byzantium, and has been recognized as one of the most powerful women in Byzantine history. She leads the [Byzantine%20%28Civ6%29](Byzantines) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Empress Theodora brings the same devotion to and [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](Religion) as you needed to rise from your humble beginnings.
Intro.
 Empress Theodora, you come from nothing to rule the remnants of Rome! With your wit and beauty you captivated the people of Constantinople, and placed your hand upon the great wheel of power. In a time of strife and division, let all be guided by your counsel and set your shining seal upon the land.
In-Game.
Theodora's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is New Rome. She focuses on generating , likes [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) with a high output and dislikes those with low output.
Her [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Metanoia. [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Sites) grant equal to their [Adjacency%20bonus%20%28Civ6%29](adjacency bonus) and [Farm%20%28Civ6%29](Farms) provide a +1 adjacency bonus to [Hippodrome%20%28Civ6%29](Hippodromes) and Holy Sites.
Detailed Approach.
Theodora wants to beeline Holy Sites and [Astrology%20%28Civ6%29](Astrology). The creation of Holy Sites with Byzantium’s additional points will give her a Religion whose forces are dominant in [Theological%20combat%20%28Civ6%29](religious combats). The Metanoia ability will give her more for her pursuit of a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Religion](Religious Victory). The Hippodrome unique [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) will make sure your people are happy.
