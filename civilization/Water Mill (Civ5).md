# Water Mill (Civ5)

Game Info.
Bonus building of the [Ancient%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Ancient Era). [City%20%28Civ5%29](City) must be built next to a [Rivers%20%28Civ5%29](River).
Strategy.
The Water Mill can only be constructed in a city built next to a River. The Water Mill is an additional way to increase the city's and supplies early on, and as such, is quite valuable.
