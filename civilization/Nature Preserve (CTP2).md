# Nature Preserve (CTP2)

 is a [List%20of%20improvements%20in%20CTP2](tile improvement) in [Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II).
Gameplay.
Nature Preserves are lands intentionally set aside to protect endangered species and keep free from the detrimental effects of human civilization. They are popular tourist destinations, and increase the yield from a tile by 30.
