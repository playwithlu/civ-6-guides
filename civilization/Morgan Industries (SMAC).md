# Morgan Industries (SMAC)

Morgan Industries is a [Factions%20%28SMAC%29](faction) in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)". It is a faction led by CEO Nwabudike Morgan, and its members are often referred to as the Morganites.
Faction characteristics.
Although Morgan himself is not restricted from using the Green economics choice, an [AI](AI)-controlled Morgan will take offense at a player's choice of such.
