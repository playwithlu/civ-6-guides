# Marrakech

Marrakech (or Marrakesh) is the [Moroccan](Moroccan) [capital](capital) and an [Arabian](Arabian) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the fourth largest city in present-day Morocco.
