# Sistine Chapel (Civ4)

Strategy.
The Sistine Chapel causes each [Specialists%20%28Civ4%29](specialist) in every [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) to generate culture and increases the culture bonus provided by religious [List%20of%20buildings%20in%20Civ4](buildings) of the state [Religion%20%28Civ4%29](religion) {{Religion4}}. The Sistine Chapel has a very high cultural value, and it increases its city's odds of generating a [Great%20Artist%20%28Civ4%29](Great Artist).
This wonder is extremely useful in winning a [Victory%20%28Civ4%29%23Cultural](Cultural Victory), and increasing the difficulty of winning a Cultural Victory for others.
