# Ship of the Line (CTP1)

The Ship of the Line is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
The Ship of the Line dominates the oceans before the discovery of [Steel%20%28CTP1%29](Steel). In naval attack strength, it is unparalleled. Unleashing a broadside of death, the Ship of the Line can rip smaller ships apart. It can also carry up to three units to any coast on the [Map%20%28CTP1%29](globe).
Great Library entry.
Displaying a growing obsession with automatons and inspired by technologies like the mechanical clock, Europeans developed machine tools in the late 1700s. This led to the cannon boring lathe and Maudley's assembly line system for making ship blocks. Together, these two inventions greatly increased the power of ship's cannons and lowered ship prices.
