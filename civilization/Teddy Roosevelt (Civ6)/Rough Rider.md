In-Game.
Rough Rider Teddy's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Big Stick Policy. He likes civilizations that have a city on his home [Continent%20%28Civ6%29](continent) and remain peaceful, while disliking those that start wars in his home continent.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Roosevelt Corollary. His [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) receive additional Combat Strength when fighting on his home continent, and each sent to a [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) with which America has a will count as two s. He also gains the [Rough%20Rider%20%28Civ6%29](Rough Rider) [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique unit) after researching [Rifling%20%28Civ6%29](Rifling).
Detailed Approach.
Rough Rider Teddy Roosevelt enjoys +5 Combat Strength on his home continent and the ability to bring city-states under their sway through trade. He wants to expand rapidly across his continent while gaining the support of all nearby city-states. The Founding Fathers' civilization ability makes his choice of [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) important and can set him up for any [Victory%20%28Civ6%29](victory) type. His two post-[Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial) unique units, including his favored Rough Riders, make him able to hit especially hard from the 20th Century onwards.
