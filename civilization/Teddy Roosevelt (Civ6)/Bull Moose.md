In-Game.
Bull Moose Teddy's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is The Bull Moose. He wants to settle in high [Appeal%20%28Civ6%29](Appeal) areas, and focuses on maximizing Appeal in his land. He respects those with many high Appeal [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles), and dislikes those with low Appeal tiles.
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Antiquities and Parks. It grants additional yields for tiles with Breathtaking Appeal: when adjacent to a [Natural%20Wonder%20%28Civ6%29](Natural Wonder) or [Mountains%20%28Civ6%29](Mountain), and when adjacent to a [Wonder%20%28Civ6%29](Wonder) or [Woods%20%28Civ6%29](Woods). It also increases the Appeal of all tiles in cities with a National Park.
Detailed Approach.
Bull Moose Teddy favors the [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) choices of [Classical%20Republic%20%28Civ6%29](Classical Republic), [Merchant%20Republic%20%28Civ6%29](Merchant Republic) and then [Democracy%20%28Civ6%29](Democracy) for they give him the Economic and Wildcard [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](policy) slots he needs as he heads toward a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory). With his [Film%20Studio%20%28Civ6%29](Film Studio), National Park bonus, and the ability to earn and from Breathtaking tiles, Teddy is a cultural juggernaut, especially when possessing lands of great natural beauty.
