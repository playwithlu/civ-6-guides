# Pyramids of Egypt (CivWorld)

The are a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". They provide a 25% bonus to Production output. They are available after discovering [Ceremonial%20Burial%20%28CivWorld%29](Ceremonial Burial) and become [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Monarchy%20%28CivWorld%29](Monarchy).
