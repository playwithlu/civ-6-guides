# Popular Front (Civ6)

Popular Front is a [Golden%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Golden Age) [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Dramatic%20Ages%20%28Civ6%29](Dramatic Ages) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is available from the [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) to the [Future%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Future Era) and uses a Wildcard slot.
Strategy.
Although it isn't available until the last two [Era%20%28Civ6%29](eras) of the game, Popular Front is indispensable for players who are aiming for a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Diplomacy](Diplomatic Victory). [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](Leaders) whose [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader abilities) encourage them to form s or facilitate becoming [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerain) of [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states) (such as [Cleopatra%20%28Civ6%29](Cleopatra), [Gilgamesh%20%28Civ6%29](Gilgamesh), [Pericles%20%28Civ6%29](Pericles), [Poundmaker%20%28Civ6%29](Poundmaker), [Tamar%20%28Civ6%29](Tamar), and [Teddy%20Roosevelt%20%28Civ6%29/Rough%20Rider](Rough Rider Teddy)) will reap particularly great benefits from it, especially if they manage to build [Kilwa%20Kisiwani%20%28Civ6%29](Kilwa Kisiwani) and the [Orsz%C3%A1gh%C3%A1z%20%28Civ6%29](Országház) as well. [Mansa%20Musa%20%28Civ6%29](Mansa Musa), [Wilhelmina%20%28Civ6%29](Wilhelmina), the [Portuguese%20%28Civ6%29](Portuguese), and the [Polish%20%28Civ6%29](Poles) also benefit from Popular Front thanks to their unique abilities and [Sukiennice%20%28Civ6%29](Sukiennice), which enhance the yields of international s.
