# Torres del Paine (Civ6)

Torres del Paine is a two-tile impassable in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". Torres del Paine may be found on , , and tiles. Each Wonder tile provide +2 to adjacent tiles. 
Torres del Paine doubles the base terrain yields of all adjacent tiles, though it does not modify the additional yields from resources, or from terrain features such as forests. Tiles adjacent to multiple wonder tiles do not receive multiple instances of this bonus.
In "", discovering Torres del Paine grants +1 [Era%20Score%20%28Civ6%29](Era Score), or +3 Score if the player is the first to do so.
