# Unload (CTP1)

Unload allows you to move loaded units from a transport into a neighboring tile. However, only the , and can unload into enemy occupied tiles.
Great Library entry.
Historically, ships and other vehicles have been critical for their use as transports, vehicles in which to move military and civilian units. In ancient times warriors sailed to battle in oar-driven Triremes. Over time the vehicles used to transport military forces became more and more advanced and included sailing vessels, troop ships, cargo planes, undersea crawlers, and space planes.
