# Strategic Simulations, Inc.

Strategic Simulations, Inc. (called SSI for short) was a video game company founded by Joel Billings in 1979. Noted for its wargames, its official computer game adaptations of "Dungeons &amp; Dragons", and its "Panzer General" series, the company was acquired by Mindscape in 1994 and later became part of Mattel and Ubisoft.
