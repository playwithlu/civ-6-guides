# Silver (Civ5)

Game Info.
Luxury resource.
Strategy.
 Silver is a "classical" luxury resource, found in less productive [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29](terrain), and usually on [Hill%20%28Civ5%29](Hills). Accessed with a [Mine%20%28Civ5%29](Mine), Silver boosts and . Its main benefit, however, is that it allows construction of the [Mint%20%28Civ5%29](Mint), a [Building%20%28Civ5%29](bonus building) which grants an additional +2 on the tile! This benefit is awesome when there are multiple sources of Silver and/or in the [City%20%28Civ5%29](city's) [Territory%20%28Civ5%29](territory) - in this case, a single building boosts production greatly. 
 Silver is also one of the two resources which benefits from the Religious Idols belief, gaining additional and on the tile.
