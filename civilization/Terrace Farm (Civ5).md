# Terrace Farm (Civ5)

Game Info.
[List%20of%20improvements%20in%20Civ5%23Unique%20Improvements](Unique improvement) of the [Incan%20%28Civ5%29](Incan) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Requires [Construction%20%28Civ5%29](Construction).
Strategy.
The Terrace Farm allows the Inca to take maximum advantage of elevated [Terrain%20%28Civ5%29](terrain) in their [Territory%20%28Civ5%29](territory). It can be built without access to fresh water and produces additional if located next to [Mountain%20%28Civ5%29](mountain) tiles. Settle as many [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) as possible near mountain ranges, then fill the surrounding [Hill%20%28Civ5%29](hills) with Terrace Farms to increase your empire's rapidly.
