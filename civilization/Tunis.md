# Tunis

Tunis is an [Arabian](Arabian) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is the [capital](capital) of and largest city in present-day Tunisia.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Fatimid](Fatimid) city in [Middle%20Ages](Middle Ages).
