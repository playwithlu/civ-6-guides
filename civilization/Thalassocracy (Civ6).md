# Thalassocracy (Civ6)

Thalassocracy is a form of [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) from the [Path%20to%20Nirvana%20%28Civ6%29](Path to Nirvana) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". Because of the special rules in the scenario, its Military [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) slots are actually Religious Policy Card slots.
