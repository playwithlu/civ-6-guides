# Stock Exchange (Civ5)

Game Info.
Advanced money-boosting building. Requires [Bank%20%28Civ5%29](Bank), [Satrap%27s%20Court%20%28Civ5%29](Satrap's Court), or [Hanse%20%28Civ5%29](Hanse).
Strategy.
The Stock Exchange is the top -boosting building. Build it for a final kick in the [City%20%28Civ5%29](city's) output of , using its 2 [Specialists%20%28Civ5%29](Specialist) slots to further increase the base production. The city must have built a Bank (or one of its replacements) before the Stock Exchange can be constructed.
