# Sydney Opera House (Civ5)

Game Info.
Must be constructed in a [Coastal%20%28Civ5%29](coastal) [City%20%28Civ5%29](city).
Strategy.
The Sydney Opera House is, along with [Broadway%20%28Civ5%29](Broadway), one of only two wonders which provide [Theming%20bonus%20%28Civ5%29](theming bonuses) for [Great%20Work%20%28Civ5%29](Great Works of Music). It is therefore quite useful to players pursuing a [Cultural%20victory%20%28Civ5%29](cultural victory), though the free [Social%20policies%20%28Civ5%29](social policy) and +50% in the city will be of great benefit to players aiming at other [Victory%20%28Civ5%29](victory) conditions as well.
