# Ornamental Carving (Civ5)

Ornamental Carving is a special technology found only in the [Paradise%20Found%20%28Civ5%29](Paradise Found) scenario of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Ornamental Carving allows your [Worker%20%28Civ5%29](Workers) to construct [Moai%20%28Civ5%29](Moai). Research it as early as possible to jump start your culture generation.
