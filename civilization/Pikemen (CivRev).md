# Pikemen (CivRev)

Pikemen are a [Ground%20unit%20%28CivRev%29](ground unit) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Unit Analysis.
Pikemen are the defensive units for the [Medieval%20Era%20%28CivRev%29](Medieval Era). They are best used in an [Army%20%28CivRev%29](Army) and with upgrades like [Engineer%20%28CivRev%29](Engineer) and [Loyalty%20%28CivRev%29](Loyalty). They are highly useful against [Knight%20%28CivRev%29](Knights) and [Catapult%20%28CivRev%29](Catapults) and are very good at defending [City%20%28CivRev%29](cities).
Unique Replacements.
[Greek%20%28CivRev%29](Greek) Pikemen are replaced by the Hoplite. It is available from the very beginning of the game, thanks to the Greeks' free knowledge of [Democracy%20%28technology%29%20%28CivRev%29](Democracy).
[Japanese%20%28CivRev%29](Japanese) Pikemen are replaced by the Ashigaru Pikemen.
