# Stock Exchange (Civ4)

The Stock Exchange is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [English%20%28Civ4%29](English) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Bank%20%28Civ4%29](Bank) which it replaces, the Stock Exchange gives an extra +15% to the [City%20%28Civ4%29](city) it is built in.
