# Melody (Civ6)

Melody is the starting title for the [Voidsingers%20%28Civ6%29](Voidsingers), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked by discovering a [Tribal%20Village%20%28Civ6%29](Tribal Village).
Effects.
Allows you to construct the [Old%20God%20Obelisk%20%28Civ6%29](Old God Obelisk) building, a powerful replacement of the [Monument%20%28Civ6%29](Monument).
