# Barbarian Horseman (Civ6)

The Barbarian Horseman is a [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique) [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Light%20cavalry](light cavalry unit) of the [Barbarian%20%28Civ6%29](Barbarians) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Strategy.
The Barbarian Horseman is weaker and slower than the standard [Horseman%20%28Civ6%29](Horseman) - for the sake of comparison, it has the same Combat Strength as a [Warrior%20%28Civ6%29](Warrior) - and spawns only from outposts near . Nonetheless, its mobility makes it troublesome in the early game, during which it can easily overtake and capture unescorted [Builder%20%28Civ6%29](Builders) and [Settler%20%28Civ6%29](Settlers). Players near an outpost that can spawn Barbarian Horsemen should use [Archer%20%28Civ6%29](Archers) or [Spearman%20%28Civ6%29](Spearmen) to protect any [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Civilian](civilian units) that leave their [Territory%20%28Civ6%29](territory).
