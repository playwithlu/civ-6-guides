Strategy.
Vanilla &amp; Warlords.
Scotland Yard is required to train [Spy%20%28Civ4%29](Spies), and adds 1 [Great%20Scientist%20%28Civ4%29](Great Scientist) point per turn.
Beyond the Sword.
Scotland Yard can be built by a [Great%20Spy%20%28Civ4%29](Great Spy), and provides a +100% bonus to Espionage output.
