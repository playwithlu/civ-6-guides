# Ocean (Civ1)

Oceans produce small amounts of [Food%20%28Civ1%29](food) and no [Production%20%28Civ1%29](production), but substantial [Trade%20%28Civ1%29](trade). Only or can enter Oceans. Landlocked Oceans are really lakes but are treated like other Oceans in all respects.
Oceans produce 1 food and 2 trade. [Fish%20%28Civ1%29](Fish) can be found in oceans which generate an extra 2 food.
With railroad (place a [Settlers%20%28Civ1%29](settler) on a ship in order to accomplish) the oceans produce one extra trade. An extra unit of trade can be produced by switching to a [Democracy%20%28government%29%20%28Civ1%29](democracy) or [Republic%20%28Civ1%29](republic).
It costs 1 Movement Point to travel over an ocean region, but only ships and aircraft can do so.
