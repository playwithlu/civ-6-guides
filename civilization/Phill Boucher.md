# Phill Boucher

Phill Boucher is an American composer and sound designer. He is known for helping to compose the [soundtrack](soundtrack) for "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
In addition to his work on the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, Boucher has composed music for "Fortnite", "Masterminds", "Uncharted 4: A Thief's End", and "Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales".
