# Uranium (C-evo)

Uranium is a [Modern%20resource%20%28C-evo%29](Modern resource) in "[C-evo](C-evo)". It appears as a glowing silver rod and is used in the [Power%20Module%20%28C-evo%29](Power Module), which requires [Impulse%20Drive%20%28C-evo%29](Impulse Drive) and costs 600 resources; four are needed, for a total cost of 2400 resources.
