# Sweyn II (Civ5)

Sweyn II is a playable [Leaders%20%28Civ5%29](leader) from the [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) in "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)". A reused [Harald%20Bluetooth%20%28Civ5%29](Harald Bluetooth), he leads the [Danish%20%28Civ5%29](Danes).
Unique Ability.
Viking Fury - Embarked units have +1 and pay just 1 point to move from sea to land. Melee units pay no point cost to pillage.
