# Survivalism (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a [Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29/Technology](technology) in the "[Final%20Frontier%20%28Civ4%29](Final Frontier)" [mod](mod) for "".
Strategy.
Survivalism allows the construction of the Alpha Fighter Squadron Unit, vital to kill enemy Bomber Squadrons or to protect one's own, and the culture-enhancing Planetary Beacon Building. The first to research the tech also founds the "Survival" Value, which can provide additional to Star Systems.
