# Magyar (Civ3)

The {{CleanPageName}} people (or Hungarians) represent the Kingdom of Hungary, a non-playable [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) from {{Link3|The Middle Ages}} [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ3](scenario) in "". They are led by {{Link3|Stephen}}.
Description.
The Magyars are a non-playable civilization in the Middle Ages. They start the game with all twelve [Advance%20%28Civ3%29](technologies) from {{Link3|Ancient Times}}. They prefer to research from the Roman Catholic optional path of the tech tree.
