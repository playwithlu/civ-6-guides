# Robber Barons (Civ6)

Robber Barons is a [Dark%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Dark Age) [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "". It is available from the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era) to the [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) and uses a Wildcard slot.
Strategy.
Robber Barons will make your empire wealthier and more productive if you have the right kinds of [Building%20%28Civ6%29](buildings) in your [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities). However, the loss of means that they'll grow more slowly and be at risk of losing [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](Loyalty), so plan for that if you adopt this policy.
