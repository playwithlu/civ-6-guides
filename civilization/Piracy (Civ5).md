# Piracy (Civ5)

Piracy is a special technology in the [Conquest%20of%20the%20New%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Conquest of the New World) [Scenario%20%28Civ5%29](scenario) of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Allows you to build the [Privateer%20%28Civ5%29](Privateer) and allows European powers to declare war on each other.
