A Temple is requisite for anyone who is serious about developing their religion. Not only does it allow the purchase of more advanced [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Religious](religious units), but it is also necessary to build the special worship buildings in this Holy Site, which boost religious endeavors considerably.
