# Nilometer (Civ5)

Game Info.
Unique building of the [Egyptian%20%28Civ5%29](Egyptian civilization) in the [Wonders%20of%20the%20Ancient%20World%20%28Civ5%29](Wonders of the Ancient World) [List%20of%20Civilization%20V%20scenarios](scenario). Replaces the [Water%20Mill%20%28Civ5%29](Water Mill). Can only be built in a city next to a [Rivers%20%28Civ5%29](river).
Strategy.
The Nilometer is the Egyptian unique building, replacing the [Water%20Mill%20%28Civ5%29](Water Mill). It increases the city's food and gold output, and should be built if the city is near a river.
