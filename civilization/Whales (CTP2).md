Whales are a common [Goods%20%28CTP2%29](good) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". They are found in [Deep%20Ocean%20%28CTP2%29](Deep Oceans), [Continental%20Shelf%20%28CTP2%29](Continental Shelves), and [Suboceanic%20Ridge%20%28CTP2%29](Suboceanic Ridges).
Gameplay.
