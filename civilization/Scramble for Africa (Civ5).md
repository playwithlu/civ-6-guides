# Scramble for Africa (Civ5)

Scramble for Africa is a "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ5](scenario) included with the "" [expansion%20pack](expansion pack). It is based on the actual Scramble for Africa which took place from 1880 to 1910, a period where multiple [Europe](Europe)an powers, including Britain, France, Portugal, Germany, Italy, Belgium, and Spain, colonized all of the [Africa](Africa)n continent, except for two countries, Ethiopia and Liberia.
Scenario Description.
Europeans had claimed most of the native lands elsewhere, but not the dark continent of Africa. Driven by a sense of adventure, a desire for wealth, and the thirst for knowledge, explorers and settlers scrambled into Africa from north and west. There they encountered lost kingdoms, exotic beasts and native empires that challenged their dreams of colonization. Can you claim the riches of Africa as a European? Or will you try and repulse the interlopers?
Gameplay.
The winner of the scenario is the [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) with the most victory points after 100 turns. In addition to normal scoring methods, European civilizations receive victory points for taking artifacts, for being the first to discover [Natural%20wonder%20%28Civ5%29](natural wonders), and for owning the longest [Railroad%20%28improvement%29%20%28Civ5%29](railroad) between two [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities); North African civilizations score victory points based on their income; and Sub-Saharan civilizations earn points for generating . Points are also awarded to the owner of the [Suez%20Canal%20%28Civ5%29](Suez Canal), and points for land are doubled.

Social policies.
There are three policy trees in this scenario. The European tree is available to [Belgian%20%28Civ5%29](Belgium), [English%20%28Civ5%29](England), [French%20%28Civ5%29](France), [German%20%28Civ5%29](Germany), [Italian%20%28Civ5%29](Italy), and [Portuguese%20%28Civ5%29](Portugal). The North African tree is available to [Egyptian%20%28Civ5%29](Egypt), [Moroccan%20%28Civ5%29](Morocco), and the [Ottoman%20%28Civ5%29](Ottomans). The Sub-Saharan branch is available to the [Boer%20%28Civ5%29](Boers), [Ethiopian%20%28Civ5%29](Ethiopia), and the [Zulu%20%28Civ5%29](Zulus).
European.
Combat units may only heal if inside friendly territory. Land combat units (other than Explorers) have -1 Visibility Range. May NOT build the [Writers%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Writers'), [Artists%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Artists'), or [Musicians%27%20Guild%20%28Civ5%29](Musicians' Guild).
North African.
Earn for each unit you kill. May NOT build the Writers', Artists', or Musicians' Guild.
Sub-Saharan.
Earn for each unit you kill except barbarians. Allows cities to build the Writers', Artists', and Musicians' Guilds.
