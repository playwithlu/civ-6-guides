Summary.
For large empires, Virtual Democracy inspires the greatest progress in science. Virtual Democracy uses nanotechnology to feed and clothe, distributes opportunity to all, and governs through virtual representation. And with the Egalitarian Act, the most virtuous of all Civilizations can increase happiness and recruit foreign revolting cities into their humane empire.
Great Library entry.
Following economic crisis brought about by new nanotechnologies and moral crisis brought about by centuries of depravity, intellectuals grabbed the reigns of virtual power and fought a successful struggle to create the Virtual Democracy. By giving a vote to each citizen and truly eliminating discrimination and corruption, the Virtual Democracy was a key to the healing of humanity.
