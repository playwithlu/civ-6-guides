# Rocketry (Civ4)

Strategy.
Rocketry allows you to build [SAM%20Infantry%20%28Civ4%29](SAM Infantry), [Guided%20Missile%20%28Civ4%29](Guided Missiles) and [Attack%20Submarine%20%28Civ4%29](Attack Submarines) (with [Radio%20%28Civ4%29](Radio)), and [ICBM%20%28Civ4%29](ICBMs) and [Tactical%20Nuke%20%28Civ4%29](Tactical Nukes) (with [Fission%20%28Civ4%29](Fission)). It enables 2 wonders: the [Aluminum%20Co.%20%28Civ4%29](Aluminum Co.) corporation, and the [Apollo%20Program%20%28Civ4%29](Apollo Program). and project.
