# Mobilization (Civ6)

Mobilization is a [Modern%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Modern Era) [Civic%20%28Civ6%29](civic) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It can be hurried by owning 3 [Corps%20%28Civ6%29](Corps) in your military.
Strategy.
Mobilization unlocks the most advanced [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](unit) combinations in the game: [Unit%20%28Civ6%29%23Combining%20units](Armies and Armadas). It also unlocks the abilities to form a [Diplomacy%20%28Civ6%29%23Defensive%20Pact](Defensive Pact) (a new diplomatic action) and declare a War of Territorial Expansion (a new [Casus%20Belli%20%28Civ6%29](Casus Belli)).
