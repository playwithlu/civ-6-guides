# Truffles (Civ6)

 Truffles are a [Resource%20%28Civ6%29%23Luxury](luxury resource) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". They are found in [Woods%20%28Civ6%29](Woods), [Rainforest%20%28Civ6%29](Rainforests), and [Marsh%20%28Civ6%29](Marshes), regardless of the underlying [Terrain%20%28Civ6%29](terrain) type.
Strategy.
 Truffles are a boon to an empire's economy, providing +5 and +1 when improved after the discovery of [Synthetic%20Materials%20%28Civ6%29](Synthetic Materials) and [Mercantilism%20%28Civ6%29](Mercantilism). Look for them near cities founded in wooded areas or marshland.
