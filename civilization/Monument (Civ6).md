# Monument (Civ6)

The Monument is a basic [Culture%20%28Civ6%29](cultural) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is built in the [City%20Center%20%28Civ6%29](City Center) and does not require any [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](technological) research.
Strategy.
Since it is available from the very beginning of the game, the Monument will likely be the first building you construct in your . After sending a [Scout%20%28Civ6%29](Scout) or two to explore the land, build a Monument to jump-start your empire's generation and begin working your way through the [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ6](civics tree).
