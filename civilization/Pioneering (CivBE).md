# Pioneering (CivBE)

Game Information.
Pioneering is a Tier 0 [Secondary%20Tech%20%28CivBE%29](Secondary Tech) of the [Tech%20Web%20%28CivBE%29](Tech Web) that can be researched from [Habitation%20%28CivBE%29](Habitation); you can also start with it for free if you include a Laboratory in your Cargo when .
Pioneering extends habitational techniques to allow new extensions of your central colony, and establish connections with them (or with other colonies). This includes the development of a new self-unpacking unit called [Colonist%20%28CivBE%29](Colonists), containing material sufficient for creation of a basic new set of habitational and administrative structures; a [Trade%20Depot%20%28CivBE%29](Trade Depot) Building to provide logistical support for long-distance goods exchange, and finally the [Trade%20Convoy%20%28CivBE%29](Trade Convoy) and [Trade%20Vessel%20%28CivBE%29](Trade vessel) units used to conduct actual trade.
There is no affinity linked to this technology.
