# Nobel Prize Competitions (Civ6)

Nobel Prize Competitions are [Competition%20%28Civ6%29](scored competitions) available when [Swedish%20%28Civ6%29](Sweden) is present in the game.
Initiation Conditions.
Starting in the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era), one of the 3 Nobel Prize competitions is randomly selected in regular [World%20Congress%20%28Civ6%29](World Congress) sessions as a proposal. If passed, the selected competition begins when the World Congress session ends and expires in 30 turns.
Nobel Peace Prize.
Scoring Criteria.
1 point for each point of gained during the competition, including those gained from liberating [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-states) and [Carbon%20Recapture%20%28Civ6%29](Carbon Recapture) city projects.
