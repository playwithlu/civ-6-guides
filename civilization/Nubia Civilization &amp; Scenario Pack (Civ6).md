# Nubia Civilization &amp; Scenario Pack (Civ6)

The Nubia Civilization &amp; Scenario Pack is [DLC%20%28Civ6%29](DLC) for "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" released on July 27, 2017. It is now available to purchase on various digital game distributors including [Steam](Steam).
