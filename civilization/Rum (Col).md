# Rum (Col)

Rum is a manufactured [Goods](good) produced from [Sugar%20%28Col%29](Sugar) in a [Rum%20Distiller%27s%20House%20%28Col%29](Rum Distiller's House), [Rum%20Distillery%20%28Col%29](Rum Distillery) or [Rum%20Factory%20%28Col%29](Rum Factory).
