# Tower of Babel (CivRev)

The Tower of Babel is an [Artifacts%20%28CivRev%29](artifact) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)", available for download with the Mythic Wonder &amp; Artifact Pack.
A player who finds the Tower of Babel is instantly put in contact with all of the other [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CivRev](civilizations) in the game.
