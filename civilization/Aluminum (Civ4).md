# Aluminum (Civ4)

Strategy.
Aluminum provides production and commerce to the city that works it. It is required for the production of the [Modern%20Armor%20%28Civ4%29](Modern Armor), [Jet%20Fighter%20%28Civ4%29](Jet Fighter), and [Stealth%20Bomber%20%28Civ4%29](Stealth Bomber). It also doubles production speed for the [Space%20Elevator%20%28Civ4%29](Space Elevator), the [Apollo%20Program%20%28Civ4%29](Apollo Program), the [SS%20Casing%20%28Civ4%29](SS Casing), the [SS%20Docking%20Bay%20%28Civ4%29](SS Docking Bay), the [SS%20Thrusters%20%28Civ4%29](SS Thrusters), and the [SDI%20%28Civ4%29](SDI).
Civilizations with cities where the [Aluminum%20Co.%20%28Civ4%29](Aluminum Co.) [Corporation%20%28concept%29%20%28Civ4%29](corporation) is present automatically receive one of this resource.
