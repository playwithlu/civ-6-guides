# Caravansary (Civ5)

Game Info.
Basic trading building. 
Strategy.
The Caravansary is a building added in "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)" that enhances the effectiveness of [Caravan%20%28Civ5%29](land trade routes). Try to build it in important city hubs and then initiate your International Trade routes from there for maximum efficiency and range.
