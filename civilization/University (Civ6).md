# University (Civ6)

The University is an advanced [Science%20%28Civ6%29](scientific) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is built in the [Campus%20%28Civ6%29](Campus) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) and requires a [Library%20%28Civ6%29](Library) (or one of its replacements).
Strategy.
The University is a mid-level building that boosts a city's output.
The can be used to instantly build a Library and a University in a Campus and to give an extra +2 to all Universities.
Building two Universities triggers the for [Printing%20%28Civ6%29](Printing), and building one next to a [Mountains%20%28Civ6%29](Mountain) triggers the for [Astronomy%20%28Civ6%29](Astronomy).
