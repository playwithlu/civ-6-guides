# Richmond

Richmond is an [American](American), [British](British), and [English](English) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and elsewhere and a [New%20English](New English) city in the "[Colonization%20%28game%29](Colonization)" games. In real life, it is the capital of the U.S. state of Virginia.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as the [Confederate](Confederate) [capital](capital) in [American%20Civil%20War](American Civil War).
