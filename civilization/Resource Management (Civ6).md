# Resource Management (Civ6)

Resource Management is an Economic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". In "", it becomes a Military Policy Card.
Strategy.
In [vanilla](vanilla) "Civilization VI" and "", Resource Management isn't all that useful most of the time - by the time it becomes available, you should have plenty of [Encampment%20%28Civ6%29](Encampments) to train [Units%20%28Civ6%29](units) without a second copy of [Resources%20%28Civ6%29%23Strategic%20Resources](strategic resources), and it does no good unless you have at least one copy of a resource anyway.
In "Gathering Storm", this policy can be helpful if you're pursuing a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science) or [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination Victory): it will aid you with the former by providing you with for [Lagrange%20Laser%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](Lagrange Laser Stations), and the latter by providing you with the resources needed to upgrade your army, navy, and air force.
