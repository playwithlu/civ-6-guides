# Polotsk

Polotsk, also known as Polatsk, is a [Kievan%20Rus%27](Kievan Rus') [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). To date, it has appeared only in [scenario](scenario)s. In real life, it is a town in Vitebsk Region in present-day Belarus.
