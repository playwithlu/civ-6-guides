Intro.
 All Spain stands ready to answer your prayers, devout King Philip II. From your palace at El Escorial you can send forth your followers: missionaries, apostles, and conquistadors ready to strike out across the seas to spread the good word about your most holy empire. May the treasures they uncover fuel your quest to unite the world under one faith and one empire, making you truly the Most Catholic king.
In-Game.
Philip II's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Counter Reformer. He likes civilizations which follow the same [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](religion) he does and wants all his [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities) to follow the same religion. He hates anyone trying to spread their religion into his empire.
Philip's [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is El Escorial. His [Inquisitor%20%28Civ6%29](Inquisitors) can Remove Heresy one extra time, and his [Military%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](military) and [List%20of%20units%20in%20Civ6/By%20class%23Religious](religious units) receive +5 Combat Strength (or Religious Strength) against [Unit%20%28Civ6%29](units) owned by players who follow other religions. In "", his Inquisitors also remove 100% of the presence of other religions.
Detailed Approach.
Philip wants everyone to adopt his beloved [Catholicism%20%28Civ6%29](Catholicism). He wants to establish a small empire on the coast of his home [Continent%20%28Civ6%29](continent) and then go sailing to find additional settlement spots. If he can't find places on other continents to settle peacefully, he will use his [Conquistador%20%28Civ6%29](Conquistadors) to force a foothold. Once there, the [Mission%20%28Civ6%29](Missions) and Treasure Fleet civilization ability will ensure that the foreign presence will be lucrative for him. He's going to push against anyone that isn't following his Religion until he has turned the entire world to his Beliefs.
