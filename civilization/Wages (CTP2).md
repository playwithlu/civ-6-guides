# Wages (CTP2)

 is a game concept in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)".
Gameplay.
Wages are the rate at which workers in are paid. They are deducted from the total produced every turn. If Wages exceed the produced per turn, the balance will be deducted from the empire's treasury. If the treasury is depleted, the Wage rate will automatically decrease to a manageable level.
Keep an eye on Wages - they directly affect the of citizens.
