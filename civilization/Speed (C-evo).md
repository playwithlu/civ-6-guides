# Speed (C-evo)

Each [unit%20%28C-evo%29](unit) in the free-to-play game "[C-evo](C-evo)" has a maximum "speed", the distance it can normally travel in one turn. 
The minimum for a [ground%20unit%20%28C-evo%29](ground unit) is 1.5 (i.e., one diagonal step - N, S, E, or W on an [Isometric%20view](isometric) [Map%20%28C-evo%29](map) - that's not using a road or river). Various additions can be made once you have researched [Horseback%20Riding%20%28C-evo%29](Horseback Riding).
[Naval%20unit%20%28C-evo%29](Naval unit)s start at 2.5 for the [predesigned%20unit%20%28C-evo%29](predesigned unit)s ([Longboats%20%28C-evo%29](Longboats)). Minimum is 3.5 for units you design. That can be increased by various factors as you do more research.
[Air%20unit%20%28C-evo%29](Air unit)s start at 8.5 but any designed after you know [Rocketry%20%28C-evo%29](Rocketry) are 12.5. You cannot enhance those.
