# Strategic Air Force (Civ6)

Strategic Air Force is a Military [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". 
Strategy.
Strategic Air Force provides increased toward air units and [Aircraft%20Carrier%20%28Civ6%29](Aircraft Carriers). It's useful if you need to build an air force to support your army and navy, or if you just need some [Bomber%20%28Civ6%29](Bombers) or [Jet%20Bomber%20%28Civ6%29](Jet Bombers) to deliver [Nuclear%20weapons%20%28Civ6%29](nuclear weapons).
Bugs.
In the "" ruleset, there is a bug causing this card not to give bonus towards pre-[Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) air units.
