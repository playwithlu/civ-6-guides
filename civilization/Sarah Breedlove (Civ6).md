# Sarah Breedlove (Civ6)

Sarah Breedlove is a [Great%20Merchant%20%28Civ6%29](Great Merchant) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)".
Unique ability.
+25% rate towards other civilizations you have a to.
Strategy.
Players working towards a [Cultural%20Victory%20%28Civ6%29](Cultural Victory) should aim for Breedlove. Otherwise, skip her and save the Great Merchant Points for the next recruit.
