# Panzer (Civ4)

The Panzer is the [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](unique unit) of the [German%20%28Civ4%29](German) [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](civilization).
Strategy.
Compared to the [Tank%20%28Civ4%29](Tank) that it replaces, the Panzer has a 50% bonus against other [Armored%20unit%20%28Civ4%29](armored units).
