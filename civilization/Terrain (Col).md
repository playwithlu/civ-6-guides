# Terrain (Col)

There are 21 different terrain types in [Sid%20Meier%27s%20Colonization](Sid Meier's Colonization). The table below gives base yields; these can be increased by specialists working the terrain, roads, rivers and bonus resources.
[Food](Food) on land tiles is harvested as [Grain](Grain); on water tiles, as [Fish](Fish). All forest tiles may be cleared by a [Pioneers%20%28Col%29](Pioneer) (using the plow action) to turn them into a treeless terrain type:
The change is permanent and any forest bonus will be deleted. As a side effect, clearance produces some [lumber%20%28Col%29](lumber) in the nearest [colony%20%28Col%29](colony).
