# Physics (Civ5)

Game Info.
A practical application of mathematics to the surrounding world, Physics allows creation of much more complex structures and machines. Its first benefit is the creation of the [Trebuchet%20%28Civ5%29](Trebuchet), the Medieval siege weapon.
Physics also unlocks the [Notre%20Dame%20%28Civ5%29](Notre Dame) World Wonder in "[Gods%20%26amp%3B%20Kings](Gods &amp; Kings)" and "[Brave%20New%20World](Brave New World)".
