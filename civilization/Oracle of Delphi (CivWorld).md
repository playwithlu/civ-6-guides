# Oracle of Delphi (CivWorld)

The is a [Wonder%20%28CivWorld%29%23List%20of%20Great%20Wonders](Great Wonder) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It provides a 25% bonus to Culture output. It is available after discovering [Alphabet%20%28CivWorld%29](Alphabet) and becomes [obsolete](obsolete) after discovering [Religion%20%28CivWorld%29](Religion).
