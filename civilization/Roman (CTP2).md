# Roman (CTP2)

The Roman people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)". Their default leader names are [Julius%20Caesar](Julius Caesar) for a male and [Livia%20Drusilla](Livia Drusilla) for a female.
History.
The Romans are part of the [Classical%20era%20%28CTP2%29](Classical era) (600 BC to 500 AD). The Roman Republic became independent from the [Etruscan%20%28CTP2%29](Etruscans) in 509 BC and continued on for many centuries. Depending on one's definition, this civilization ended in either 476 AD with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, or in 1453 AD with the fall of [Constantinople](Constantinople) (present-day Istanbul) to the [Turkish%20%28CTP2%29](Turks).
