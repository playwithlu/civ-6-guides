# Tupac Amaru (Civ6)

Tupac Amaru is a [Great%20General%20%28Civ6%29](Great General) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". He was added in the [Babylon%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Babylon Pack).
Tupac Amaru can be [Great%20People%20%28Civ6%29](retired) in enemy territory to receive a [Musketman%20%28Civ6%29](Musketman) in each of the target city's indefensible and undefended districts.
