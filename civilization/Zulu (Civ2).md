# Zulu (Civ2)

The Zulu people represent the Zulu Kingdom, a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ2](default civilization) in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)".
Color.
The Zulus are a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ2](green civilization). A game involving the Zulus will not include the {{Link2|Babylonian}}s or {{Link2|Japanese}}.
Leaders.
The default male leader of the Zulus is [Shaka](Shaka) (c. 1787–1828). An illegitimate son of the Zulu chief, he rose to power by assassinating at the behest of his overlord . Reforming Zulu culture and fighting methods, he turned his tribe into a militaristic empire. The default female leader is [Shakala](Shakala), an ahistorical figure who is apparently a feminization of Shaka.
