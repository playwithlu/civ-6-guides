# Oxford University (Civ6)

Oxford University is an in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It must be built on s or adjacent to a with a .
Strategy.
Oxford University is immensely valuable to any player who wants to win a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Science](Science Victory), providing two free [Technology%20%28Civ6%29](techs) and an impressive bonus to its [City%20%28Civ6%29](city's) output in addition to the points. However, its two slots for Great Works of Writing are also valuable to -oriented players, who may want to build Oxford University just to deny it to their rivals and stop them from pulling too far ahead in the technological race.
Before completing Oxford University, it is advisable to complete the leaf technologies from the previous [Era%20%28Civ6%29](eras), such as [Astrology%20%28Civ6%29](Astrology) or [Celestial%20Navigation%20%28Civ6%29](Celestial Navigation), or else these may be the free technologies rewarded.
