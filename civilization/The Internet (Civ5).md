# The Internet (Civ5)

Game Info.
A network connecting computers worldwide is the next step in communications technology, and the [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization) which develops The Internet immediately receives a powerful medium to influence other civilizations.
The Internet automatically doubles the output of all your [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities), helping you greatly to advance toward a [Victory%20%28Civ5%29%23Cultural%20victory%20in%20Brave%20New%20World](Cultural Victory). This doubled does not apply to the civilization which has built the [Great%20Firewall%20%28Civ5%29](Great Firewall).
Strangely, [Computers%20%28Civ5%29](Computers) is not a prerequisite for this [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20Civ5](tech), making it possible to invent The Internet before discovering Computers.
