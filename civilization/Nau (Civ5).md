# Nau (Civ5)

Game Info.
Unique unit of the [Portuguese%20%28Civ5%29](Portuguese) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Caravel%20%28Civ5%29](Caravel).
Strategy.
The Nau is a naval melee unit that replaces the Caravel and is skilled at exploration. When it is in a tile next to land owned by another civilization or [City-state%20%28Civ5%29](City-State) you are at peace with, it may perform a one-time trade mission (Sell Exotic Goods) to earn and [XP](XP). The farther the land is from your own , the greater the bonus, so it is best to use on distant players. Use this unit to explore the [Map%20%28Civ5%29](map) and uncover the locations of other players.
