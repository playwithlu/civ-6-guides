# Stonehenge (Civ4)

Strategy.
Stonehenge provides a free [Monument%20%28Civ4%29](Monument) in all your [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities) and centers the [Map%20%28Civ4%29](world map). It increases its city's odds of generating a [Great%20Prophet%20%28Civ4%29](Great Prophet).
[List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ4](Civilizations) with [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique buildings) replacing the Monument will find it highly advantageous to build this wonder. The [Native%20American%20%28Civ4%29](Native Americans), for example, can use the experience bonus [Totem%20Pole%20%28Civ4%29](Totem Poles) bring for a stronger defense in case of an attack.
