# Viking

The Viking people represent a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games and "[C-evo](C-evo)". Their [capital](capital) is [Trondheim](Trondheim), and they can be led by [Canute](Canute), [Gunnhild](Gunnhild), [Ragnar%20Lodbrok](Ragnar Lodbrok), [Sweyn%20Forkbeard](Sweyn Forkbeard), or [Thyra%20Danebod%20%28CTP2%29](Thyra Danebod).
The [Danish](Danish), [Norwegian](Norwegian), and [Swedish](Swedish) people may also be represented by the Vikings.
