# Victory (Civ1)

Victory in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Civilization](Sid Meier's Civilization)" can be achieved in three different ways.
Space Race.
Build a [Spaceship%20%28Civ1%29](Spaceship) and reach [Alpha%20Centauri%20%28star%20system%29](Alpha Centauri) first.
Conquest.
Defeat all rival [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ1](civilizations) by capturing all of their [City%20%28Civ1%29](cities).
Time.
Survive until time runs out.
