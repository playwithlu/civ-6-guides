# War Department (Civ6)

The War Department is a Tier 3 [Government%20%28Civ6%29](government) [Buildings%20%28Civ6%29](building) in "". It is built in the [Government%20Plaza%20%28Civ6%29](Government Plaza) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) and requires a Tier 3 government ([Communism%20%28Civ6%29](Communism), [Democracy%20%28Civ6%29](Democracy), or [Fascism%20%28Civ6%29](Fascism)) and a Tier 2 government building ([Foreign%20Ministry%20%28Civ6%29](Foreign Ministry), [Grand%20Master%27s%20Chapel%20%28Civ6%29](Grand Master's Chapel), [Intelligence%20Agency%20%28Civ6%29](Intelligence Agency), or [Queen%27s%20Bibliotheque%20%28Civ6%29](Queen's Bibliotheque)).
Strategy.
You'll want to build the War Department if you're trying to win a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination Victory). It provides free healing to your units with each kill, helping your army and navy stay fighting fit. It can also be useful for finishing a Religious Victory, as the heal applies to religious units as well.
