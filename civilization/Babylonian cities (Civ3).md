# Babylonian cities (Civ3)

 are [City%20%28Civ3%29](cities) that can be founded by the [Babylonian%20%28Civ3%29](Babylonian) [Civilizations%20%28Civ3%29](civilization) in "[Civilization%20III](Civilization III)" and its expansions. The first city founded is the [Capital%20%28Civ3%29](capital), and the names of future cities are chosen in the order they appear on the list below.
The names listed are default names; players can change their cities' names if they choose.
