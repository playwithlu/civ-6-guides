# Village Green (CivWorld)

The Village Green is a [Building%20%28CivWorld%29](building) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)".
Strategy.
Village Greens are drop off points for all [Resource%20%28CivWorld%29](resources). Placing it near a [House%20%28CivWorld%29](House) will increase the productivity of the nearby buildings. It costs 500 production to build and upgrade.
