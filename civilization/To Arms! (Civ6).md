# To Arms! (Civ6)

To Arms! is a [Golden%20Age%20%28Civ6%29](Golden Age) [Policy%20card%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) from the [Dramatic%20Ages%20%28Civ6%29](Dramatic Ages) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is available from the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era) to the [Information%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Information Era) ([Atomic%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Atomic Era) in "") and uses a Wildcard slot.
Strategy.
To Arms! is perhaps the most straightforward Golden Age Policy Card in the game: the 50% [XP](XP) bonus and 15% bonus when training [Military%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](military units) make it an obvious choice for militaristic players. Any player who's pursuing a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Domination](Domination Victory) has an excellent incentive to slot it, especially when playing as a [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilization) or [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) whose [Unique%20unit%20%28Civ6%29](unique unit) is unlocked in the later [Era%20%28Civ6%29](eras) (such as [American%20%28Civ6%29](America), [Australian%20%28Civ6%29](Australia), [German%20%28Civ6%29](Germany), [Gran%20Colombian%20%28Civ6%29](Gran Colombia), [Hungarian%20%28Civ6%29](Hungary), [Russian%20%28Civ6%29](Russia), or [Victoria%20%28Civ6%29](Victoria)).
