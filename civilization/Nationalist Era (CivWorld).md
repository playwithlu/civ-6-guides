# Nationalist Era (CivWorld)

The Nationalist Era is the twelfth [Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Era) in "[CivWorld](CivWorld)". It comes after the [Exploration%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Exploration Era) and before the [Colonial%20Era%20%28CivWorld%29](Colonial Era). During this Era, players receive a 25% bonus to their Production output.
