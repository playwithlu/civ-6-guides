Intro.
 Ramses, pharaoh of the black land and the red, beloved like Horus, your imperious face gazes out across the desert in countless stone images. Others claim to be God’s messenger, but you claim to be the sun incarnate. From temples built from the living rock of Egypt, your command calls your people to you. Rule now, and may none stand against the rising flood.
In-Game.
Ramses II's unique [Agenda%20%28Civ6%29](agenda) is Ma'at. He will clear all features and improve all possible [Tile%20%28Civ6%29](tiles), and likes [Civilizations%20%28Civ6%29](civilizations) who do the same. He dislikes civilizations with few improved tiles or that found [National%20Park%20%28Civ6%29](National Parks).
His [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability) is Abu Simbel. He receives equal to 15% of the cost after completing a building, and 30% after completing a wonder.
