# Research Lab (Civ4)/Final Frontier

 is a building in "[Final%20Frontier](Final Frontier)", a mod in "".
Strategy.
The Research Lab increases output, allowing technologies to be researched faster, although it requires a University already be built on the Planet.
Civilopedia entry.
Freed from the daily rigors of academia, scientists affiliated with Research Labs are at the forefront of modern research and development. Whether funded by private industry or public coffers, Research Labs have been responsible for many of history's most influential discoveries. Any Star System that funds a Research Lab is certain to reap great rewards from the investment.
