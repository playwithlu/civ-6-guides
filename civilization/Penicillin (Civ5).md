# Penicillin (Civ5)

Game Info.
The invention of Penicillin represents the great advancement in the field of medicine in modern times. Healing becomes more and more effective when scientific principles are applied to it, and the invention of this miraculous medicine proves it.
The technology allows building of the [Medical%20Lab%20%28Civ5%29](Medical Lab), the ultimate population growth-enhancing building; it also allows the creation of the [Marine%20%28Civ5%29](Marine) infantry, specialized in sea warfare.
