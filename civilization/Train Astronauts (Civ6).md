# Train Astronauts (Civ6)

Train Astronauts is a special [Project%20%28Civ6%29](project) in "". It becomes available during [International%20Space%20Station%20%28Civ6%29](International Space Station) competitions.
A [City%20%28Civ6%29](city) must have a [Spaceport%20%28Civ6%29](Spaceport) to undertake this project. It increases the player's score for the competition by 30 points each time it is completed.
