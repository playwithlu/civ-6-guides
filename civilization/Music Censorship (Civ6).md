# Music Censorship (Civ6)

Music Censorship is a Diplomatic [Policy%20Cards%20%28Civ6%29](Policy Card) in "", introduced in the [Civilization%20VI%20June%202019%20Update](June 2019 Update).
Strategy.
Music Censorship will prevent other players' [Rock%20Band%20%28Civ6%29](Rock Bands) from entering your territory, making it helpful when one of your opponents is closing in on a [Victory%20%28Civ6%29%23Culture](Culture Victory). However, it will also impose an penalty on larger [City%20%28Civ6%29](cities), stymieing their potential growth and productivity.
