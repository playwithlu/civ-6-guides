# Shale Plant (Civ4)

The Shale Plant is the [Unique%20building%20%28Civ4%29](unique building) of the [Japanese%20%28Civ4%29](Japanese civilization).
Strategy.
The Shale Plant provides the and of the [Coal%20Plant%20%28Civ4%29](Coal Plant) it replaces without requiring [Coal%20%28Civ4%29](Coal) . This makes it good if you can't find Coal, but still have the extra to handle the that comes with Coal Plants (which can be provided late-game by the [Environmentalism%20%28Civ4%29](Environmentalism) [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic)). In addition, it gives +10% in any city that it is built in, which makes it even more helpful for a high city.
