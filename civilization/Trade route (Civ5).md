# Trade route (Civ5)

Vanilla "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)" and "[Gods%20%26amp%3B%20Kings](Gods &amp; Kings)" Trade Routes.
A Trade Route is a link formed between a civilization's [City%20%28Civ5%29](cities) and its , which traders regularly use to move and conduct their trading. This might happen via land, using s, [Railroad%20%28improvement%29%20%28Civ5%29](railroads), or via sea, using s. A Trade Route is open with a city if one of the above-mentioned infrastructures is in place at the beginning of your turn.
