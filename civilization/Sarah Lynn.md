# Sarah Lynn

Sarah Lynn (formerly Sarah Darney) is a video game producer and writer who previously worked for [Firaxis%20Games](Firaxis Games) as a marketing manager. She served as the associate producer for "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)" and was also involved with quality assurance, production, and writing for ', ' and its "" expansion, and "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Starships](Sid Meier's Starships)".
Lynn was the narrator for all "Civilization VI" How To and First Look videos until the [Portugal%20Pack%20%28Civ6%29](Portugal Pack).
