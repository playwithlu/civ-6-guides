# Tank (CivRev)

The Tank is the latest and greatest ballistic unit in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Unit Analysis.
Tanks have an attack of 10 and [Defense%20%28CivRev%29](defense) of 6, making them very versatile. This adds up to an amazing 30 attack and 18 defense for an [Army%20%28CivRev%29](Army) of Tanks.
It is time-consuming and costly but advantageous to build Tanks. They have a cost of 50 resources, which is equal to [Stonehenge%20%28CivRev%29](Stonehenge). Tanks require [Combustion%20%28CivRev%29](Combustion) to be built, so [Oil%20%28CivRev%29](Oil) should be mined for a new production boost.
Unique Replacements.
[American%20%28CivRev%29](American) Tanks are replaced by the Sherman Tank.
[German%20%28CivRev%29](German) Tanks are replaced by the Panzer Tank.
[Russian%20%28CivRev%29](Russian) Tanks are replaced by the T34 Tank.
