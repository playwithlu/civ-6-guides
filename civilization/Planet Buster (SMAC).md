# Planet Buster (SMAC)

 is a unit missile payload type in "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)".
Game info.
The quasi-nuclear Planet Buster is the future's ultimate . Planet Busters destroy everything within a radius equal to their reactor size, often leaving immense craters. Under the U.N. Charter, the use of Planet Busters is punishable by immediate expulsion from the and total military destruction.
