# Catherine (Civ4)

Catherine the Great (2 May 1729 – 17 November 1796) was Empress of Russia (1762 – 1796) after her husband's assassination. She leads the [Russian%20%28Civ4%29](Russians) in "[Civilization%20IV](Civilization IV)".

AI Traits.
Catherine is one of the leaders who will plan wars when pleased. She is also the only leader who can be bribed into declaring war on a leader with whom she is friendly.
