# Tattoo (Civ5)

Tattoo is a special tech from the [Paradise%20Found%20%28Civ5%29](Paradise Found) scenario of "[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)".
Game Info.
Allows you to enhance your military with buildings such as the [Barracks%20%28Civ5%29](Barracks) and [Heroic%20Epic%20%28Civ5%29](Heroic Epic).
