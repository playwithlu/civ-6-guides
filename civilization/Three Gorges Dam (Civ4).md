# Three Gorges Dam (Civ4)

Strategy.
The Three Gorges Dam provides clean [Power%20%28Civ4%29](power) to all [City%20%28Civ4%29](cities) on the same [Continent%20%28Civ4%29](continent), which is needed to increase the production of the [Factory%20%28Civ4%29](Factory). It increases the odds that its city will generate a [Great%20Engineer%20%28Civ4%29](Great Engineer).
This wonder is useful in ensuring a supply of power after the [Environmentalism%20%28Civ4%29](Environmentalism) [List%20of%20civics%20in%20Civ4](civic) is adopted.
