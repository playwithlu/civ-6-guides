# Swarm (CTP1)

The Swarm is a [List%20of%20units%20in%20CTP1](unit) in "".
Summary.
The Swarm offers the distant strike of the [Paratrooper%20%28CTP1%29](Paratrooper) while bypassing air defenses and instead dropping down directly from [Space%20%28CTP1%29](space). Fast and dangerous, the Swarm's only weakness is that its lightweight jetpacks can't launch it back into orbit.
Great Library entry.
Developed first as a weapon to attack from space city to space city, the Swarm soon proved more useful as an invader of distant lands.
