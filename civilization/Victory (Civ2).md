# Victory (Civ2)

Victory in "[Civilization%20II](Civilization II)" can be achieved in three ways.
Space Race.
Build a [Spaceship%20%28Civ2%29](Spaceship) and reach [Alpha%20Centauri%20%28star%20system%29](Alpha Centauri) first.
Conquest.
Defeat all rival [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20Civ2](civilizations) by capturing all their [City%20%28Civ2%29](cities).
Time.
Survive until [2020%20AD](2020 AD).
