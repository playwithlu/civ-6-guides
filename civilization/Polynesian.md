# Polynesian

The Polynesian people represent Polynesia, a [Civilizations](civilization) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games. Their [capital](capital) might be [Haraiki](Haraiki) or [Honolulu](Honolulu), and they can be led by [Kamehameha](Kamehameha) or [Lili%CA%BBuokalani](Liliʻuokalani).
The [Hivan](Hivan), [M%C4%81ori](Māori), [Samoan](Samoan), [Tahitian](Tahitian), and [Tongan](Tongan) people may also be represented by the Polynesians.
