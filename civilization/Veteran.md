# Veteran

Veteran is a status that can be attained by [military%20unit](military unit)s in "[C-evo](C-evo)" and the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)", "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)", and "[Colonization%20%28game%29](Colonization)" games. It is awarded for winning battles (or being trained in a [city](city) with the proper [building](building)s and/or [Great%20Wonder](wonders)) and grants bonuses in [combat](combat).
In some games, Veteran [unit](unit)s can become [Elite](Elite) units by winning additional battles.
