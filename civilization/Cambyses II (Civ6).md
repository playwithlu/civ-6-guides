# Cambyses II (Civ6)

Cambyses II is a non-playable [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". A reused [Gandhi%20%28Civ6%29](Gandhi), he leads the [Persian%20%28Civ6%29](Persians) and has no [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability).
