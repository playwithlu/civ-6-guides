# Swedish (CTP2)

The Swedish people represent a [List%20of%20civilizations%20in%20CTP2](civilization) in "[Call%20to%20Power%20II](Call to Power II)", introduced in [Apolyton%20Edition%20%28CTP2%29](Apolyton Edition). Their default leader names are [Gustavus%20Adolphus](Gustavus Adolphus) for a male and Anna Lindh for a female.
