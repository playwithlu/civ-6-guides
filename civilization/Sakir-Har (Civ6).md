# Sakir-Har (Civ6)

Sakir-Har is a non-playable [Leaders%20%28Civ6%29](leader) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". A reused [Tomyris%20%28Civ6%29](Tomyris), he leads the [Hyksos%20%28Civ6%29](Hyksos) and has no [Unique%20ability%20%28Civ6%29](leader ability).
