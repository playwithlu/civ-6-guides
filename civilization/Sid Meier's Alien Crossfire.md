# Sid Meier's Alien Crossfire

Sid Meier's Alien Crossfire (called SMACX or SMAX for short) is an [expansion%20pack](expansion pack) to "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri](Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri)", which was released by [Firaxis%20Games](Firaxis Games). Both pre-patched games were later bundled together in the "[Sid%20Meier%27s%20Alpha%20Centauri%20Planetary%20Pack](Alpha Centauri Planetary Pack)".
Gameplay changes.
Victory changes.
For diplomatic victory, you must eliminate any alien faction first.
An alien faction can win by completing the [Resonance%20Communicator%20%28SMAC%29](Resonance Communicator), and signalling their people to send a fleet to wipe out everyone else.
New content.
New factions.
There are seven new [Factions%20%28SMAC%29%23Alien%20Crossfire%20factions](factions) added to the game, five of them human and two of them alien. The two alien factions are determined to destroy each other, humans just caught in this "alien crossfire", thus the name of the expansion pack.
