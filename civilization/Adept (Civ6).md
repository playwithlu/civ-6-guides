# Adept (Civ6)

Adept is the Level 1 title for the [Hermetic%20Order%20%28Civ6%29](Hermetic Order), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Medieval%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Medieval Era).
Effects.
Allows you to construct the [Alchemical%20Society%20%28Civ6%29](Alchemical Society), a powerful replacement of the [University%20%28Civ6%29](University).
