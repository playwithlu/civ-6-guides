# Temple to Amun (Civ6)

The Temple to Amun is an advanced [Religion%20%28Civ6%29](religious) [Building%20%28Civ6%29](building) from the [Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile%20%28Civ6%29](Gifts of the Nile) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is purchased in the [Holy%20Site%20%28Civ6%29](Holy Site) [District%20%28Civ6%29](district) and requires a [Shrine%20%28Civ6%29/Gifts%20of%20the%20Nile](Shrine).
Strategy.
In Gifts of the Nile, [Egyptian%20%28Civ6%29](Egypt) and [Nubian%20%28Civ6%29](Nubia) compete to build Temples to Amun. The first civilization to build 7 of them achieves victory in the scenario.
