# Mohawk Warrior (Civ5)

Game Info.
[Unique%20unit%20%28Civ5%29](Unique) frontline unit of the [Iroquois%20%28Civ5%29](Iroquois) [Civilizations%20%28Civ5%29](civilization). Replaces the [Swordsman%20%28Civ5%29](Swordsman).
Strategy.
The Mohawk Warrior has a nice combat bonus in [Jungle%20%28Civ5%29](jungles) and [Forest%20%28Civ5%29](forests), as well as another interesting advantage: it doesn't require ! The Iroquois are the only civilization whose [Classical%20Era%20%28Civ5%29](Classical Era) frontline unit doesn't require a [Resources%20%28Civ5%29](resource), making it possible for them to immediately upgrade their [Warrior%20%28Civ5%29](Warriors) to Mohawk Warriors without bothering to discover and secure deposits. They can then be used to attack enemies which are settled in forests or jungles, and defeat them easily thanks to the special combat bonus.
