# Patala (Civ6)

Patala is a militaristic [City-state%20%28Civ6%29](city-state) from the [Conquests%20of%20Alexander%20%28Civ6%29](Conquests of Alexander) [List%20of%20scenarios%20in%20Civ6](scenario) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is able to produce the [Varu%20%28Civ6%29](Varu).
Strategy.
Because the player cannot obtain s in Conquests of Alexander, it is not possible to interact with Patala diplomatically. As such, the city-state does not provide any or [Suzerain%20%28Civ6%29](Suzerain) bonuses.
