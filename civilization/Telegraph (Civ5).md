# Telegraph (Civ5)

Game Info.
The invention of the Telegraph greatly expedites long-distance communication and allows for advancements in military and naval technology. It also enables the construction of the [Cristo%20Redentor%20%28Civ5%29](Cristo Redentor) wonder.
In the expansion sets, Telegraph is removed from the tech tree and replaced by [Telecommunications%20%28Civ5%29](Telecommunications), a technology of the [Information%20era%20%28Civ5%29](Information Era).
