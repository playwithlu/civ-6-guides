# Steam

Steam is a digital content distribution platform and gaming community developed and run by Valve Corporation.
"[Civilization%20V](Civilization V)", "[Beyond%20Earth](Beyond Earth)" and "[Starships](Starships)" require the player to register to Steam. Steam keeps the games updated and offers numerous [achievements](achievements) for "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" players to boast with. Steam also allows companies to sell [DLC](extra content) for their games. There is also a virtual [Steam%20trading%20cards](trading cards) system in place for collector-minded users.
