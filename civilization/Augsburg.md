# Augsburg

Augsburg, also known by its historical names of Augusta Vindelicorum or Augusta Vindelicum, is a [German](German) and [Holy%20Roman](Holy Roman) [city](city) in the "[Civilization%20%28series%29](Civilization)" and "[Call%20to%20Power%20games](Call to Power)" games, and also appears as a [city-state](city-state). In real life, it is a city in the state of Bavaria in present-day Germany.
In [scenario](scenario)s, it may appear as a [Western%20Roman](Western Roman) city in [Fall%20of%20Rome](Fall of Rome).
