# Canticle (Civ6)

Canticle is the Level 2 title for the [Voidsingers%20%28Civ6%29](Voidsingers), a Secret Society exclusive to the [Secret%20Societies%20%28Civ6%29](Secret Societies) [Game%20mode%20%28Civ6%29](game mode) in "[Civilization%20VI](Civilization VI)". It is unlocked in the [Industrial%20Era%20%28Civ6%29](Industrial Era).
Effects.
Unlocks the [Cultist%20%28Civ6%29](Cultist) unit. This unit is purchased with , and uses charges to reduce [Loyalty%20%28Civ6%29](Loyalty) in foreign cities and generate Relics of the Void.
