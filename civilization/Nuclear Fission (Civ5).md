# Nuclear Fission (Civ5)

Game Info.
Nuclear Fission, the ability to split the atom, provides your civilization with the concrete technology to build WMDs - Weapons of Mass Destruction, in the form of the [Atomic%20Bomb%20%28Civ5%29](Atomic Bomb). The era of total war is opened...
The tech can also be used for peaceful purposes, with the [Nuclear%20Plant%20%28Civ5%29](Nuclear Plant) - a powerful late-game -boosting building.
