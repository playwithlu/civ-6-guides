# Steel (CivRev)

Steel is a [List%20of%20technologies%20in%20CivRev](technology) in "[Civilization%20Revolution](Civilization Revolution)".
Strategy.
Steel allows construction of the most powerful ship in the sea: the [Battleship%20%28CivRev%29](Battleship). If you are the first to research Steel, you will receive a free Battleship unit!
